Middle Primitive Elvish Phrases

    • e mati-te “he eats him(self)”
    • e mati-the “he eats him (another man)”
    • epe ndere “after the man”
    • gondōm dantār “(the) rocks fall”
    • kabinjē antāsa “I can give it”
    • ke mati-te “thou eatest it”
    • kirya kyulma “a ship’s mast”
    • ni mati-te “I eat it (him/her)”
    • ni mati-te mati-the “I eat it and that”
    • ni matite yu the “I eat it and that”
    • ni-ndakubākyē, uklaina ndere “I will slay thee, wicked man”
    • the “him (another man)”
