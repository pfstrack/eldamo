Lalaith’s Guide to Adûnaic Grammar (LGtAG)

  • Andreas Moehn; 28 June 2006; http://lalaith.vpsurf.de/Tolkien/Grammar.html

This article discusses the various iterations of Adûnaic grammar and analyses the different versions of the Lament of Akallabêth, which is the Adûnaic version of Q. Lament of Atalante. It is one of the best sources of information for this language available on the web.
