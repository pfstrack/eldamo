Parma Eldalamberon #14 (PE14)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editors) Patrick Wynne, Christopher Gilson, Carl Hostetter, Bill Welden, Arden Smith; 2003

The fourteenth issue of Parma Eldalamberon. This issue includes a number of early name and word lists (p. 5-24) and a discussion of (Early) Qenya Verb Forms (p. 25-34), but its most notable document is an Early Qenya Grammar in manuscript and typescript form (p. 35-86). The remainder of the issue discusses the Valmaric Script (p. 89-134).
