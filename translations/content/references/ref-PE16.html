Parma Eldalamberon #16 (PE16)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editors) Christopher Gilson, Arden Smith, Patrick Wynne, Carl Hostetter, Bill Welden; 2006

The sixteenth issue of Parma Eldalamberon. The seventeenth issue of Parma Eldalamberon. This issue includes a discussion of Pre-Fëanorian alphabets (p. 5-51), early Qenya poetry (p. 53-104) and some early Qenya grammar and vocabulary from the 1920s (p. 105-148). The Qenya poetry consists of drafts and alternate versions of the poems presented in the “Secret Vice” essay from The Monsters and the Critics (MC).
