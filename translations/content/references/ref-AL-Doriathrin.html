Ardalambion — Doriathrin (AL-Doriathrin)

  • Helge Fauskanger; 1997; http://folk.uib.no/hnohf/doriath.htm

Helge Fauskanger’s article on Doriathrin, with a word-list including etymological notes. I found this article particularly useful for my own analysis of the language. Mr. Fauskanger analyzed the Doriathrin dialect separately from the main Ilkorin language, whereas this lexicon groups the two dialects together. References are by word name.
