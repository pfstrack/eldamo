Lord of the Rings, 50th Anniversary One-Volume Edition (LotR)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien; 2004, original publication 1954-55

This is the canonical source for Tolkien’s languages, the only work “authorized” by Tolkien while he was still alive. Even The Silmarillion was still a work in progress.

Individual words that appear repeatedly (such as lembas or palantír) are generally cited only once in this lexicon, either for their first appearance or the one that is more linguistically relevant. Words appearing in phrases, however are referenced for every appearance, except where words are repeated within the same phrase. Proper names in the main text are only cited if they include linguistically relevant information; other names are cited from the Lord of the Ring Index.
