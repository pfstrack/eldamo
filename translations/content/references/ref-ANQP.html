Atanquesta: a NeoQuenya Primer (ANQP)

  • Tamas Ferencz; 2018; https://middangeard.org.uk/atanquesta/

A recent Neo-Quenya course, the one most current in terms of its use of published linguistic material (up-to-date as of VT50 and PE22). Though I have quibbles with some of Mr. Ferencz’s vocabulary choices, it is the best introductory Neo-Quenya course available as of its publication (2018).
