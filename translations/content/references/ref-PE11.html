Parma Eldalamberon #11 (PE11)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editors) Christopher Gilson, Patrick Wynne, Arden Smith, Carl Hostetter; 1995

The eleventh issue of Parma Eldalamberon. This issue publishes two documents written in the 1910s: the Gnomish Grammar (GG, PE11/7-16) and the Gnomish Lexicon (GL, PE11/17-75). These two documents are referenced separately under their entry in this lexicon, leaving no direct references to be PE11 itself. See the references to GG and GL for further discussion.
