ᴱQ. poimine n. “lather, foam; mermaid” (Category: Soap)

⚠️ᴱQ. poiminë, n. “lather, foam; mermaid” (Category: Soap)
ᴺQ. !lipsanë “lather”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “lather”, an elaboration of ᴱQ. poime “soap” (QL/75). In the Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa it was glossed “lather, foam; a mermaid” (PME/75).
