ᴱQ. vaite adj. “skied” (Category: Sky, Heavens)

⚠️ᴱQ. vaitë, adj. “skied” (Category: Sky, Heavens)
Q. vilya “air, sky”

An adjective appearing as part of karnevaite “red-skied” in the Oilima Markirya poem and its drafts from around 1930 (MC/221) related to ᴱQ. vaiya “sky” (PE16/75).

References ✧ MC/221; PE16/62, 72, 74-75, 77

Glosses

  • “skied” ✧ PE16/75
  • “skies” ✧ MC/221
  • “sky” ✧ PE16/62

Elements

vaiya¹ “sky, skies”

Element In

  • ᴱQ. karnevaite úri kilde hísen níe nienaite “amid the red skies the Sun with wet eyes dropped tears of mist” ✧ MC/221; PE16/62; PE16/72; PE16/74
  • ᴱQ. karnevaite úri kilivande hísen nie nie nienaite “*red-skied the sun will gaze through a haze of tears” ✧ PE16/77
