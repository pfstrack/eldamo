ᴱQ. Ponórir loc. “the Northlands, Scandanavia”

⚠️ᴱQ. Ponórir, loc. “the Northlands, Scandanavia”

Qenya name for Scandanavia in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/74), a combination of root ᴱ√PO having to do with the north, and the plural of nóre “land”.
