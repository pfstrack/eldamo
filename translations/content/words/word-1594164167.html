S. tauron n. “forester” (Category: Woods, Forest)

S. tauron, n. “forester” (Category: Woods, Forest)

A word for “forester”, based on the name Tauron of the same meaning (PM/358). It is simply taur “forest” with the agental suffix S. -on¹.
