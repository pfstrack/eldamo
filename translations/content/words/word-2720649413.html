ᴱQ. palaswin (palaswing-) n. “wave-crest” (Category: Wave, Surge)

⚠️ᴱQ. palaswin (palaswing-), n. “wave-crest” (Category: Wave, Surge)
Q. falma “(crested or foaming) wave, breaker; ⚠️[ᴱQ.] foam”

A word for “wave-crest” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s, derived from ᴱ√Palas (QL/72). It has a stem form palaswing-, so was perhaps ᴱ√Palas + ᴱQ. winge “foam”.
