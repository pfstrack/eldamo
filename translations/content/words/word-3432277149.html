ᴹQ. Eldamir m. “Elfstone”

ᴹQ. Eldamir, m. “Elfstone”

A precursor to Q. Elessar, a compound of Elda “Elf” and míre “jewel” (LR/276), with an early variant Qendemir using Qende “Elf” instead (LR/276).

See Q. Elessar for further discussion.
