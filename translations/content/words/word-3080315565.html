ᴱQ. Silmo m. “the Moon”

⚠️ᴱQ. Silmo, m. “the Moon”

Guardian of Silpion, one of the Two Trees, in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/73). In the Qenya Lexicon his name was glossed “the Moon” and given as a derivative of the root ᴱ√SILI (QL/83).
