S. Drúwaith loc. “the wilderness of the Drû-folk”

S. Drúwaith, loc. “the wilderness of the Drû-folk”

A Sindarin term for the wilderness inhabited by the Woses (UT/385), a compound of Drû “Wose” and the lenited form of gwaith “region”.
