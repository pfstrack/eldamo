N. Ar Uiar pn. “Day of Ulmo, second day of the Valian week”

N. Ar Uiar, pn. “Day of Ulmo, second day of the Valian week”

The Noldorin name for the 2nd day of the Valian 5-day week, a combination of the prefix ar-² “day” and Uiar, the Noldorin name of Ulmo (Ety/LEP).
