      Phonetic Development

      ᴱQ. [ai] became [e] before unstressed [a], [o] or [u], but [aj] before stressed [a], [o] or [u] eu < aiu ✧ PE12/12 (áı̯u > eu (aiyu))
      ᴱQ. [ei] became [e] before [a], [o] or [u], but [i] before [e] eu < eiu ✧ PE12/12 (éı̯u > eu (iu))
      ᴱQ. [eu], [iu] became [euw], [iuw] before unstressed vowel, [ew], [iw] before stressed vowel euwV < euV ✧ PE12/12 (éu̯- + vowel > euv-)
