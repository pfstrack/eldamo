✶Ad. [j] ‹y›
The primitive semi-vowel “y” (IPA [j]) sometimes changed to or merged with [i] by the time of Classical Adûnaic (SD/419). It is the only sound of the c-series that survived into Classical Adûnaic (SD/418).
