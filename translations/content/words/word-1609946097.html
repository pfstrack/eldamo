ᴱ√QOTO root.

⚠️ᴱ√QOTO root.
√NOT “count, reckon”

An unglossed root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s with derivatives ᴱQ. qoto- “count up, reckon” and ᴱQ. qotta “sum, number” (QL/78). It was probably displaced by √NOT “count, reckon”.
