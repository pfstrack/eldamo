ᴱQ. Ruamóre loc. “*Still Night”

⚠️ᴱQ. Ruamórë, loc. “*Still Night”

Home of Ungweliante (Ungoliant) in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/80; PME/81), a combination of rua “still” with móre “night”.
