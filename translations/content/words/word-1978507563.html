N. lhant n. “clearing in the forest” (Category: Woods, Forest)

⚠️N. lhant, n. “clearing in the forest” (Category: Woods, Forest)
ᴺS. ^lant “clearing in the forest”

A word given as N. lhant “clearing in the forest” in The Etymologies of the 1930s as a derivative of the root ᴹ√LAT “lie open” (Ety/LAT).

Neo-Sindarin: If adapted to Neo-Sindarin, this word would be ᴺS. lant as suggested in Hiswelókë’s Sindarin Dictionary (HSD).
