G. tinwithli n. “group of stars, star-cluster, constellation” (Category: Star)

⚠️G. tinwithli, n. “group of stars, star-cluster, constellation” (Category: Star)
ᴺS. !tiniath “group of stars, star-cluster, constellation”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “group of stars, star-cluster, constellation”, an elaboration of G. tinwin “small star” (GL/71).
