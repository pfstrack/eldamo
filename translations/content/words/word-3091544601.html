G. ô n. “the sea” (Category: Sea)

⚠️G. ô, n. “the sea” (Category: Sea)
S. gaear “sea”

A word for “the sea” in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s, derived from primitive ᴱ✶ou̯a and probably based on the early root ᴱ√’O’O (GL/61; QL/70).
