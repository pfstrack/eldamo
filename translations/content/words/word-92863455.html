ᴱN. urnor adj. “landless” (Category: Earth, Land)

⚠️ᴱN. urnor, adj. “landless” (Category: Earth, Land)

An example negative adjective in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s that was a combination of the privative prefix ᴱN. or- and ᴱN. dôr “land” (PE13/156); it replaced a deleted form ᴱN. unnor “without a land” that was based on the rejected privative prefix ᴱN. um- (PE13/155).
