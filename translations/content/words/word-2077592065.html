ᴱQ. Teamalle loc. “High St.”

⚠️ᴱQ. Tëamallë, loc. “High St.”

A place name mentioned only in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/90), a combination of tea¹ “straight” and malle “street”.
