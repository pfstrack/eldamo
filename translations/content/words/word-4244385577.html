S. Emyn Uial loc. “Hills of Evendim”

S. Emyn Uial, loc. “Hills of Evendim”

Hills north of the Shire translated “Hills of Evendim” on the map of The Lord of the Rings (LotR/1082), a combination of the plural of amon “hill” and uial “(evening) twilight”.
