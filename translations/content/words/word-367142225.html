Q. Ardamírë m. “Jewel of the World”

Q. Ardamírë, m. “Jewel of the World”
The mother-name of Eärendil, it is a compound of Arda “World” and mírë “jewel” (PM/348). It also appeared in the shorter form Ardamir (PM/346, WJ/235).
