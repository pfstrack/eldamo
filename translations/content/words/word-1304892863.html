ᴱN. dalhung adj. “not heavy” (Category: Light, Nimble)

⚠️ᴱN. dalhung, adj. “not heavy” (Category: Light, Nimble)

An adjective for “not heavy” in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s, a combination of ᴱN. lhung “heavy” with the negative prefix for adjectives ᴱN. dan- (PE13/141).
