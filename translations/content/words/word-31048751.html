ᴹAQ. initial [d] became [l]; [d-] > [l-]

Reference ✧ PE19/32

Order (02900)

After 01200 initial [d] assimilated to following nasal
After 02700 initial [dr-], [dl-] became [r-], [l-]
Before 03000 voiced stops became spirants except after nasals and liquids
