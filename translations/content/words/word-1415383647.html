N. gwastar n. “hummock” (Category: Mound, Pile)

N. gwastar, n. “hummock” (Category: Mound, Pile)

A noun in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “hummock”, a combination of the prefix N. gwa- “together” and the root ᴹ√STAR “stiff” (Ety/STAR; WŌ).
