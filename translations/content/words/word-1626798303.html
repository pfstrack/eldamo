ᴱQ. kate (kati-) n. “gleam, ray” (Category: Light)

⚠️ᴱQ. catë (cati-), n. “gleam, ray” (Category: Light)
ᴹQ. alca “ray of light, ⚠️[ᴱQ.] light of day; shining”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “a gleam, ray” derived from the early root ᴱ√KATYA (QL/45).
