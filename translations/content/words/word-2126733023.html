ᴺS. !sarneg n. “pebble” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺS. !sarneg, n. “pebble” (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism for “pebble” coined by Paul Strack in 2021 specifically for Eldamo, a diminutive form of S. sarn “stone”.

Elements

S. sarn “(small) stone, pebble; stony (place)”
