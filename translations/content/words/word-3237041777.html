Q. Nur-menel loc. “*Under Heaven”

Q. Nur-menel, loc. “*Under Heaven”

A term for the “lesser firmament” created by Varda in imitation of the true heavens Tarmenel (MR/388). It is probably a compound of some form of nu “under” and menel “the heavens”.
