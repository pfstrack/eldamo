S. Mornedhel pn. “Dark-elf”

S. Mornedhel, pn. “Dark-elf”

A Sindarin term for the Avari, a combination of morn “dark” and Edhel “Elf” (WJ/377), also appearing as Moredhel (PE17/139, 141).
