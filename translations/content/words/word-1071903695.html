S. Barad Eithel loc. “Tower of the Well”

S. Barad Eithel, loc. “Tower of the Well”

A fortress on the river Sirion, translated “Tower of the Well” (S/191). It is a combination of barad “tower” and eithel “spring, (water) source” (SA/barad, eithel).
