ᴱQ. Tamar m.

⚠️ᴱQ. Tamar, m.

Another name for Aule, along side variant Tamildo, appearing in the Qenya Lexicon and the Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/88; PME/88), derived from the root ᴱ√TAMA “smelt, forge”.
