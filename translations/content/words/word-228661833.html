N. Taur-nan-Erig loc. “Forest of Region”

N. Taur-nan-Erig, loc. “Forest of Region”

A name appearing only in The Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/ERÉK), a combination of taur¹ “forest”, nan “of” and ereg¹ “holly”.
