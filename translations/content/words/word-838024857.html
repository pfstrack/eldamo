Ilk. taig adj. “steep, tall, deep” (Category: Tall)

An adjective meaning “steep, tall, deep” derived from primitive ᴹ✶taikā (Ety/AYAK, EtyAC/TĀ). The [k] became [g] after a vowel as usual.
