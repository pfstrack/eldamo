ᴹQ. Sindo [þ] m. “The Grey”

ᴹQ. Sindo [þ], m. “The Grey”

An earlier variant of Q. Singollo glossed “The Grey” (LR/215, MR/088), apparently a masculinized form of the adjective sinde “grey” (Ety/THIN).

See Q. Singollo for further discussion.
