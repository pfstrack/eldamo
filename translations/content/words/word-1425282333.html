Q. Aratar coll. “High Ones, The Exalted, The Supreme”

Q. Aratar, coll. “High Ones, The Exalted, The Supreme”
A term used for the greatest of the Valar (S/29), it is the adjective arata “high, exalted” used as a plural name.
