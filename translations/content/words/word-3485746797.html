S. Thinrod m. “noble member of the Thindrim”

S. Thinrod, m. “noble member of the Thindrim”

A Sindarin name glossed “noble member of the Thindrim (Sindar)”, a combination of thind “grey” and the suffixal form of arod “noble” (VT41/9).
