ᴱN. Borosaith m. “Everhungry”

⚠️ᴱN. Borosaith, m. “Everhungry”

A sobriquet of Carcaras [Carcharoth] glossed “Everhungry” (SM/112, 115 note #9), apparently a compound of a variant form of bóra “ever” and saith “hunger”.
