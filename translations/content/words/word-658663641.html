G. gobli n. “dell” (Category: Dale, Valley)

⚠️G. gobli, n. “dell” (Category: Dale, Valley)
S. imlad “deep valley, narrow valley with steep sides, gap, gully, [N.] dell, glen”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “dell” (GL/40), an elaboration on G. gob “hollow of hand” so perhaps originally meaning “*hollowness”.
