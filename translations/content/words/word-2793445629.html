N. Haramon loc.

N. Haramon, loc.

An earlier name for Emyn Arnen (WR/363), apparently a combination of the prefixal form har- of harn¹ “south” and amon “hill”, as suggested by Roman Rausch (EE/3.38).

See S. Emyn Arnen for further discussion.
