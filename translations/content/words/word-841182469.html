Q. arcalima ar eleni “A is brightest of all”

Q. arcalima ar eleni, “A is brightest of all”

Reference ✧ PE17/56 ✧ [arcalima] ar [eleni] “far and away brighter than”

Related

  • arcalima ar eleni “A is brightest of all” for its use of ar compare it to A anamelda na ar ilyan “A is dearest of all”
  • [arcalima] ar [eleni] “far and away brighter than” with ar written above and possibly replacing the ep’ in arcalima ep’ eleni “far and away brighter than stars” ✧ PE17/56

Elements

calima “bright, luminous” superlative ✧ PE17/56 (arcalima)
ar(a) “beside, next [to]; out” ✧ PE17/56 (ar)
elen “star” plural ✧ PE17/56 (eleni)
