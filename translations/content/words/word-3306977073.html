ᴺQ. !nartanwë n. “firework” (Category: Match, Fire Stick)

ᴺQ. !nartanwë, n. “firework” [created by NQ-Wiki] (Category: Match, Fire Stick)

A neologism for “firework” appearing in the Neo-Quenya Wiki, a combination of Q. nár “fire” and [ᴹQ.] tanwe “device”.

Elements

Q. nár “fire (as an element)”
ᴹQ. tanwe “craft, thing made, device, construction”
