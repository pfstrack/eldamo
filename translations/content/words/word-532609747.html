ᴱQ. Vorotemnar pn.

⚠️ᴱQ. Vorotemnar, pn.

Manacles binding Melko in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/101), a combination vor(o) “ever” and some form of the root ᴱ√TEME “tie”, as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Vorotemnar).
