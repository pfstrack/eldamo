S. Arroch m. “*Noble-horse”

S. Arroch, m. “*Noble-horse”

The name of Húrin’s horse (UT/70). The name is probably a combination of ar(a)- “noble” and roch “horse”.

Reference ✧ UTI/Arroch

Elements

ar(a)- “noble, royal, high”
roch “horse”
