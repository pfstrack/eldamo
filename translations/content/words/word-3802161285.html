ᴱQ. Vanasan pn. “Wednesday”

⚠️ᴱQ. Vanasan, pn. “Wednesday”
Q. Menelya “*Wednesday, Heavens-day”

Name of the second Wednesday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the goddess Vána and sana “day”.
