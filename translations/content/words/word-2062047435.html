ᴱQ. kapalinda n. “spring of water” (Category: Spring, Well)

⚠️ᴱQ. capalinda, n. “spring of water” (Category: Spring, Well)
Q. ehtelë “spring, issue of water”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s for a “spring of water”, derived from the root ᴱ√KAPA “leap, spring” (QL/45).
