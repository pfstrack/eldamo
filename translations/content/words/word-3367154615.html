ᴱQ. Ui f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Ui, f.

Another name for Ónen (Uinen) in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/97; PME/70, 97), derived from the root ᴱ√IWI having to do with fish.
