G. ôr n. “dry sand” (Category: Sand, Grit)

⚠️G. ôr, n. “dry sand” (Category: Sand, Grit)
S. lith “ash; [N.] sand”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “dry sand”, a cognate of ᴱQ. āra of similar meaning (GL/62; QL/32). The Gnomish form is due to the sound change whereby [ā] became [ō].
