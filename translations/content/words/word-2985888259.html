Ilk. [r̥] ‹rh›

A voiceless liquid [r̥] developed in Ilkorin from the initial clusters [kʰr] and [sr] (PE19/21). It seems likely the change would have occurred medially as well for the cluster [kʰr], though there it may have become a long voiceless [r̥r̥].
