ᴱQ. nalda adj. “lowly, valley (aj.), dale (aj.)” (Category: Dale, Valley)

⚠️ᴱQ. nalda, adj. “lowly, valley (aj.), dale (aj.)” (Category: Dale, Valley)
Q. tumba “[ᴹQ.] deep, lowlying; ⚠️[Q.] deep valley”

An adjective in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s with the gloss “lowly, valley (aj.), dale (aj.)” and variants nalda and nalba, both derived from the root ᴱ√NḶĐḶ (QL/66).
