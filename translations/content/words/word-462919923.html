ᴱQ. sinqele n. “mine” (Category: Cave)

⚠️ᴱQ. sinquelë, n. “mine” (Category: Cave)
Q. felya “mine, boring, tunnel, ⚠️underground dwelling; [ᴹQ.] cave”

A noun for “mine” in the Qenya Lexicon, an elaboration of ᴱQ. sink (sinq-) “mineral, gem, metal” (QL/83). This word was also mentioned in the Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa (PME/83).
