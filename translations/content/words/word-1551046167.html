⚠️ᴱQ. ran (ram-), n. “noise” (Category: Noise, Thunder)
Q. hlón(a) “noise, sound”

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s given as ran (ram-) “noise”, from the early root ᴱ√RAMA (QL/78-79).
