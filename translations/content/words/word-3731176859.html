    Phonetic Development

    ᴱQ. [au] became [o] before unstressed vowel, but [aw] before stressed vowel oi < aui ✧ PE12/12 (áu̯i > oi (ávi))
    ᴱQ. [ou] became [u] before [e] and [o], but [o] before [i] and [a] oi < oui ✧ PE12/12 (óu̯i > oi (ui)); PE12/13 (ou̯í > oi (ui))
    ᴱQ. [oi], [ui] became [oij], [uij] before unstressed vowel, [oj], [uj] before stressed vowel oijV < oiV ✧ PE12/12 (óı̯- + vowel > oiy-)
