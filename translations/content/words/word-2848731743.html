ᴱQ. Aiwala m.

⚠️ᴱQ. Aiwala, m.

An (Early) Qenya equivalent to Eöl in an early name list along with a number of variants (PE13/104). In some other early notes, it appeared as Arval (LT2I/Arval). Its etymology is unclear.
