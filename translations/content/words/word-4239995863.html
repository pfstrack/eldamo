N. Nauglavir pn.

N. Nauglavir, pn.

An adaptation of Ilk. Nauglamír (Ety/NAUK), with the final element mîr “jewel” lenited as it would be in Noldorin.

See S. Nauglamír for further discussion.

      N. mîr na Nauglin pn.

      The proper Noldorin name for the Ilk. Nauglamír (Ety/NAUK) along with variant mîr i-nuig (EtyAC/NAUK), a combination of mîr “jewel”, na “of” and (?archaic plural) Nauglin “Dwarves”.
