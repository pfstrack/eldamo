ᴱQ. Ilterendi pn.

⚠️ᴱQ. Ilterendi, pn.

Name of the chain of Angaino in the earliest Lost Tales, described as “fetters that might not be filed or cleft” (LT1/101). It might be derived from the root YḶTḶ and related to G. ilt- “to yoke, join”.
