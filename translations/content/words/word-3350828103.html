ᴹQ. Vinya loc. “Young, New Land”

ᴹQ. Vinya, loc. “Young, New Land”

A name for Númenor in the initial versions of the tales of its fall (LR/19, 64; SD/332). It is simply vinya¹ “young, new” used as a name.
