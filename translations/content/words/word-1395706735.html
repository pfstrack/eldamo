ᴹQ. hyóla n. “trump” (Category: Horn, Trumpet)

ᴹQ. hyóla, n. “trump, *trumpet” (Category: Horn, Trumpet)
Glossed “trump”, perhaps in the older English sense of the word meaning “trumpet”. It seems unlikely it would refer to a suit of cards.
