S. Arothir m. “Noble Lord”

S. Arothir, m. “Noble Lord”

A name Tolkien considered as a replacement for Orodreth in some of his late writings from 1965 (PM/350). The name is a combination of arod “noble” and hîr “lord”. @@@ Etymology
