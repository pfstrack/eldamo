S. Arossiach loc. “Ford(s) of Aros”

S. Arossiach, loc. “Ford(s) of Aros”
A ford across the river Aros, translated “Ford(s) of Aros” (S/121, WJ/338). It is a compound of the river name Aros and the (singular) noun iach “ford”.
