ᴱN. gwaithlos n. “*sea-shore” (Category: Shore, Beach)

⚠️ᴱN. gwaithlos, n. “*sea-shore” (Category: Shore, Beach)
S. falas “beach, shore, strand, surf(line)”

A noun Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s, a combination of ᴱN. gwai “sea” and ᴱN. thlos “shore” and thus = “*sea-shore” (PE13/146).
