⚠️ᴱQ. niquista-, v. “to snow” (Category: Snow)
ᴺQ. ^hris- “to snow”

A verb for “to snow” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s with variants niqista- and niqisya-, both elaborations of ᴱQ. niqis “snow” (QL/66).
