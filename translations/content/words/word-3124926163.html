Dan. aspirates became voiceless spirants; [{ptk}ʰ] > [{fθh}]
Primitive aspirates became voiceless spirants in Danian as they did in all the Eldarin languages (PE19/27), a change that is well documented in the Comparative Tables but supported only by Dan. hrassa < ᴹ✶khrassē in The Etymologies (Ety/KHARÁS).
