⚠️ᴱN. bala, n. “round hump, hump, hillock” (Category: Mound, Pile)

A noun in Early Noldorin Word-lists from the 1920s glossed “round hump, hump, hillock”, derived from primitive ᴱ✶balga (PE13/138).
