Q. Eärwen f. “*Sea-maiden”

Q. Eärwen, f. “*Sea-maiden”

The daughter of Olwë and wife of Finarfin (S/60). Her name is a probably compound of ëar “sea” and the suffixal form -wen of wendë “maiden”.
