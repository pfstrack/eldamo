Dan. [g]

The primitive voiced velar stop was preserved initially and medially in both Danian dialects, as well as developing from initial nasalized [ŋg] (PE19/18, 20). In addition, primitive [ŋ], [ɣ] became [g], both medially and initially (PE19/19, 23).
