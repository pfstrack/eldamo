ᴺS. !cethaeg n. “horizon” (Category: Sky, Heavens)

ᴺS. !cethaeg, n. “horizon” [created by Elaran] (Category: Sky, Heavens)

A neologism for “horizon” coined by Elaran from the VinQuettaParma Wiki (VQP), a combination of S. cen- “see/sight” and S. taeg “boundary”.

Elements

S. #cen- “to see, perceive, look”
S. #taeg “boundary, limit, boundary line”
