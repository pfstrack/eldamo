Ilk. Thorntor m. “King of Eagles”

Ilk. Thorntor m. “King of Eagles”

Ilkorin name for N. Thorondor appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s, translated “King of Eagles” (Ety/THOR). It is a combination of thorn “eagle” and tôr “king”.
