S. Turcomund m. “Chief of Bulls”

S. Turcomund, m. “Chief of Bulls”

A name coined by Tolkien as a possible Elvish name for a bull (Let/423). The initial element of the name is (Quenya?) turco¹ “chief” and its final element is mund “bull”.
