ᴱQ. lúkima adj. “slimy” (Category: Mud, Mire)

⚠️ᴱQ. lúcima, adj. “slimy” (Category: Mud, Mire)

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “slimy”, an adjectival form of ᴱQ. lúke “slime” (QL/56).
