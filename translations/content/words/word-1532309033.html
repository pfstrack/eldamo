Q. Aiwendil m. “Lover of Birds”

Q. Aiwendil, m. “Lover of Birds”
The Quenya name of Radagast (UT/393). Christopher Tolkien translated the name as “Lover of Birds” (UT/401), a compound of aiwë “bird” and -(n)dil “-lover”.
