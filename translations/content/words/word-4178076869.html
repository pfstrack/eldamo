S. Arvegil m.

S. Arvegil, m.

The 11th king of Arthedain (LotR/1038). The initial element of his name is the prefix ar(a)- “noble” and its final element is probably the lenited form vegil of megil “sword”.

References ✧ LotRI; PMI

Elements

ar(a)- “noble, royal, high”
megil “sword, longsword”
