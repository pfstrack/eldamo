G. cectha- v. “to spout out” (Category: Spring, Well)

⚠️G. cectha-, v. “to spout out” (Category: Spring, Well)

A verb in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “to spout out” (GL/25), probably a verb formation from the root ᴱ√KEKE¹ “to bubble up” (QL/46). It had an archaic variant †ectha- “to spout, jet” (GL/31).
