ᴱQ. Ninwi pn. “Nineveh”

⚠️ᴱQ. Ninwi, pn. “Nineveh”

Qenya name for the city Nineveh in the earliest Lost Tales, first given as Ninwë (LT2/203). It is likely a phonetic adaption of the original name.
