ᴱN. darhod adj. “not free” (Category: Free)

⚠️ᴱN. darhod, adj. “not free” (Category: Free)

An adjective for “not free” in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s, a combination of ᴱN. rhód “bright” with the negative prefix for adjectives ᴱN. dan- (PE13/141).

Reference ✧ PE13/141 ✧ “not free”

Elements
