ᴱQ. Maiwendi pn. “*Greatest Maiden”

⚠️ᴱQ. Maiwendi, pn. “*Greatest Maiden”

A variant of the name Maiwe (Vána) in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/60), apparently a combination of the root ᴱ√MAẎA with ᴱQ. wen(di) “maiden”, so perhaps “*Greatest Maiden”. It also appeared as Maiwen in the Gnomish Lexicon (GL/18).
