    Phonetic Development

    ᴱQ. voiceless spirant combinations assimilated except to [θ] xɸ > ɸɸ ✧ PE12/21 (x + ꝑ > ꝑꝑ)
    ᴱQ. initial [x-] became [h-] x- > h- ✧ PE12/16 (x > h); PE12/18 (χ/h > h)
    ᴱQ. geminate voiceless spirants reduced xx > h ✧ PE12/18 (χχ > h)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids rx > rɣ ✧ PE12/18 (liquids + χ > rg)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids lx > lɣ ✧ PE12/18 (liquids + χ > lg)
    ᴱQ. final voiceless spirants were weakened and voiced -x > -ɣ ✧ PE12/20 (h > ʒ)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants became stops before stops, [s] or [z] xs > ks ✧ PE12/18 (χs > ks)
    ᴱQ. [x] assimilated to preceding stop kʷx > kk ✧ PE12/18 (qχ > cc)
    ᴱQ. [x] assimilated to preceding stop kx > kk ✧ PE12/18 (kχ > kk)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants became stops after nasals ŋx > ŋk ✧ PE12/18 (nasal + χ > nc)
    ᴱQ. [x] assimilated to preceding stop px > pp ✧ PE12/18 (pχ > pp)
    ᴱQ. [x] assimilated to preceding stop tʲx > tt ✧ PE12/18 (k͡χ > tt)
    ᴱQ. [x] assimilated to preceding stop tx > tt ✧ PE12/18 (tχ > tt)
    ᴱQ. intervocalic voiceless spirants became voiced VxV > VɣV ✧ PE12/16 (x > -; medial); PE12/18 (χ/h > ɣ)
