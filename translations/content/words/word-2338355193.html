√GWAL “be stirred, excited; emotion, movement of feelings”

A hypothetical root to explain words in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s such as G. ictha- “excite, agitate” and G. igin “excited, agitated” (GL/50). There are no signs of this root in Tolkien’s later writing, and it was likely replaced by later roots such as √GWAL.
