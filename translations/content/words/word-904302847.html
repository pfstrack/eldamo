Q. Siriondil m. “*Sirion-lover”

Q. Siriondil, m. “*Sirion-lover”
The 11th king of Gondor (LotR/1038), as well as the name of the father of Eärnil II (LotR/1050). This name seems to be a compound of the river name S. Sirion and the suffix -(n)dil “-friend, -lover”.

References ✧ LotRI; PMI

Elements

S. Sirion “Great River”
-(n)dil “-friend, -lover; devotion, disinterested love”
