ᴹQ. Nuaran pn. “*West-king”

ᴹQ. Nuaran, pn. “*West-king”

Part of a hypothetical title for the king of Númenor in Tolkien’s unfinished story “The Lost Road”. It is apparently a combination of the root NDŪ “down” with aran¹ “king” (LR/60, 71).
