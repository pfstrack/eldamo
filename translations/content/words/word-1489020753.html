S. Faenor m. “Spirit of Fire”

S. Faenor, m. “Spirit of Fire”

The proper form of Fëanor if it were a true Sindarin name (MR/217, PM/343). This name is a combination of fae “spirit” and the suffixal form -nor of naur “fire”.
