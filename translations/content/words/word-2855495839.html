⚠️ᴱQ. silë, n. “glint (of white)” (Category: Flash, Lightning)
ᴹQ. tindë “glint”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “a glint (of white)” derived from the early root ᴱ√SILI (QL/83).
