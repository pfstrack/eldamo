Q. Minohtar m. “*First Warrior”

Q. Minohtar, m. “*First Warrior”

Commander of the Gondorian forces against the Wainriders after the fall of king Ondoher (UT/292-4). His name seems to be a compound of minya “first” and ohtar “warrior”.
