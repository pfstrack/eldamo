G. gusta- v. “to burn (tr.), destroy” (Category: to Burn, Scorch)

⚠️G. gusta-, v. “to burn (tr.), destroy” (Category: to Burn, Scorch)
ᴺS. ^orna- “to burn (tr. and intr.)”
ᴺS. tertha- “to destroy, *ruin, †(orig.) to make pierced; ⚠️to devour”

A verb in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “burn (tr.), destroy” with variants gusta- and husta- derived from the root gudh- (GL/42), the Gnomish form of the early root ᴱ√USU/UŘU [ƷUÐU] (QL/98).
