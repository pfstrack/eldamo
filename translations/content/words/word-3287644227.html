ᴺQ. !menelwa adj. “heavenly” (Category: Sky, Heavens)

ᴺQ. !menelwa, adj. “heavenly” [created by ABNW] (Category: Sky, Heavens)

A neologism for “heavenly” appearing in Ales Bican’s New Words (ABNW) from the early 2000’s. It is simply an adjectival form of menel “the heavens”.

Elements

Q. menel “the heavens, firmament, sky”
