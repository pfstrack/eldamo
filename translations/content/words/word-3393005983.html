ᴺS. !ilu n. “universe, the whole, cosmos” (Category: Universe)

ᴺS. !ilu, n. “universe, the whole, cosmos” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Universe)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2021 specifically for Eldamo, the Sindarin equivalent of Q. ilu and Q. ilúvë as terms for the entire universe.

Cognates

  • Q. ilúvë “the whole, the all, allness”

Derivations

  • < ✶ilū “all, everything, the whole”
