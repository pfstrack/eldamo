S. †Rochirrim coll. “Horse-lords”

S. †Rochirrim, coll. “Horse-lords”

Archaic form of the name Rohirrim, a combination of roch “horse”, hîr “lord” and the class-plural suffix -rim “land” (Let/178, UT/318).
