N. Nírnaeth Aronoded pn.

N. Nírnaeth Aronoded, pn.

A variant of Nirnaith Arnediad appearing only in The Etymologies from the 1930s, using aronoded for “countless” instead of arnediad (Ety/NAY).

See S. Nirnaeth Arnoediad for further discussion.
