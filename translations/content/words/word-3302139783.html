N. Inglormindon loc.

N. Inglormindon, loc.

Earliest name for the Minas Tirith of Beleriand in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (LR/146 note #14), a combination of the name Inglor and mindon “tower”.

See S. Minas Tirith for further discussion.
