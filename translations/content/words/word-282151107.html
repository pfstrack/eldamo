Q. ambuna adj. “hilly” (Category: Hill, Mountain)

Q. ambuna, adj. “hilly” (Category: Hill, Mountain)

An adjectival form of Q. ambo “hill”, used when “describing ground that was generally flat but dotted with hummocks, hills, knolls” (PE17/92-93).
