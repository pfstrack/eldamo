ᴱQ. tunda- v. “to kindle” (Category: to Light, Kindle)

⚠️ᴱQ. tunda-, v. “to kindle” (Category: to Light, Kindle)
ᴹQ. narta- “to kindle, *ignite, inflame, set fire to”
Q. tinta- “to kindle, cause to spark, [ᴹQ.] make to spark”

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “kindle” with variants tunda- and turu- (the latter marked † as archaic), appearing under the early root ᴱ√TUŘU [TUÐU] of the same meaning (QL/96).
