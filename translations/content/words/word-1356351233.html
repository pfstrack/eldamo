Q. Telufinwë m. “Last Finwë”

Q. Telufinwë, m. “Last Finwë”

The father-name of S. Amras, youngest of the sons of Fëanor (PM/353). It is a compound of telu “last” and the name of his grandfather Finwë. The short form of his name was Telvo.
