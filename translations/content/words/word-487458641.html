ᴱQ. Tuivána f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Tuivána, f.

Another name for Vána in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/96, 99), a combination of her name with a prefixal form of the root ᴱ√TUẎU having to do with “spring”.
