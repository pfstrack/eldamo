G. gerd(h)olm n. “mine” (Category: Cave)

⚠️G. gerd(h)olm, n. “mine” (Category: Cave)
S. fela “mine, boring, tunnel, underground dwelling; ⚠️minor excavations, den; [N.] cave”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “mine” with variants gerdolm and gerdholm, a combination of G. groth “ore” and G. dolm “pit” (GL/38).
