S. Tegilbor m. “*Pen-fist”

S. Tegilbor, m. “*Pen-fist”

A term for one skilled in calligraphy (PM/318), a combination of tegil “pen” and the suffixal form -bor of paur “fist”.

References ✧ PM/318; VT47/8
