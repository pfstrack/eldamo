Q. Nornalië pn. “People of the Dwarves”

Q. Nornalië, pn. “People of the Dwarves”

A name for the Dwarvish people (WJ/388). This name is a compound of norna “stiff, tough”, from which the word Norno “Dwarf” is derived (WJ/413), and lië “people”.
