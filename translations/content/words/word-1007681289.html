Q. Tauremornalómë loc. “*Forest (of) Black Night”

Q. Tauremornalómë, loc. “*Forest (of) Black Night”

A name of Fangorn forest, longer form of Tauremorna (LotR/469). This name is a compound of taurë “forest”, morna “black” and lómë “night”.

References ✧ LotR/469; LotRI
