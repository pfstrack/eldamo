S. Methed-en-Glad loc. “End of the Wood”

S. Methed-en-Glad, loc. “End of the Wood”

A stronghold of the followers of Túrin translated “End of the Wood” (UT/153), a combination of methed “end”, en¹ “of the” and glad “wood”.
