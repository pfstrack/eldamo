N. Ennyn Dûr pn. “Gates of Mordor”

⚠️N. Ennyn Dûr, pn. “Gates of Mordor”

Earliest name (rejected) for S. Morannon (WR/113), a combination of the plural of annon “gate” and dûr “dark”.
