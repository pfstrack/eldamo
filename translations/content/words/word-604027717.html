Ilk. Regornion loc. “Hollin”

Ilk. Regornion loc. “Hollin”

A Doriathrin variation of Region appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/ÉREK). It was translated “Hollin” and appears to be the genitive plural of Ilk. regorn “holly-tree”.
