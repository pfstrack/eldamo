Ad. kulub coll. (strong-I neut) “roots, edible vegetables that are roots not fruits” (Category: Root)

Ad. kulub coll. (strong-I neut) “roots, edible vegetables that are roots not fruits” (Category: Root)

A collective-noun meaning “all edible root vegetables”, as opposed to kulbu used for an individual root (SD/431).
