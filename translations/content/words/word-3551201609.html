ᴱQ. Aulisan pn. “Tuesday”

⚠️ᴱQ. Aulisan, pn. “Tuesday”
Q. Aldúya “*Tuesday, Day of the Two Trees”

Name of the first Tuesday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the god Aule and sana “day”.
