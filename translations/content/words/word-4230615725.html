ᴺQ. !pertol (pertoll-) n. “peninsula” (Category: Isle, Island)

ᴺQ. !pertol (pertoll-), n. “peninsula” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Isle, Island)

A neologism coined by Tamas Ferencz for “penisula”, a combination of Q. per- “half” and Q. tol “island”, so literally “*semi-island”.

Elements

Q. #per- “half”
Q. tollë¹ “island, (steep) isle”

Cognates

  • ᴺS. !perthol “peninsula”
