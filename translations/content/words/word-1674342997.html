    OS. aspirates became voiceless spirants θθ < ttʰ ✧ VT47/14 (tth > tt > þþ); VT47/16 (tth > þþ)
    S. final [mf], [nθ], [ŋx], [lθ] became [mp], [nt], [ŋk], [lt] -lθ > -lt ✧ VT42/27 (lth > lt; finally)
    S. final [mf], [nθ], [ŋx], [lθ] became [mp], [nt], [ŋk], [lt] -nθ > -nt ✧ VT42/27 (nth > nt; finally)
    S. medial [mf], [nθ], [ŋx], [lθ] became [mm], [nn], [ŋg], [ll] -lθ- > -ll- ✧ PE17/131 (lþ > lh > ll); VT42/27 (lth > lh; medially)
    S. medial [mf], [nθ], [ŋx], [lθ] became [mm], [nn], [ŋg], [ll] -nθ- > -nn- ✧ PE17/131 (nþ > nh > nn); VT42/27 (nth > nh; medially)
    S. long voiceless spirants shortened θθ > θ ✧ VT47/14 (þþ > þ); VT47/16 (þþ > þ)
