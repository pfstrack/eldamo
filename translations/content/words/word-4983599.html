Q. Etyangoldi coll. “Exiled Noldor”

Q. Etyangoldi, coll. “Exiled Noldor”

A term for the Noldor as exiles (WJ/374). It is a compound of the adjective etya “exiled” and an archaic plural ✶Ñgoldi of Noldo.
