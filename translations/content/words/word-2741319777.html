ᴱQ. note (notsi-) n. “drizzle” (Category: Rain)

⚠️ᴱQ. notë (notsi-), n. “drizzle” (Category: Rain)
Q. mistë “drizzle, [ᴹQ.] fine rain”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “drizzle” derived from the early root ᴱ√NOTO from primitive *[not]i, hence with stem form notsi- since [ti] became [tsi] in Early Qenya; compare with the adjective form ᴱQ. notsiva “drizzling” (QL/68).
