Q. Írimon m.

Q. Írimon, m.
The given name of Tar-Meneldur (UT/219). The elements of this name are difficult to discern, but might be írë “desire” and a variant of the agental suffix -mo.

References ✧ UT/219; UTI

Elements

#írë? “desire”
-mo? “agental suffix”
