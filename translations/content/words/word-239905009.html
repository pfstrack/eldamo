Ilk. Lómendor loc.

Ilk. Lómendor loc.

Ilkorin name of N. Dor-lómen, from which the Noldorin name was derived (Ety/LAM). It is a combination of lómen “echoing” and dôr “land”.

See North S. Dor-lómin for further discussion.
