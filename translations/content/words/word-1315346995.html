Q. Tatalië pn. “*Company of Tata”

Q. Tatalië, pn. “*Company of Tata”

Term for the second tribe of Elves as companions of ✶Tata, the first Elf of that tribe to awake, a combination of his name and Q. lië “people”

References ✧ NM/103, 118

Elements

✶Tata “Two”
lië “people, folk”
