S. Aramund m. “Kingly Bull”

S. Aramund, m. “Kingly Bull”

A name coined by Tolkien as a possible Elvish name for a bull (Let/423). The initial element of the name is the prefix ar(a)- “noble” and its final element is mund “bull”.
