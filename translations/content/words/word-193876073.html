Q. Firindil m. “Friend of Men”

Q. Firindil, m. “Friend of Men”

A variant form of Atandil (VT41/14). It is probably a compound of Fírima “Mortal Man” and the suffix -(n)dil “friend”.

Reference ✧ VT41/14 ✧ “Friend of Men”

Elements

Fírima “Mortal, (lit.) One Apt to Die”
-(n)dil “-friend, -lover; devotion, disinterested love”
