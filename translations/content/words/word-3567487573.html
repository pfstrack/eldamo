S. Gaurwaith pn. “Wolf-men”

S. Gaurwaith, pn. “Wolf-men”

Name of the band of outlaws that Túrin joined, translated “Wolf-men” (UT/85), a combination of gaur “werewolf” and gwaith “people”.
