ᴹQ. Máraher m. “Good Steward”

ᴹQ. Máraher, m. “Good Steward”

The earliest name of Mardil, translated “Good Steward” (WR/153), most likely a compound of mára “good” and heru “lord”, as suggested by Roman Rausch (EE/3.15).
