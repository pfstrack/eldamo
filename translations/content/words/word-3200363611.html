ᴱ√VṚTYṚ root. “serve”

⚠️ᴱ√VṚTYṚ root. “serve”
√(N)DUR “to show special interest in things; [ᴹ√] bow down, bend (low), obey, serve; ⚠️grow/be dark”
ᴹ√BEW “follow, serve”

A root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “serve”, with derivatives like ᴱQ. vartyane “service” and ᴱQ. virti- “serve” (QL/102). There are no signs of this root in Tolkien’s later writing.
