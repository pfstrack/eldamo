    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids rβ < rɸ ✧ PE12/20 (liquids + ꝑ > rb)
    ᴱQ. [ɸ] usually became [f] βu < ɸu ✧ PE12/20 (ꝑ > ƀ; before ū̆)
