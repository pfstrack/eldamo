ᴱQ. winwe (winwi-) n. “a sparkling, flash” (Category: Flash, Lightning)

⚠️ᴱQ. winwë (winwi-), n. “a sparkling, flash” (Category: Flash, Lightning)
ᴺQ. ^tintilië “sparkling, twinkling”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s given as ’winwe “a sparkling, flash” derived from the early root ᴱ√GWINI “sparkle” (QL/104).
