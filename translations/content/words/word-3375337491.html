Q. Hravani coll. “Wild-men, Savages”

Q. Hravani, coll. “Wild-men, Savages”

A term for savage humans, appearing both as Hravani (WJ/219) and Hrávani (PE17/18). The term is the plural of the noun hravan “wild beast”.
