ᴺQ. !tauron (taurond-) n. “forester” (Category: Woods, Forest)

⚠️ᴺQ. !tauron (taurond-), n. “forester” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Woods, Forest)
Q. ornendur “tree-keep, forester, woodsman”

A neologism for “forester” created by Boris Shapiro in the early 2000s, equivalent to S. Tauron (PPQ). I think it is preferable to use the now-attested word Q. ornendur, published in 2021.

Cognates

  • Q. Taurosso “Forest-warden”
  • S. Tauron “Lord of Forests, (lit.) Forester”
