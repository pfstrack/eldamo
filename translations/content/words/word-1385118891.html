S. Ras Mewrim loc. “*Cape of the Gulls”

S. Ras Mewrim, loc. “*Cape of the Gulls”

A variant name of Bar-in-Mŷl (WJ/190), apparently a combination of ras(t) “cape” and the class plural of maew “gull”.
