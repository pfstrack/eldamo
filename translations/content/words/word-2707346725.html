S. Aranrúth pn. “King’s Ire”

S. Aranrúth, pn. “King’s Ire”

The name of Thingol’s sword (S/201), translated by Christopher Tolkien as “King’s Ire” (SI/Aranrúth), a combination aran “king” and rûth “anger, wrath” (SA/ar(a), rûth).
