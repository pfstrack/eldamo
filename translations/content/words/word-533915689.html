ᴱQ. †Varastirion loc.

⚠️ᴱQ. †Varastirion, loc.

Archaic Qenya cognate of G. Gwarestrin “Tower of Guard” in an early name list (PE13/102), perhaps a combination of some form of the root ᴱ√VṚÐṚ having to do with rulership and tirion “tower”.
