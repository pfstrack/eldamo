ᴹQ. Andalóke pn. “Long Serpent”

ᴹQ. Andalócë, pn. “Long Serpent”

Earlier name for Alcarondas from Númenórean stories from the 1940s (SD/350), apparently a compound of anda “long” and lóke “dragon”.
