Ad. êphalak adj. “far away” (Category: Far)

Ad. êphalak adj. “far away” (Category: Far)

A combination of êphal “far” and the suffix -ak (SD/247, 312), which could either mean “away” or be some kind of intensifier. See the entry for -ak for further discussion.
