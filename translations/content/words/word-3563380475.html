S. Halbarad m.

S. Halbarad, m.

A Dúnadan ranger (LotR/744). The meaning of this name is unclear, but it might be a combination of (N.) hall² “high” and barad “tower” (as suggested by David Salo, GS/352).

Conceptual Development: This character was already N. Halbarad when he first appeared in Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s (WR/297).

References ✧ LotRI; TII

Elements

N. hall²? “exalted, high”
barad? “tower, great towering building”
