ᴱQ. Mavoine f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Mavoinë, f.

Qenya cognate of Mavwin in the earliest Lost Tales (LT2/70), it is maivoine “great longing” used as a name, according to Christopher Tolkien (LT2A/Mavwin).

See S. Morwen for further discussion.
