Q. Eärnil m. “*Lover of the Sea”

Q. Eärnil, m. “*Lover of the Sea”

The name of the 13th and 32nd kings of Gondor (LotR/1038), it is essentially a shorter form of Eärendil, with the same elements (SA/(n)dil).
