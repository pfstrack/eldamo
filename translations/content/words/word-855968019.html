ᴱQ. Rávi f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Rávi, f.

A name Meásse in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/79), derived from the root ᴱ√RAVA having to do with hunting, as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Meássë).
