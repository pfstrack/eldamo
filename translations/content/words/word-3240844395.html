✶Ad. voiced-stops

✶Ad. voiced-stops
These sounds are pronounced as they are in English, with the exception of the palatal [ɟ] which does not exist in English. These sounds mostly survived into Classical Adûnaic (again, with the exception of the palatal [ɟ], which became [z]).
