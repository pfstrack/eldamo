S. Gonnhirrim coll. “Masters of Stone”

S. Gonnhirrim, coll. “Masters of Stone”

A name of the Dwarves translated “Masters of Stone” (S/91), a combination of gond “stone”, hîr “lord, master” and the class-plural suffix -rim (SA/gond, heru).
