Q. Luinil pn. “*Blue Star”

Q. Luinil, pn. “*Blue Star”
The name of a blue star (S/48, SI/Luinil), possibly Neptune (MR/435). This name is perhaps a compound of luinë (luini-) “blue” and †él “star”.
