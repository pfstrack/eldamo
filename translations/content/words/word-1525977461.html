ᴱQ. niqilis n. “fine snow” (Category: Snow)

⚠️ᴱQ. niquilis, n. “fine snow” (Category: Snow)
Q. lossë¹ “snow, fallen snow; snow-white, snowy”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “fine snow” derived from the early root ᴱ√NIQI “white” (QL/66).
