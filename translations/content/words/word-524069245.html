ᴱQ. yanda adj. “dark, gloomy” (Category: Dark, Murky)

⚠️ᴱQ. yanda, adj. “dark, gloomy” (Category: Dark, Murky)
Q. lumba “gloomy”
Q. yanda “wide”

An adjective in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “dark, gloomy”, a derivative of the early root ᴱ√YAÐA (QL/105).
