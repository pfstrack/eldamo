Q. Arda Envinyanta pn. “Arda Healed”

Q. Arda Envinyanta, pn. “Arda Healed”

A term for the future state Arda, after the corruption of Morgoth is eliminated (MR/405).

Conceptual Development: This term was first written Arda Vincarna.
