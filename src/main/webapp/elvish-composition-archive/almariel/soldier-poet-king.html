Soldier, Poet, King

by Almáriel

Cé tuluva taura mahtar
coluvas andamacil
ostolya nancaruvas

Cé tuluva finya maitar
quettarya maica nauva
nahtuvas lambaryanen

Cé tuluva mára cáno
cáno i lieno
calma i huineo!

Maybe a mighty warrior will come
he will carry a longsword
he will destroy your city

Maybe a clever poet will come
His word will be a blade
he will slay with his tongue

Maybe a good ruler will come
A ruler from the people
The light from the gloom
