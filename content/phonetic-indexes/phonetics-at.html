    [mt], [md] became [mpt] [m{ttʰd}|m{kkʰg}] > [mpt]
    short unstressed vowels vanished before [l], [r] to produce favored initial clusters [{ptkpʰkʰbdgm}V₁{rl}V́₁-] > [{ptkpʰkʰbdgm}ø{rl}V́₁-] 00100
    labialized velars became labials [kw|kʰw|gw|ŋgw|ŋkw|ŋw-] > [p|pʰ|b|mb|mp|m-] 00200
    [j] was lost after initial dental [{ttʰdnl}j-] > [{ttʰdnl}-] 00300
    [ln] became [ll] [ln] > [ll] 00400
    final voiceless stops and [s] vanished in polysyllables [-SV{ptks}] > [-SVø] 00500
