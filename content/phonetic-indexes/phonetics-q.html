Quenya Phonetics

My analysis of Late Quenya phonology is based mainly Tolkien’s “Outline of Phonology” from the 1950s (PE19/68-107), though it references material written throughout Tolkien’s life, and includes discussion of the conceptual development of Tolkien’s thoughts on Quenya phonology from the 1910s up through 1970. This analysis was completed in October of 2019, and may not reflect material published after that point.
