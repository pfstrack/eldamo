Sindarin Grammar

This section of Eldamo outlines what we know about Sindarin grammar, including both its conceptual development and recommendations for its use for the purposes of Neo-Sindarin writing. It includes quite a bit of detailed research, and is not particularly aimed at “beginners”, though someone with a good background with languages in general should be able to follow the material. If you are new to Sindarin and just want a general overview of how to use the language, you should look at the entry on basic grammar instead.
