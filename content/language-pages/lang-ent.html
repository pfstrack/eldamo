Entish

  • Words (2)

Tolkien included a couple partial phrases from the long-winded and rambling Entish language in The Lord of the Rings (LotR/465-6) but not enough so say much definite about it. Some of the flavor of the language can also be seen in the “Ent-style” English and Quenya used by Treebeard (LotR/467, 979).
