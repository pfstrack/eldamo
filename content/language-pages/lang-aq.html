Ancient Quenya

  • Words (8)
  • Phonetics (91)

The form of the Quenya language spoken by the Vanyar and Noldor when they first arrived in Aman. It predates the invention of tengwar and thus survived mostly in ancient songs, though some texts were recorded in the older Rúmilian alphabet (PE19/68). Some phonetic developments in Quenya can be dated specifically to this period, which is roughly contemporary with Ancient Telerin.
