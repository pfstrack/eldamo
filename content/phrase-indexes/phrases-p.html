Primitive Elvish Phrases

    • aba-kar ā
    • eledāim orontēr “the Elves arose”
    • galadā atta “2 trees”
    • galadā kanta “4 trees”
    • kiryā kyulmā “a ship’s mast”
    • lāni/lanjē karnē-sa “I did not do it”
    • le tulir “come ye!”
    • lḗ tuli, tuli lḗ, āle tuli! “come (you)!, now you, come!”
    • sē tuli, tuli sḗ, āse tuli! “let him come! etc.”
