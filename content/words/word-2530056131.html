ᴺQ. !epta- v. “to offer, place at somebody’s disposal, (lit.) to [make be in] front” (Category: Sacrifice, Offering)

ᴺQ. !epta-, v. “to offer, place at somebody’s disposal, (lit.) to [make be in] front” [created by Ellanto] (Category: Sacrifice, Offering)

A neologism coined by Ellanto posted on 2024-05-27 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of epë “before (spatially)” with the causative suffix -ta, so more literally “to [make be in] front”.

Elements

Q. epë “after (of time), following; before (in all relations but time)”
Q. -ta “causative verb suffix”
