S. Esgaliant loc. “Bridge of Esgalduin”

S. Esgaliant, loc. “Bridge of Esgalduin”

A variant name of Iant Iaur, the bridge over the river Esgalduin (WJ/333). This name is a combination of esgal “shadow, screen” and iant “bridge”.
