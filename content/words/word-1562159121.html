Q. Almarian f.

Q. Almarian, f.
The daughter of Vëantur and wife of Tar-Meneldur (UT/173). Her name might contain (ᴹQ.) almare “blessedness”.

References ✧ UT/210; UTI

Elements

ᴹQ. almare “blessedness, blessings, good fortune, bliss”
