Reference ✧ PE12/20

Order ()

After final [ɸ] became [xʷ]
Before later [j], [w] became [i], [u] finally or between a vowel and a consonant
Before [ð] became [z]
Before 02200 final [ɣ] vanished with compensatory lengthening
