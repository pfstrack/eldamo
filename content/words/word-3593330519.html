ᴺQ. !eretildo n. “unicorn” (Category: Horse (generic))

ᴺQ. !eretildo, n. “unicorn” [created by Petri Tikka, PPQ] (Category: Horse (generic))

A neologism coined by Petri Tikka in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, a combination of Q. er “one” and Q. tildë “point”.

Elements

Q. er “one, alone”
Q. tildë “a fine sharp point”
