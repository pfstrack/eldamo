Q. Culúrien, pn.

Another name for Laurelin (S/38). The initial element is probably a derivative of √KUL “golden-red” (SA/cul), but the meaning of the rest of the name is unclear.

Conceptual Development: The name first appeared as ᴹQ. Kulúrien in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (LR/210).
