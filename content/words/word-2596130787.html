ᴱQ. Manwisan pn. “Sunday”

⛔️ᴱQ. Manwisan, pn. “Sunday”
Q. Anarya “Sunday, (lit.) Sun-day”

Name of the first Sunday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the god Manwe and sana “day”.
