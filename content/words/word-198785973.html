G. gwaid n. “kinsmen, relations; companion” (Category: Relative)

⚠️G. gwaid, n. “kinsmen, relations; companion” (Category: Relative)
N. gwanur “kinsman, kinswoman”
N. gwador “(sworn) brother, associate”
N. gwathel “[sworn] sister, associate [f.]”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s appearing as {gwâd >>} gwaid “kinsman, relations” derived from primitive ᴱ✶ŋuaʒet- = gwa- “together” + ged “†kinsman”, with a secondary meaning of “companion” (GL/43).
