ᴱQ. wintil n. “a glint” (Category: Flash, Lightning)

⚠️ᴱQ. wintil, n. “a glint” (Category: Flash, Lightning)
ᴹQ. tindë “glint”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s given as ’wintil “a glint” derived from the early root ᴱ√GWINI “sparkle” (QL/104).
