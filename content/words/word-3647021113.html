Ilk. esgar¹ n. “wound”

A noun for “wound” derived from the root ᴹ√SKAR¹ (Ety/SKAR). Apparently the initial [sk-] became syllabic [ṣk-], which then became [esk-]. Afterwards the [esk] became [esg].
