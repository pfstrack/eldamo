ᴹQ. umanima adj. “of some kind” (Category: Some, Several)

⚠️ᴹQ. umanima, adj. “of some kind” (Category: Some, Several)
ᴺQ. !umaitë “of some kind”

The correlative ᴹQ. umanima “of some kind” appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/107), a combination of ᴹQ. uma- “some” and ᴹQ. -ima “kind”.

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. umaitë “of some kind” using the later suffix Q. -itë.
