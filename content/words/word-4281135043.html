S. Bar-en-Danwedh loc. “House of Ransom”

The name that Mîm gave to his home after he was forced to give it to Túrin in exchange for his life, translated “House of Ransom” (S/203). The initial element of the name is bar “house” (SA/bar), followed by en¹ “of” and danwedh “ransom”.
