N. Eredvyrn loc. “Black Mountains”

N. Eredvyrn, loc. “Black Mountains”

Earliest name for S. Ered Nimrais glossed “Black Mountains” instead of “White Mountains” (TI/124), with the lenited plural of morn “black” instead of nimrais “white-horns”.
