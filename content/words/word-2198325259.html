S. Taur-na-Neldor loc. “Beech-forest”

S. Taur-na-Neldor, loc. “Beech-forest”

Another name for Neldoreth (LotR/469) translated “Beech-forest” (RC/384). It is a combination of taur “forest”, na(n) “of” and neldor “beech”.
