Q. Valarauko, pn. “Demon of Might”

Quenya name for a Balrog, a combination of some derivative of the root √BAL “power” with rauco “demon” (SA/rauko, val; PE17/48). It also appeared as Valarauka (PE17/48).

Conceptual Development: In the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s, their name was either ᴱQ. Valkarauke “*Cruel Demon” or ᴱQ. Malkarauke “*Torment Demon” (QL/58, 60, 101-2). In the Etymologies from the 1930s, the name became ᴹQ. Malarauko of similar meaning: “*Torment Demon” (Ety/RUK). The forms Valarauka or Valarauko emerged in Tolkien’s later writing (PE17/48, WJ/415).
