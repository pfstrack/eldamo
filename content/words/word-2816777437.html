G. fadrog adj. “tiresome” (Category: Tired, Weary)

⚠️G. fadrog, adj. “tiresome” (Category: Tired, Weary)
N. amben “uphill; [ᴱN.] arduous, difficult, tiresome”

A word in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “tiresome”, an adjectival form of G. fadra- “to sate, weary” (GL/33).
