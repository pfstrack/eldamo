ᴱQ. Oresul (Oresúl-) pn. “Eastwind”

⚠️ᴱQ. Oresul (Oresúl-), pn. “Eastwind”

Name of the eastern wind in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/71), a combination of óre “dawn, East” with a variant of súlime “wind”.
