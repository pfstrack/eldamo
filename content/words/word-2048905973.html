✶Ad. plural-nouns grammar.

The only actual grammatical rule we know of from Primitive Adûnaic was the formation of noun plurals. According to Tolkien, the original plural inflexion was ✶-yī or perhaps ✶-yi, which developed into -î- after medial [w] and [j] vanished before [u] and [i] (SD/424).

Examples (plural)
izray+yi ← izray ✧ SD/424.2805
khăwī ← khăw ✧ SD/426.2803
