ᴺQ. !minatulië n. “introduction; (lit.) into-leading” (Category: to Meet)

ᴺQ. !minatulië, n. “introduction; (lit.) into-leading” [created by Röandil] (Category: to Meet)

A neologism coined by Röandil posted on 2024-06-03 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of mina “into” and the gerund tulië of tulya- “lead”.

Elements

Q. mina “into, in”
Q. tulya- “*to lead; to fetch”
