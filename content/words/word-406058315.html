ᴺS. !rostaur n. “rainforest” (Category: Woods, Forest)

ᴺS. !rostaur, n. “rainforest” [created by Elaran] (Category: Woods, Forest)

A neologism for “rainforest” originally coined by Elaran in 2019 (revised from rostor to rostaur in 2021), a combination of S. ross “rain” and S. taur “forest”.

Elements

S. ross¹ “rain; spindrift, spray, foam”
S. taur “forest, wood”

Cognates
