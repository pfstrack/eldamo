ᴺQ. !carpo n. “frog” (Category: Animals (other))

⚠️ᴺQ. !carpo, n. “frog” [created by Eddin Najetovic, PPQ] (Category: Animals (other))
Q. quácë “frog”

A neologism for “frog” created by Eddin Najetovic in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, equivalent to N. cabor “frog”. I think it is preferable to use the now-attested word Q. quácë “frog”, published in 2005.
