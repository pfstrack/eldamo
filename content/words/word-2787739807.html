ᴺQ. !mellaman (mellamn-) n. “pet, (lit.) love-animal” (Category: Animal)

ᴺQ. !mellaman (mellamn-), n. “pet, (lit.) love-animal” [created by Luinyelle, NotD] (Category: Animal)
A neologism coined by Luinyelle in the “Neologism of the Day” series on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) on 2023-04-11, a combination of mel- “love” and laman “animal”.

Elements

Q. #mel- “to love”
Q. laman “animal”
