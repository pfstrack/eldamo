Ad. other-continuants

Ad. other-continuants
A group for continuants that do not fall into other categories. These are mostly survivals of the same sounds in Primitive Adûnaic, with the exception of breath-[h] which developed from Primitive Adûnaic [x], which Tolkien also represented with “H” (SD/418).
