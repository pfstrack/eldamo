ᴺQ. !semellë adv. “in few ways, in few respects” (Category: Few, Little)

ᴺQ. !semellë, adv. “in few ways, in few respects” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Few, Little)
See ᴹQ. semel(de) for discussion.

Elements

ᴹQ. sem(p)- “few, little, not many, not much”
Q. *-llë⁴ “like, in the same way/manner”

      ᴹQ. semel(de) adv. “in few ways, in few respects” (Category: Few, Little)

      The correlative ᴹQ. semel(de) “in few ways, in few respects” appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/111), a combination of ᴹQ. sem(p)- “few” and ᴹQ. -l(de) “ways”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. semellë using the later suffix -llë.
