Q. Estelmo, m. “*Hope-person”

Q. Estelmo, m. “*Hope-person”

The squire of Elendur (UT/276). His name seems to be a compound of estel “hope” and the agental suffix -mo.

Elements

estel “hope, trust, *faith”
