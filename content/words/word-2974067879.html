N. gilhof n. “dew, *(lit.) star-juice” (Category: Mist, Fog, Haze)

N. gilhof, n. “dew, *(lit.) star-juice” (Category: Mist, Fog, Haze)

A noun for “dew” in a rough notes from 1940, apparently a combination of N. geil “star” and ON. sōba “juice” (TMME/53).
