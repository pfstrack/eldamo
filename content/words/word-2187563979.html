ᴺQ. !carcatangwa n. “zip fastener, zipper, (lit.) tooth-clasp” (Category: Adornment (personal))

ᴺQ. !carcatangwa, n. “zip fastener, zipper, (lit.) tooth-clasp” [created by Etil Tolvo, Luinyelle] (Category: Adornment (personal))

A neologism for a “zip fastener” coined by Etil Tolvo and Luinyelle, posted on 2024-09-27 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of carca “tooth” and [ᴹQ.] tangwa “clasp”.

Elements

Q. #hasta- “to mar”
ᴹQ. tangwa “hasp, clasp”
