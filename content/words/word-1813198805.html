Q. Yávien f.

Q. Yávien, f.
First child of Nolondil, known only from a genealogy chart on UT/210. Her name seems to be a compound of yávë “fruit” and the feminine suffix -ien¹. This name sometimes appeared as Yávië (UT/9).
