ᴺQ. !lantasírë n. “waterfall, (lit.) falling river” (Category: Brook, Stream, River)

ᴺQ. !lantasírë, n. “waterfall, (lit.) falling river” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Brook, Stream, River)
ᴱ√ASAKA “*waterfall”
ᴱQ. axa¹ “waterfall”

A neologism for “waterfall” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, a combination with Q. lanta “falling” and Q. sírë “river”.

Elements

Q. lanta “fall; falling”
Q. sír(ë) “river”

Cognates

  • S. lanthir “waterfall”
