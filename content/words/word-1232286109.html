ᴺQ. !linítë adj. “of several kinds, of several sorts” (Category: Much, Many)

ᴺQ. !linítë, adj. “of several kinds, of several sorts” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Much, Many)
See ᴹQ. linima for discussion.

Elements

Q. #li(n)- “many”
Q. -itë “adjectival ending; [with verbs] capable of doing, generally (and naturally) doing”

      ᴹQ. linima adj. “of several sorts, of several kinds” (Category: Much, Many)

      The correlative ᴹQ. linima “of several sorts” appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/107), a combination of ᴹQ. li(n)- “several, many” and ᴹQ. -ima “sorts”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. linítë “of several kinds, of several sorts” using the later suffix -itë, as in taitë “of that sort”.
