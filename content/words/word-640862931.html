G. Thlim Losglóriol coll.

⚠️G. Thlim Losglóriol, coll.

Name for the people of Glorfindel in an early name list (PE13/104), a combination of thlim “race”, lôs “flower” and glôriol “golden”. A reduced form Los Glōriol >> Los ’lōriol also appeared in the Name-list to the Fall of Gondolin (PE15/25).
