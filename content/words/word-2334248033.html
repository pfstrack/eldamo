ᴺQ. !nestando n. “healer, physician” (Category: Physician)

⚠️ᴺQ. !nestando, n. “healer, physician” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Physician)
Q. asarta “doctor, leech”

A neologism for “healer, physician” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), an elaboration of [N.] nesta- “to heal”. I would use the attested nouns Q. asarta [aþarta] or Q. asar(o) [aþar(o)] “doctor, leech” instead (PE22/166).
