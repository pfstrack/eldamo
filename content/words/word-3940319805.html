ᴱQ. tump-¹ v. “to build; to beat” (Category: to Build)

⚠️ᴱQ. tump-¹, v. “to build; to beat” (Category: to Build)
ᴹQ. #ampana- “to build”
ᴹQ. palpa- “to beat, batter; *to clap, applaud (with palms)”

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “build” under the early root ᴱ√TṂPṂ of the same meaning (QL/93). It reappeared in the Early Qenya Grammar of the 1920s with this same derivation but with the gloss “beat” (PE14/58).
