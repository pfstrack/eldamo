S. Parth Celebrant loc. “Field of Celebrant”

S. Parth Celebrant, loc. “Field of Celebrant”

Sindarin name of the lands around the river Celebrant, generally translated “Field of Celebrant” (PM/330, UT/260), a combination of parth “field” and the river name.
