N. Enedhin pn. “Midyear week”

N. Enedhin, pn. “Midyear week”

A term for the Midyear week appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s (LotR/LEP, EtyAC/LEP), apparently a combination of enedh “middle” and în “year”.
