S. faran n. “rowan”

A Sindarin cognate of Q. farnë (PE17/83), possibly replacing N. fêr “beech-tree”. For the purpose of Neo-Sindarin writing, it is probably preferable to use the better-known brethil¹ for beech-trees and their relatives.

See Q. ‽farnë for further discussion.
