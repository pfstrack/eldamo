S. Taur-na-Foen loc. “Forest of the Foen”

Another name for Dorthonion translated “Forest of the Foen” in a philological fragment of uncertain date, a combination of taur¹ “forest”, na² “of” and the mountain name Foen (WJ/187 note #32).
