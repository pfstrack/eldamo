ᴱQ. pekte (peksi-) n. “plume, cox-comb” (Category: Feather)

⚠️ᴱQ. pectë (pexi-), n. “plume, cox-comb” (Category: Feather)
ᴹQ. lúpë “plume”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “plume, comb (of cock)” under the early root ᴱ√PEKE (QL/73), also mentioned in the contemporaneous Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa in its stem-form pektĭ- “cox-comb” (PME/73).
