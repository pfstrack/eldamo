ᴱQ. Qerkaringa loc. “Chill Gulf”

⚠️ᴱQ. Qercaringa, loc. “Chill Gulf”

The gulf between Helkarakse and Middle-earth in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/167), a combination of qerka “throat” with ringa “cold”.
