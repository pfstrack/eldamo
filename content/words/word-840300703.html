Ad. Angelimir m.

The 20th prince of Dol Amroth (PM/223). The language and meaning of this name are not clear, but based on the idea that the princes of Dol Amroth took Adûnaic names (as discussed in the entry for Adrahil), this lexicon assumes the name is Adûnaic. David Salo suggested instead it was Sindarin (GS/341).
