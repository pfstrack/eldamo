Q. lumba adj. “gloomy” (Category: Dark, Murky)

Q. lumba, adj. “gloomy” (Category: Dark, Murky)
ᴱQ. yanda “dark, gloomy”

A word for “gloomy” appearing in some Notes on Galadriel’s Song (NGS) from the late 1950s or early 1960s, an adjective form of lumbo “gloom” (PE17/72).
