S. Edhellos f. “Elven-flower”

S. Edhellos, f. “Elven-flower”

The Sindarin name of the wife of Angrod, a direct translation of her Quenya name Eldalótë (PM/346). This name is a compound of Edhel “Elf” and -los “flower”.
