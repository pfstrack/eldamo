ᴺQ. !nindornë n. “palm-tree” (Category: Tree (other))

⚠️ᴺQ. !nindornë, n. “palm-tree” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Tree (other))
ᴺQ. ^paltalda “palm tree”

A neologism for “palm tree” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT). The second element is ornë “tree”, and the first element might be ninda “wet” or [ᴹQ.] ninde “slender”. I prefer ᴺQ. paltalda “palm tree” adapted from ᴱQ. paltyalda.

Elements

ᴹQ. ninde “slender”
Q. ornë “(tall) tree”
