ᴺQ. !lirda n. “stanza, verse, (lit.) a product of singing” (Category: Music)

ᴺQ. !lirda, n. “stanza, verse, (lit.) a product of singing” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Music)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-05-23 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of lir- “sing” and the suffix -da “product of an action”.

Elements

Q. #lir- “to sing”
Q. -da “product of an action”
