ᴺQ. !tyocolat n. “chocolate” (Category: Food (Other))

ᴺQ. !tyocolat, n. “chocolate” [created by Orondil] (Category: Food (Other))

A neologism coined by Orondil on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) in 2021-03-04, a loan word from Spanish “chocolate” (< Nahuatl “chocola-tl”). Possible variants include tyocolátë, tyocolattë.
