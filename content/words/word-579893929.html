ᴹQ. Alataire loc. “Great Sea”

ᴹQ. Alatairë, loc. “Great Sea”

Another name of the “Great Sea” (N. Belegoer) appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s as compound of alta “great (in size)” and aire “sea” (Ety/ÁLAT, AY).
