Dan. snæ̂s n. “gore”

A noun for “gore” (triangle) developed from the root ᴹ√SNAS/SNAT (Ety/SNAS/SNAT), perhaps from a primitive form *✶snais, since [ai] became [ǣ] in Ossiriandic.
