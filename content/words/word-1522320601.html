ᴱN. thrond n. “sky lord” (Category: Eagle, Hawk)

⚠️ᴱN. thrond, n. “sky lord; [G.] (eyrie), pinnacle” (Category: Eagle, Hawk)
S. Thorondor “King of Eagles”

A noun in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s glossed “sky lord”, clearly related to ᴱN. thorn “eagle” (PE13/154).

Reference ✧ PE13/154 ✧ “sky lord”

      G. thrond n. “(eyrie), pinnacle” (Category: Eagle, Hawk)

      A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “(eyrie) pinnacle”, clearly related to G. thorn “eagle”, and either a variant of or replacement for G. inthorn “eagle’s nest, eyrie” (GL/73).
