ᴱQ. famba n. “belly, fat body” (Category: Belly, Stomach)

⚠️ᴱQ. famba, n. “belly, fat body” (Category: Belly, Stomach)
ᴺQ. !cumbo “belly”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “belly, fat body” under early root ᴱ√FAɃA (QL/36).
