ᴺQ. !umaurë adv. “someday” (Category: Some, Several)

ᴺQ. !umaurë, adv. “someday” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Some, Several)
See ᴹQ. umanar(yas) for discussion.

Elements

ᴹQ. uma- “some, a, a certain”
Q. aurë “day (as opposed to night), daylight, sunlight, morning”

      ᴹQ. umanar(yas) adv. “someday; in a certain day” (Category: Some, Several)

      The correlatives ᴹQ. umanar “someday” and um(an)aryas “in a certain day” appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/109), a combination of ᴹQ. uma- “some” and ᴹQ. -ar(yas) “day”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. umaurë “someday” using Q. aurë “day”.
