ᴱQ. †Tiri(o)stirion loc.

⚠️ᴱQ. †Tiri(o)stirion, loc.

Archaic Qenya cognate of G. Gwarestrin “Tower of Guard” in an early name list (PE13/102), perhaps a combination of some form of the verb tiri- “to guard” and tirion “tower”.

Reference ✧ PE13/102

Related

  • †Tiri(o)stirion or †Varastirion ✧ PE13/102

Elements

tiri- “to watch; to keep, guard, preserve; to look at, gaze at, observe”
tirion “(great or mighty) tower; city on a hill”

Cognates

  • G. Gwarestrin “Tower of Guard, Watchtower” ✧ PE13/102
