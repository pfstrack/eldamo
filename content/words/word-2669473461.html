ᴺQ. !rúcinon (rúcinond-) n. “breccia, (lit.) ruined stone” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !rúcinon (rúcinond-), n. “breccia, (lit.) ruined stone” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism for “breccia” coined by Luinyelle in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of rúcina “ruined” and ondo “stone”.

Elements

Q. rúcina “confused, shattered, disordered, ruined”
Q. ondo “stone (as a material), (large mass of) rock”
