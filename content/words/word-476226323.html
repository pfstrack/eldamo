N. Gwathfuin-Daidelos loc. “Deadly Nightshade”

An earlier name for S. Deldúwath appearing in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, glossed “Deadly Nightshade” (LR/133) or “Night of Dread’s Shadow” (LR/406). It is a combination of gwath¹ “shade”, fuin “night” and the lenited form of Daedhelos “Shadow of Fear”.

See S. Deldúwath for further discussion.
