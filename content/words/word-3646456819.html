ᴺQ. !enaurë, adv. “on that (future) day” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: After)
A neologism coined as part of my research into Quenya correlatives.

Elements

Q. en “then soon”
Q. aurë “day (as opposed to night), daylight, sunlight, morning”
