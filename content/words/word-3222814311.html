ᴺQ. !narasta- v. “to pasture, *graze, give to feed” (Category: to Pasture)

⚠️ᴺQ. !narasta-, v. “to pasture, *graze, give to feed” [created by Boris Shapiro] (Category: to Pasture)
ᴺQ. nesta-¹ “to graze, pasture, give to feed, feed”

A neologism for “to pasture” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, based on N. nadhras “pasture”. I prefer instead to adapt the verb nesta- from the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s as the verb for “to graze, pasture, give to feed, feed”.
