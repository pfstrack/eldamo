Q. Tumbaletaurëa pn. “Deepvalleyforested”

A descriptive name of Lórien appearing in the even longer Entish description of that land: Taurelilómëa-tumbalemorna Tumbaletaurëa Lómëanor (LotR/467). The name is a combination of tumbalë “deep valley, depth” and taurëa “forested” (LotR/1131, PE17/80).
