N. Angrobel loc. “Irongarth”

N. Angrobel, loc. “Irongarth”

Earliest form of the name of Angrenost, translated “Irongarth” (TI/71). It seems to be a combination of a form of ang “iron” and gobel “village”, as suggested by Roman Rausch (EE/2.4).
