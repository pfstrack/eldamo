Ilk. Tor Tinduma pn. “King of Twilight”

Ilk. Tor Tinduma pn. “King of Twilight”

A title for Thingol appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s, a combination of tôr “king” and the genitive of tindum “starry twilight” (Ety/THIN, TIN).
