ᴱQ. raustea adj. “of prey” (Category: to Hunt)

⚠️ᴱQ. raustëa, adj. “of prey” (Category: to Hunt)
ᴹQ. farastëa “of the chase (that it is proper to hunt), *of prey”

An adjective form of the noun ᴱQ. rauste “hunting” with the translation “of prey” (QL/79), hence used of animals do hunting.
