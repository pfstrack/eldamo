Q. Essecarmë pn. “Name-making”

Q. Essecarmë, pn. “Name-making”

A ceremony in which the father of a newborn elf announced the child’s given name, also known as his or her “father-name” (MR/214). It is a compound of essë¹ “name” and carmë “making”.
