Q. Hyarnustar loc. “Southwestlands”

Q. Hyarnustar, loc. “Southwestlands”

The southwestern region of Númenor (UT/165), a combination of the prefix hyar- “south”, the prefix nu- “west” and the plural of the suffix -sta¹ “land”.
