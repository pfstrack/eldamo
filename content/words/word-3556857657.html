✶Ad. [u]
The primitive vowel [u] remained unchanged in Classical Adûnaic except as part of the diphthongs [au] and [ōu], both of which became [ō] (SD/422-3).

    Phonetic Development

    Ad. adjacent like vowels contract and lengthen uu > ū ✧ SD/424 ([uu] > [ū])
    Ad. glide consonant [w] or [j] was inserted between [u] or [i] and a following distinct vowel uøV > uwV ✧ SD/424 (ū̆-ā̆,ū̆-ī̆ > ū̆wā̆,ū̆wī̆)
