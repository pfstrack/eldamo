      ᴱQ. milkin n. “wealth” (Category: Wealth, Riches)

      A noun appearing as ᴱQ. milkin “wealth” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√MḶKḶ “possess” (QL/62).
