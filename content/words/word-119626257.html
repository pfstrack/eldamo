ᴺQ. !andanyarro n. “weasel, ferret, mink, stoat, polecat, (lit.) long-rat” (Category: Animals (other))

ᴺQ. !andanyarro, n. “weasel, ferret, mink, stoat, polecat, (lit.) long-rat” [created by Dírheron] (Category: Animals (other))

A neologism for “weasel, ferret, mink, stoat, polecat” coined by Dírheron on Discord in 2019, a combination of anda “long” and [ᴹQ.] nyarro “rat”.
