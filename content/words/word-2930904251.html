Q. olass(i)ë n. “foliage, collection of leaves” (Category: Leaf)

Q. olass(i)ë, n. “foliage, collection of leaves” (Category: Leaf)
ᴹQ. farnë “foliage”

A noun for “foliage” appearing as either olassië or olassë, a combination of o- “together” and lassë “leaf” (Let/282; PE17/56, 84).
