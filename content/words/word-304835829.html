ᴹQ. umainen adv. “*by some means” (Category: Some, Several)

⚠️ᴹQ. umainen, adv. “*by some means” (Category: Some, Several)
ᴺQ. ^umanen “by some means”

The correlative ᴹQ. umainen appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/111), a combination of ᴹQ. uma- “some” and the instrumental suffix ᴹQ. -nen.

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. umanen “by some means” to be more consistent with the later use of singular instrumentals in correlatives: Q. sinen “by this means”.

Reference ✧ PE23/111 ✧ “*by some means”

Elements

uma- “some, a, a certain”
-nen “instrumental”
uma “some (when the identity is unknown); something or other” instrumental plural ✧ PE23/111 (umainen)
