G. gomol n. “the teeth, two rows of teeth” (Category: Tooth)

⚠️G. gomol, n. “the teeth, two rows of teeth” (Category: Tooth)
S. carach “jaws, rows of teeth”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “the teeth, the two rows of teeth”, a combination of G. go- “together” and G. môl “(inside of the) mouth” (GL/41). It appeared with a superscript ³ of unclear function.
