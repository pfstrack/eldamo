N. Cilgalad loc. “Pass of Light”

Noldorin equivalent of ᴹQ. Kalakilya appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s glossed “Pass of Light”, a combination of cîl “pass” and the lenited form of calad “light” (Ety/KIL).
