ᴺQ. !colma n. “stretcher” (Category: Medicine, Drug)

ᴺQ. !colma, n. “stretcher” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Medicine, Drug)

A neologism for “stretcher” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination √KOL “carry” and the suffix ✶-mā, hence = “*carry-thing”.

Elements

√KOL “bear, carry, wear”
✶-mā “instrumental suffix”
