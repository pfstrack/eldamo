ᴹQ. karno n. “wright” (Category: Artisan, Craftsman)

⚠️ᴹQ. carno, n. “wright” (Category: Artisan, Craftsman)
Q. ahto “wright, maker”
A word appearing in the first version of Quenya Personal Pronouns (QPP1) from the late 1940s (PE23/87), an agental form of ᴹQ. kar- “do, make” (PE23/87). It also had a feminine variant ᴹQ. karnóze.
