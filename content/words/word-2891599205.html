ᴺQ. !cantalya adj. and n. “four-legged, quadruped, (lit.) four-footed” (Category: Leg)

ᴺQ. !cantalya, adj. and n. “four-legged, quadruped, (lit.) four-footed” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Leg)

A neologism for “four-legged, quadruped” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), modeled after Q. attalya “biped” but using Q. canta “four” instead of Q. atta “two”.

Related

  • !cantalya “four-legged, quadruped, (lit.) four-footed” cf. Q. #attalya “biped, *(lit.) two-footed”

Elements

Q. canta¹ “four”
Q. tál “foot”
Q. -ya¹ “adjective suffix”
