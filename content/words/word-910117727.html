ᴺS. !redhia- v. “*to return (something)” (Category: to Return, Give Back)

ᴺS. !redhia-, v. “*to return (something)” [created by Shihali] (Category: to Return, Give Back)

A neologism resulting from a Discord conversation between Elaran, Shihali and Paul Strack on 2018-07-08, a transitive verb meaning “return (something)” derived from ᴹ√RAD “back, return”, as opposed to intransative [ᴺS.] radh- “return (to somewhere)”.

Cognates

  • ᴺQ. !ralya- “to return (something)”
