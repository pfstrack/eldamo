ᴺQ. !elentirmen, n. “astronomical observatory, (lit.) star-watch-place” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Star)

A neologism for an “astronomical observatory” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-12-24 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of elen “day” and tirmen “observatory”.

Elements

Q. elen “star”
!tirmen “theater, observatory”
