Ilk. mir(nang)eluið pn.

A rejected and hard-to-analyze Ilkorin name for the Silmarils appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s, revised to N. Mîr in Geleidh (EtyAC/MIR). It seems to contain mîr “jewel”.
