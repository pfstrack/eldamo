N. Gothrog pn. “Dread Demon”

N. Gothrog, pn. “Dread Demon”

A name glossed “Dread Demon” appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s, a combination of gost “dread” and the suffixal form -rog of rhaug “demon” (Ety/GOS, EtyAC/MBAW).
