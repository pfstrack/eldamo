ᴺQ. !mirumírë n. “amethyst, (lit.) wine-jewel” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !mirumírë, n. “amethyst, (lit.) wine-jewel” [created by Valerie] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “amethyst” coined by Valerie in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of [ᴱQ.] miru “wine” and mírë “jewel”.

Elements

miru “wine”
Q. mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
