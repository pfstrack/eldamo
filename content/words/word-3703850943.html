ᴺQ. !nityahan (nityahán-), n. “little brother (informal/familiar)” [created by Röandil] (Category: Brother)

A neologism coined by Röandil posted on 2023-11-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of nitya “little” and háno “brother”, with longer variant nityaháno.

Q. #nitya “little”
