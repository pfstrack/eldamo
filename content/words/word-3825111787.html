ᴱQ. Ulbandi f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Ulbandi, f.

Mother of Melko’s son Kosomoko (precursor to Gothmog) in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/93), it seems to be a feminine form of ulban “monster, giant”.
