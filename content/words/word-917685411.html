S. Othrondir, pn. “?Stronghold Man”

Name of a man of Gondor (WJ/417), first written Othrondor (WJ/421 note #16), appearing in Tolkien’s aborted sequel to the The Lord of the Rings: The New Shadow. His name seems to be a combination of othrond “underground stronghold” and dîr¹ “man”.
