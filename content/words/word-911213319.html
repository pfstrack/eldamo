ᴺQ. !elentaula adj. “impressive” (Category: Wonder, Astonishment)

ᴺQ. !elentaula, adj. “impressive” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Wonder, Astonishment)

A neologism for “impressive” coined by Luinyelle and posted on 2024-08-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of ᴺQ. elenta- “impress” with the suffix -ula “apt to do”, thus meaning “apt to impress”.

Elements

!elenta- “to impress, (lit.) make marvel”
Q. *-ula “likelihood or aptitude”
