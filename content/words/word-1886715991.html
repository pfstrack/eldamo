ᴱQ. kauko (kauku-) n. “humpback” (Category: Lame)

⚠️ᴱQ. cauco (caucu-), n. “humpback” (Category: Lame)

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “a humpback” from the early root ᴱ√KAẆA (QL/45), cognate to G. caug of the same meaning (QL/45).
