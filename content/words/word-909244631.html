ᴺQ. !emmata- v. “to draw/create (a picture); to photograph” (Category: Painting, Picture)

ᴺQ. !emmata-, v. “to draw/create (a picture); to photograph” [created by Luinyelle, NotD] (Category: Painting, Picture)

A neologism for “to photograph” coined by Luinyelle in the “Neologism of the Day” (NotD) series on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server posted at 2023-05-30, a verbal elaboration of emma “picture”. In the context of Elvish culture, I would use this verb in the sense “to draw/create (a picture)”.
