ᴺQ. !ëavëa adj. “universal, natural” (Category: Universe)

⚠️ᴺQ. !ëavëa, adj. “universal, natural” [created by NQ-Wiki] (Category: Universe)
ᴺQ. !ilúvëa “universal”
ᴺQ. !nassëa² “natural”

A neologism for “universal, natural“ from the Neo-Quenya Wiki, an adjectival form of Eä “Creation”. I prefer ᴺQ. ilúvëa for “universal” and ᴺQ. nassëa for “natural”.

Elements

Q. Eä “Creation, the (Material) Universe, Cosmos, World; it is, let it be”
