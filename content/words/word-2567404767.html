S. Ossir loc. “Seven-rivers”

S. Ossir, loc. “Seven-rivers”

A shorter form of the name Ossiriand glossed “Seven-rivers” (LotR/469), a reduction of primitive otoso “seven” combined with sîr “river” (RC/384).
