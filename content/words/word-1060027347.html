ᴺQ. !nortanémë n. “driving licence, driver’s license, (lit.) drive-permission” (Category: to Drive)

ᴺQ. !nortanémë, n. “driving licence, driver’s license, (lit.) drive-permission” [created by Chaered] (Category: to Drive)

A neologism for a “driving or driver’s license” coined by Chaered posted on 2025-08-22 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of norta- “make run” = “drive” and néme “permission”. Some people are uncomfortable with néme given later nem- “seem”, and prefer lávë for “permission” instead based on lav- “allow”.

Elements

Q. norta- “to ride, [lit.] make run (specially used of riding horses or other animals)”
ᴹQ. néme “leave, *permission”
