Q. Tauremorna loc. “Black Forest”

Q. Tauremorna, loc. “Black Forest”

A name of Fangorn forest, shorter form of Tauremornalómë (LotR/469), translated “Black Forest” (PE17/82). This name is a compound of taurë “forest” and morna “black” (RC/385, PE17/82).
