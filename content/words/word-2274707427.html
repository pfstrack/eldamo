⚠️ᴺQ. !rosta-, v. “to hollow out, excavate” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: to Dig, Delve)
Q. felehta- “*to excavate, tunnel, mine”

A neologism created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s inspired by N. rosta- of the same meaning (Ety/ROD). I think the attested word Q. felehta- from 1957 (PE17/118) might also mean “to excavate”, so I prefer it for purposes of Neo-Quenya.
