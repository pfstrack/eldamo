ᴺQ. !tallimë (tallimi-) n. “ankle, (lit.) foot-link” (Category: Foot)

ᴺQ. !tallimë (tallimi-), n. “ankle, (lit.) foot-link” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Foot)

A neologism for “ankle” coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo inspired by Q. málimë “wrist” = “hand-link”, a combination of Q. tál “foot” and Q. limë “link”.
