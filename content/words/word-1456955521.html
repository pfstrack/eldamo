ᴺQ. !andosan (andosamb-) [þ] n. “vestibule, (lit.) gate room” (Category: Door, Gate)

ᴺQ. !andosan (andosamb-) [þ], n. “vestibule, (lit.) gate room” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Door, Gate)

A neologism coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of ando “gate” and [ᴹQ.] sambe “room”.

Elements

Q. ando “gate, [great] door; †entrances, approaches”
ᴹQ. sambe “room, chamber”
