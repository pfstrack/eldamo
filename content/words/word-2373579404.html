ᴺQ. !lap- v. “to fold, ⚠️bend” (Category: Conjunction)

ᴺQ. !lap-, v. “to fold, ⚠️bend” [created by Arael, Luinyelle] (Category: Conjunction)

A neologism for “fold” coined by Arael and Luinyelle, posted on 2024-10-04 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) and derived from √LAP “fold, bend” (VT47/35). Arael and Luinyelle also suggested this verb might mean “bend” like its root, but for that purpose I prefer attested cúna- “bend”.
