ᴺQ. !impanwenen adv. “respectively, (lit.) by same arrangement” (Category: Painting, Picture)

ᴺQ. !impanwenen, adv. “respectively, (lit.) by same arrangement” [created by Luinyelle, Vyacheslav Stepanov] (Category: Painting, Picture)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle and Vyacheslav Stepanov, posted on 2024-09-16 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of im- “same”, [ᴺQ.] panwë “arrangement”, and the instrumental suffix -nen “same”.

Elements

Q. im- “same, self”
!panwë “arrangement, plan; structure, ordering; (musical) theme”
Q. -nen “instrumental”
