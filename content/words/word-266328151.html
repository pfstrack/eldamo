S. Arthor na Challonnas, loc. “Realm of the South-harbourage”

An old (Númenórean?) name for the region of Gondor (PE17/28). It is a combination of arthor “realm”, the preposition na² “of” and the lenited form Challonnas of its shorter name Hallonnas “South-harbourage”.
