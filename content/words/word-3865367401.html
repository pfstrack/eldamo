ᴺQ. !lancöa n. “tent, (lit.) cloth shelter” (Category: Tent)

ᴺQ. !lancöa, n. “tent, (lit.) cloth shelter” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Tent)

A neologism for “tent” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴹQ.] lanne “cloth” and Q. cöa “house”.

Elements

ᴹQ. lanne “tissue, cloth”
Q. cöa “house; outhouse, shed, hut, booth; building used for a dwelling or other purposes; †body”
