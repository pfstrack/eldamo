S. Hurinionath coll. “Descendants of Húrin”

S. Hurinionath, coll. “Descendants of Húrin”

A title for the stewards of Gondor as the descendants Húrin (PM/202), a combination of that name with the patronymic suffix -ion¹ and the class-plural suffix -ath.
