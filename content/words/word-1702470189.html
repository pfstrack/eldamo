ᴱN. urwedhw adj. “without mate” (Category: Husband)

⚠️ᴱN. urwedhw, adj. “without mate” (Category: Husband)
ᴺS. !best “marriage, matrimony, wedlock, ⚠️wedding”

A term for “without mate” in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s, a combination of ᴱN. gwedhw “matrimony” with the privative prefix ᴱN. ur- (PE13/155-6). It had a deleted variant N. uwedheb or uwedhw “not married” that instead used the (rejected) privative prefix ᴱN. u(m)- (PE13/155).
