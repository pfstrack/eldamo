ᴺQ. !atapengëa, adj. “bilabial (of consonants)” [created by Orondil] (Category: Phonetic)

A neologism coined by Orondil in 2023-10-08 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of at(a)- “bi-” and [] pengëa “labial”.

Elements

Q. at(a)- “double, second time”
!pengëa “labial, of the lips”
