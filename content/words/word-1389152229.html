S. Dúnedhel pn. “West-Elf”

S. Dúnedhel, pn. “West-Elf”

A term for the Elves of the West who fought against Morgoth, coined to match Dúnadan (WJ/378, PE17/141). This name is a combination of dûn “west” and Edhel “Elf”. It also appeared in the variant form Dúnel using Ell “Elf” (PE17/141).
