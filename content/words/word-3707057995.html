S. Nan Gondresgion loc. “Stonewain Valley”

S. Nan Gondresgion, loc. “Stonewain Valley”

Another Sindarin name for the Stonewain Valley (PE17/28), apparently a combination of nan(d) “valley”, gond “stone”, the plural of rasg “wagon” and the suffix -ion² “-land”.

Reference ✧ PE17/28 ✧ “Stonewain Valley”

Related

  • Nan Gondresgion “Stonewain Valley” is a variant of Imrath Gondraich “Stonewain Valley”

Elements

nan(d) “vale, valley”

rasg “wain, *wagon” plural ✧ PE17/28 (resg)
-ion² “-region, -land”
