Q. mírenel, n. “jewel-triad” (Category: Gem, Jewel)
A name for the Silmarils in notes from 1969, a combination of mírë “jewel” and a reduction of neldë “three”.

Reference ✧ PE23/142 ✧ “jewel-triad”

Elements

mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
neldë “three”
