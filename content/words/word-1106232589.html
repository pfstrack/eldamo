ᴺQ. !imanna adv. “to the identical place” (Category: Equal, Alike)

ᴺQ. !imanna, adv. “to the identical place” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Equal, Alike)
See ᴹQ. o(na)nna for discussion.

Elements

Q. im- “same, self”
Q. -nna “(movement) to, towards, onto, at (arriving at a point); allative suffix”

      ᴹQ. o(na)nna adv. “*to the identical place” (Category: To, Towards)

      The correlatives ᴹQ. onna or onanna appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112), a combination of ᴹQ. on- “same” and the allative suffix ᴹQ. -nna.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. imanna “to the identical place” using im- “same”.
