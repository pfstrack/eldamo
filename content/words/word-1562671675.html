S. Amon Carab, loc. “Hill of the Hat”

An earlier name of Amon Rûdh that appeared in various forms, one of which (Amon Carab) was translated “Hill of the Hat” (WJ/187). It seems to be a combination of amon “hill” and carab “hat”, not second of which is not otherwise attested.
