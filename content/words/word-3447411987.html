ᴺQ. !†uvassë, n. “future tense, (lit.) uva-ness” [created by Röandil] (Category: Verb Inflection)

An archaic term for the “future tense”, a neologism coined by Röandil on 2023-08-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), an abstract noun based on the ancient future element ✶ubā. In modern Quenya it would be tulussë.
