ᴹQ. maire n. “*rose” (Category: Rose)

⚠️ᴹQ. mairë, n. “*rose” (Category: Rose)
ᴺQ. !merillë “rose”
The word {melle >>} maire appears to be a word for “rose” in the untranslated phrase eane sasse maire ma from notes written in the late 1940s (PE23/92), probably meaning “*there was a rose”. Later in the paragraph Tolkien said “t would to Eldar refer to the (red) rose”, with “rose” apparently referring back to maire. The etymology of this word is unclear.
