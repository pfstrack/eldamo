ᴱQ. Sainen pn. “Saturday”

⚠️ᴱQ. Sainen, pn. “Saturday”
Q. Elenya “Saturday, *Star-day”

Name of Saturday in the seven-day week of the Elves (otsola) in an early word list (PE14/21). The day was related to fire and metals, and was probably derived from sá “fire”.
