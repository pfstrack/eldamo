Q. Arda Alahasta pn. “Arda Unmarred”

Q. Arda Alahasta, pn. “Arda Unmarred”
A term for Arda in its original state, before evil was created by the actions of Morgoth (MR/254). Tolkien also used the term Arda Vanya, of the same meaning (PE17/150).
