Q. rusco (ruscu-) n. “fox” (Category: Fox)

Q. rusco (ruscu-), n. “fox” (Category: Fox)

The Quenya word for “fox” from the root √(u)rus “brownish red” (VT41/10). It has a stem form of ruscu- and hence a plural form is rusqui.
