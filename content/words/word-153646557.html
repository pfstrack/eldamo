ᴹQ. rakkalepta adj. “*claw-fingered” (Category: Nail, Claw)

ᴹQ. raccalepta, adj. “*claw-fingered” (Category: Nail, Claw)

A word appearing in Lord of the Rings drafts as part of Treebeard’s description of orcs (SD/68), likely the equivalent of English “claw-fingered” from the finished text (LotR/979), a combination of (otherwise unattested) rakka “claw” and lepta “finger”.
