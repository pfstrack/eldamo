ᴱQ. san² (samb-) n. “hall, dwelling, house” (Category: House)

⚠️ᴱQ. san² (samb-), n. “hall, dwelling, house” (Category: House)
Q. mar(da) “dwelling, (great) house, residence, mansion, a thing or place dwelt in, ⚠️home; [ᴱQ.] world”

The word ᴱQ. san² (sam(b)-) “hall, dwelling, house” appeared in Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√SAMA (QL/81).
