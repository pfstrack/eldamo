Q. talo adv. “thence” (Category: That)

Q. talo, adv. “thence” (Category: That)

The words talo and tō “thence” appeared in a list of demonstratives from 1968 (VT49/11), combinations of ta “that” and the ablative suffix -llo. Similarly formed ᴹQ. tallo appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112).
