ᴺQ. !epemat (epematt-) n. “dessert, (lit.) after-meal” (Category: Food (Other))

ᴺQ. !epemat (epematt-), n. “dessert, (lit.) after-meal” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Food (Other))

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-01-14 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of epë “after” and [ᴹQ.] mat (matt-) “meal”.

Elements

Q. epë “after (of time), following; before (in all relations but time)”
ᴹQ. mat “food, meal”
