ᴺQ. !morteccon (morteccond-) n. “graphite, (lit.) black writing rock” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !morteccon (morteccond-), n. “graphite, (lit.) black writing rock” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism for “graphite” coined by Luinyelle in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of morë “black”, [ᴹQ.] tek- “write” and ondo “stone”.

Elements

Q. morë “dark, black; darkness, night”
ᴹQ. tek- “to write”
Q. ondo “stone (as a material), (large mass of) rock”
