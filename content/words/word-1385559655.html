ᴺS. !anneth n. “(act of) giving” (Category: to Give)

⚠️ᴺS. !anneth, n. “(act of) giving” [created by David Salo, GS] (Category: to Give)
S. #anna- “to give, †add to”

A neologism coined by David Salo in his book Gateway to Sindarin (GS/239), a combination of anna- “give” with the suffix -th. I would simply use aned “*giving” the gerund of the verb “to give”, published after Salo’s book.

Elements

S. #anna- “to give, †add to”
S. -th “abstract noun”
