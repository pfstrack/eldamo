ᴺS. !pimp n. “tail” (Category: Tail)

ᴺS. !pimp, n. “tail” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Tail)

A neologism for “tail” coined by Paul Strack in 2021 specifically for Eldamo, the Sindarin equivalent of ᴺQ. pimpë. Jenna Carpenter suggested ᴺS. pim “tail” in VinQuettaParma Wiki (VQP), but I see no reason why the p would vanish; compare to S. nimp “pale”.
