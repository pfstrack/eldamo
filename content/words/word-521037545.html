Q. Návarot loc. “Hollowbold”

Q. Návarot, loc. “Hollowbold”

The Quenya translation of Kh. Tumunzahar “Hollowbold” (WJ/389). It is a compound of náva¹ “hollow” and rotto “cave, tunnel”.
