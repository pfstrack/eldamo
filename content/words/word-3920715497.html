ᴺQ. !nailyë interj. “go ahead, you are welcome to proceed” (Category: to Go)

ᴺQ. !nailyë, interj. “go ahead, you are welcome to proceed” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Go)

A neologism for “go ahead, you are welcome to proceed” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of nai “may it be that” and lye “you (polite)”. Familiar and plural variants naityë and naildë also exist.

Elements

Q. nai “maybe; be it that, may it be that; perhaps, it may be, there is a chance or possibility”
Q. lye “you (polite)”
