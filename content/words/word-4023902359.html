ᴺS. !gwendren adj. “maidenly, girlish” (Category: Girl)

ᴺS. !gwendren, adj. “maidenly, girlish” (Category: Girl)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo as an adjectival form of S. gwend “maiden”, serving as a replacement for G. gwenniniol of the same meaning (GL/45).

Elements

S. gwend “maiden”

      G. gwenniniol adj. “maiden(ly), girlish” (Category: Girl)

      A word in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s appearing as {gwend(r)iol >>} gwenniniol “maiden (aj.), maidenly, girlish”, an adjectival form of {gwen >>} G. gwennin “girl” (GL/45).
