ᴺQ. !hap-² v. “to clothe, enfold” (Category: to Clothe, Dress)

⚠️ᴺQ. !hap-², v. “to clothe, enfold” [created by Petri Tikka, PPQ] (Category: to Clothe, Dress)
ᴹQ. vaita- “to enfold, [ᴱQ.] wrap”
ᴺQ. vaimata- “to robe, *clothe; to get dressed, put on clothing”

A neologism for “to clothe, enfold” created by Petri Tikka in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, equivalent to N. hab- “to clothe” (Ety/KHAB). I prefer to use [ᴱQ.] vaimata- for this purpose.
