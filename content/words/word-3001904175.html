S. Dor-Cúarthol loc. “Land of Bow and Helm”

S. Dor-Cúarthol, loc. “Land of Bow and Helm”

The region where Túrin and Beleg campaigned against Morgoth, translated “Land of Bow and Helm” (S/205), a combination dôr “land”, cû “bow”, a variant ar of a¹ “and” with thôl “helm” (SA/cú, thôl).
