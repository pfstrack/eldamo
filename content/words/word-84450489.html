          ᴱQ. Falmasan pn. “Friday”

          Name of the first Friday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the god Falman (Osse) and sana “day”.

          Reference ✧ PE14/22 ✧ “Friday”

          Elements

          Falman “Osse”
          sana “day (24 hours)”

              ᴱQ. Onensan pn. “Friday”

              Name of the second Friday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the goddess Ónen and sana “day”.

              Reference ✧ PE14/22 ✧ “Friday”

                  ᴱQ. Palúrien² pn. “Friday”

                  Name of Friday in the seven-day week of the Elves (otsola) in an early word list (PE14/21). The day was related to the Earth and was possibly derived from Palúrien¹ “Wide Earth”, another name for Yavanna.

                  Reference ✧ PE14/21 ✧ Palūrien “Friday”
