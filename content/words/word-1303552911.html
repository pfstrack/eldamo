Q. Silmarien, f.

Eldest child of Tar-Elendil and ancestor of Valandil of Andúnië (LotR/1035, S/268). Her name seems to be a compound of silma¹ “silver, shining white” and the feminine suffix -ien¹.

References ✧ LotRI; PMI; SI; UT/210.0501; UTI

Elements

silma¹ “crystal (white)”
-ien¹ “feminine ending; -daughter”
