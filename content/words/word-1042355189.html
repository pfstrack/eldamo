ᴹQ. Ingweqenya pn.

ᴹQ. Ingwequenya, pn.

A term for the dialect of Qenya spoken in the house of Ingwe appearing in linguistic notes from the 1930s (LR/172, 193), it is simply a combination of the name of the Elf-lord prepended to the language.
