G. muir n. “heifer” (Category: Cow)

⚠️G. muir, n. “heifer” (Category: Cow)

A word for a “heifer” in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s with variants muir and muthli, feminine forms of G. mû “ox” (GL/58).
