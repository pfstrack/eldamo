ᴺQ. !nucuv- v. “to humble oneself, submit, pay homage, genuflect” (Category: Proud)

ᴺQ. !nucuv-, v. “to humble oneself, submit, pay homage, genuflect” [created by Parmandil] (Category: Proud)

A neologism coined by Parmandil posted on 2024-05-12 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of nu “under” and [ᴹQ.] kuv- “to bow”, inspired by [ᴹQ.] nukumna “humbled”. The past tense of past tense of this verb is nucumbe.

Elements

Q. nu “under, beneath”
ᴹQ. kuv- “to bow”

Element In

  • ᴺQ. !nucupta- “to humble (someone), force to bow, vanquish”
