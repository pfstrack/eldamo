Q. Elerossë m. “Star Foam”

Q. Elerossë, m. “Star Foam”

The Quenya name of S. Elros (PM/349), a compound of elen “star” and rossë “dew”. The final -n of elen was assimilated to the r, which also happened in the names Elerondo and Elerrína.
