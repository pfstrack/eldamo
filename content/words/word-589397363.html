ᴱQ. †Ondostamar (Ondostamard-) loc.

⚠️ᴱQ. †Ondostamar (Ondostamard-), loc.

Archaic Qenya cognate of G. Gondothlimbar “City of the Dwellers in Stone” in an early name list (PE13/102), a combination of ondo “stone”, osta “homestead” and mar¹ “dwelling”.

Reference ✧ PE13/102 ✧ †Ondostamard-

Elements

ondo “stone, rock”
osta “homestead”
mar¹ “house, home, dwelling (of men); -land, the Earth”

Cognates

  • G. Gondothlimbar “City of the Dwellers in Stone, Dwelling of the Folk of Stone” ✧ PE13/102
