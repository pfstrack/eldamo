Q. Elatan m. “*Star Man”

Q. Elatan, m. “*Star Man”
The husband of Silmarien and father of Valandil, ancestor of the kings of Gondor and Arnor (UT/173). His name seems to be a compound of él “star” and Atan “Man”.

References ✧ UT/210; UTI
