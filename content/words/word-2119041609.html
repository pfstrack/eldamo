ᴺQ. !cáralannë n. “bandana, kerchief, (lit.) head-cloth” (Category: Hat, Cap)

ᴺQ. !cáralannë, n. “bandana, kerchief, (lit.) head-cloth” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Hat, Cap)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-08-22 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of cas (car-) “head” and [ᴹQ.] lanne “cloth”.

Elements

Q. cas “head”
ᴹQ. lanne “tissue, cloth”
