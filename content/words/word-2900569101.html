S. Bar-in-Gwael loc.

An earlier name for Bar-in-Mŷl “Home of the Gulls” (WJ/418). The initial element of the name is bar “home” and the second element is the plural in of the definite article i¹ “the”, but the meaning of the final element is unclear. It may be a variation of gwae(w) “wind”.
