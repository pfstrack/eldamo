ᴱQ. hakka n. “the hams, buttocks” (Category: Back)

A word appearing as ᴱQ. hakka in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s as a cognate to G. hacha “the hams, buttocks” (GL/47).

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya, I would adapt this word as ᴺQ. hacca “the hams, buttocks” from a Neo-Root ᴺ√KHAK “squat”.
