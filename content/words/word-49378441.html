ᴺQ. !nahamnar n. “church (as a group of people), (lit.) the summoned” (Category: Religion)

ᴺQ. !nahamnar, n. “church (as a group of people), (lit.) the summoned” [created by Parmandil] (Category: Religion)

A neologism for a “church” as a group of people rather than a building, coined by Parmandil posted on 2025-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a plural noun form of nahamna “summoned”.
