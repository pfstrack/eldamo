N. Erebras loc. “Lonely Mountain”

N. Erebras, loc. “Lonely Mountain”

Earlier name for Erebor appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s (EtyAC/ERE), a combination of ereb “isolated” and the lenited form of rhas “horn”.
