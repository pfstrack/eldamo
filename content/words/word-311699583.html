Q. Ambidexters Sentence

Elements

eldar ataformaiti “the Elves were ambidexters”
epetai i hyarma ú ten ulca símaryassen “consequently the left hand was not to them evil in their imaginations”
úsië, an cé mo quernë cendelë númenna, ve senya “on the contrary, for if one turned the face westward, as was usual”
i hyarma tentanë Melcorello “the left hand pointed away from Melkor”
ar cé mo formenna tentanes Amanna “and if northwards, it pointed toward towards Aman”
