ᴺQ. !cemendur n. “farmer” (Category: Farmer)

ᴺQ. !cemendur, n. “farmer” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Farmer)

A neologism for “farmer” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of cemen “earth” and -(n)dur “servant”, a suffix sometimes used in the names of professions such as arandur “minister” and ornendur “forester”.

Elements

Q. cemen “the earth; earth”
Q. -(n)dur “servant; to serve”
