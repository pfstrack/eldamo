ᴱQ. Nosse Tuilinda pn. “*People of Tuilindo”

⚠️ᴱQ. Nosse Tuilinda, pn. “*People of Tuilindo”

Qenya cognate of Nos Duilin (PE15/22), a combination of nosse “people” and the genitive of tuilindo “swallow”.
