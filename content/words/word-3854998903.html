ᴺQ. !hrúfinta- v. “to cheat” (Category: Deceit)

ᴺQ. !hrúfinta-, v. “to cheat” [created by Tamas Ferencz, NotD] (Category: Deceit)

A neologism for “to cheat” coined by Tamas Ferencz in the “Neologism of the Day” (NotD) series on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server posted at 2023-05-22, a combination of hrú- “evil-” and finta- “to show skill”; compare fintalë “trick”.

Elements

Q. hru- “evil-”
Q. finta- “to make, finish off, decorate a thing with delicate work; to show skill”
