S. adaneth n. “(mortal) woman” (Category: Woman)

S. adaneth, n. “(mortal) woman” (Category: Woman)

A term for a mortal woman appearing in Athrabeth Finrod ah Andreth written around 1959 (MR/349), a feminized form of Adan “Man (as as species)”.
