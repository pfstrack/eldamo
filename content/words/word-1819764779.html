ᴺQ. !estatina adj. “divided, shared, distributed” (Category: to Divide)

ᴺQ. !estatina, adj. “divided, shared, distributed” (Category: to Divide)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo to replace ᴱQ. tirtina, it is simply the passive participle of Q. estat- “distribute in even portions”.

Elements

Q. estat- “to distribute in even portions”

      ᴱQ. tirtina adj. “divided, shared, distributed” (Category: to Divide)

      An adjective in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “divided, shared, distributed” under the early root ᴱ√TṚT͡YṚ (QL/94).
