ᴱQ. Hanstovánen loc.

⚠️ᴱQ. Hanstovánen, loc.

A name of the beaching place of the ship Mornie, first appearing as (rejected) Mornielta and Vane Hansto (LT1/170). The meaning of this name is unclear.
