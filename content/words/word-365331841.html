S. Gódhel pn. “(Exiled) Noldo”

S. Gódhel, pn. “(Exiled) Noldo”

A Sindarin term for the Exiled Noldor, properly †Ódhel but with a G added by analogy with their clan name Golodh (WJ/364, WJ/379).
