ᴹQ. veaner¹ n. “(adult) man” (Category: Man)

ᴹQ. vëaner¹, n. “(adult) man” (Category: Man)

A word for an “(adult) man” in The Etymologies of the 1930s, a combination of ᴹQ. vea “adult” and ᴹQ. nér “man” (Ety/WEG).
