G. Gwenninûr f.

⚠️G. Gwenninûr, f.

Another name for the Sun Maiden ᴱQ. Urwen(di) (Arien) in the Gnomish Lexicon from the 1910s (GL/18), possibly a combination of gwennin “girl” with the root URU having to do with the Sun.
