ᴺQ. !linvainëa n. “onion, (lit.) many-sheathed” (Category: Onion)

ᴺQ. !linvainëa, n. “onion, (lit.) many-sheathed” [created by Luinyelle, NotD] (Category: Onion)

A neologism for “onion” coined by Luinyelle in the “Neologism of the Day” (NotD) series on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server posted at 2023-05-22, a combination of li(n)- “many” and an adjectival form of [ᴱQ.] vainë “sheath”, hence literally “many-sheathed”.

Elements

Q. #li(n)- “many”
vainë “sheath, pod”
