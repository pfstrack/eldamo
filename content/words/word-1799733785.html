ᴱQ. Sári pn. “Sun”

⚠️ᴱQ. Sári, pn. “Sun”

A name for the Sun in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/186), probably a derivative of the root ᴱ√SAH(Y)A “be hot” as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Sári).
