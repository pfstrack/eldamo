N. Lhúnorodrim loc. “Blue Mountains”

N. Lhúnorodrim, loc. “Blue Mountains”

A variant name for Eredluin in The Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/LUG²), a combination of lhûn “blue” and the class-plural of orod “mountain”.
