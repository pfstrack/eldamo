ᴹQ. Sanavaldo m. “Almighty”

ᴹQ. Sanavaldo, m. “Almighty”

A name of Ilúvatar in tales of the fall of Númenor from the 1940s, glossed “Almighty” (SD/401). Etymologically, its middle element is probably derived from the root ᴹ√BAL.
