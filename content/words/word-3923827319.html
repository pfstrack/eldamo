ᴹQ. Silmerosse pn. “Glimmering Rain”

A name of Silpion from Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (LR/210), also appearing in the Etymologies as compound of silme “silver (light)” and rosse “rain” (Ety/ROS¹, SIL).
