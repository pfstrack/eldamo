S. Athrad Angren loc. “Ford of Isen”

S. Athrad Angren, loc. “Ford of Isen”

A Sindarin name for the Ford of Isen, a combination of athrad “ford” and the adjective angren “of iron” (UT/318). It also appeared in a plural form Ethraid Engrin.
