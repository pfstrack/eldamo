S. Forfalas loc. “North Falas”

S. Forfalas, loc. “North Falas”

A name for the northern part of Falas on the later Silmarillion map revisions from the 1950s-60s (WJ/186), a combination of the prefixal form for- of forn “north” and falas “shore”.
