Ilk. lóm n. “echo”

A Doriathrin noun for “echo” derived from the root ᴹ√LAM (Ety/LAM). Its Quenya cognate láma suggests a primitive form *✶lāmā, from which the long [ā] became [ō], as suggested by Helge Fauskanger (AL-Doriathrin/lóm).
