ᴱQ. Qalmisan pn. “Tuesday”

⚠️ᴱQ. Qalmisan, pn. “Tuesday”
Q. Aldúya “*Tuesday, Day of the Two Trees”

Name of the second Tuesday in the Valinorian fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of qalme “death” and sana “day”.

Reference ✧ PE14/22 ✧ “Tuesday”

Elements

qalme “death, agony”
sana “day (24 hours)”
