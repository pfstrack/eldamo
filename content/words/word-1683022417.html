ᴱN. yrlhith adj. “dustless” (Category: Dust, Powder)

⚠️ᴱN. yrlhith, adj. “dustless” (Category: Dust, Powder)

An example negative in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s that was a combination of the privative prefix ᴱN. ur- and ᴱN. lhith “dust”, with the prefixal form yr- the result of i-affection (PE13/156).
