ᴹ√SWIN root. “whirl, eddy”

A root in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “whirl, eddy” with derivatives like ᴹQ. hwinde/N. chwinn “eddy, whirlpool” and ᴹQ. hwinya-/N. chwinia- “eddy, twirl, swirl” (Ety/SWIN).
