Dan. initial [x] became [h], unvoicing following [l], [r], [w]; [x-|x{lrw}-] > [h-|ø{l̥r̥w̥}-]

According to the Comparative Tables, the initial voiceless velar spirant [x] that developed from [kʰ] became [h] (PE19/18, 21). There was a similar change in Noldorin and Ilkorin, as well as in the Germanic languages languages that inspirited Danian (ref/@@@). Where the initial [x] was followed by a [l], [r] or [w], it seems to have unvoiced that consonant, similar to the changes in Ilkorin.

      Phonetic Rule Elements

      [x-] > [h-]
      [xl-] > [l̥-]
      [xr-] > [r̥-]
      [xw-] > [w̥-]
