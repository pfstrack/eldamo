ᴺQ. !sacillë [þ] n. “splinter” (Category: Wood, Timber)

⚠️ᴺQ. !sacillë [þ], n. “splinter” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Wood, Timber)
ᴺQ. ^cirda “splinter, wood shaving”

A neologism for “splinter” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, derived from the root ᴹ√STAK “split, insert”. I prefer instead to adapted the early word ᴱQ. kinda “splinter” as ᴺQ. cirda.
