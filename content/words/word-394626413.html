G. masgar n. “pasture” (Category: Pasture)

⚠️G. masgar, n. “pasture” (Category: Pasture)
N. nadhras “pasture”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “pasture” (GL/56), perhaps a combination of G. mast “feed” and G. gar “place”.
