ᴹQ. manome(s), adv. “in what place” (Category: What)

A correlatives appearing in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/108), a combination of interrogative ᴹQ. ma and ᴹQ. nome “place”.
