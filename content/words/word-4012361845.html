              ᴱ√HYA root. “this by us”

              A “near demonstrative” root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “this by us” (QL/41). In the Gnomish Lexicon written shortly afterwards, was replaced by ᴱ√SI(N) “this here by me” (GL/68), which Tolkien used thereafter as the basis for “this” words.
