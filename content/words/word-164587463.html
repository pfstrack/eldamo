S. Haerast loc. “Far Shore”

S. Haerast, loc. “Far Shore”

A name of the coast of Aman translated “Far Shore”, as opposed to Nevrast “Hither Shore” (PE17/27). It is a combination of hae “far” and ras(t) “shore”.
