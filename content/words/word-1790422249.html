S. Gilmith f. “*Grey Star”

S. Gilmith, f. “*Grey Star”

Sister of S. Galador, the first lord of Dol Amroth (UT/248), perhaps a combination of S. gil “star” and S. mith “grey”.

References ✧ PMI; UTI

Elements

gil “star; (bright) spark, silver glint, twinkle of light”
mith “grey, light grey, pale grey”
