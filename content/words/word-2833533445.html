N. Elfaron pn. “Sky-hunter”

N. Elfaron, pn. “Sky-hunter”

A Noldorin name the Moon glossed “Sky-hunter” appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s, a combination of ell “sky” and faron “hunter” (Ety/SPAR, EtyAC/SPAR).
