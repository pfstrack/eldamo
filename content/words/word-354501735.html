ᴺQ. !hwarrissë n. “blizzard, (lit.) blowing fall of snow” (Category: Snow)

ᴺQ. !hwarrissë, n. “blizzard, (lit.) blowing fall of snow” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Snow)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2023-11-04 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of hwá “violent wind” and hrissë “fall of snow”.

Elements

Q. hwarwa “violent wind”
Q. hrissë “fall of snow”
