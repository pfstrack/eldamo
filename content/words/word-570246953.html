ᴱQ. Lunde Susúlima pn. “March”

⚠️ᴱQ. Lunde Susúlima, pn. “March”
Q. Súlimë “March, *Windy-one”

A name for the month of March in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/56), a combination of lunde “month” and susúlima “windy”.

Reference ✧ QL/56 ✧ (Lunde) susūlima “March”

Elements

lunde “(lunar) month; (lit.) period” ✧ QL/56
susúlima “full of wind, windy, airy” ✧ QL/56 (susūlima)
