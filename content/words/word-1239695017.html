Q. Lindissë f. “*Singer (f.)”

First child of Axantur, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. Her name seems to be a compound of lindë “singing, song” and the feminine suffix -issë.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0505

Elements

lindë “singing, song, musical sound; air, tune”
-issë “ending in feminine names”
