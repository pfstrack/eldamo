S. Hador, m.

Leader of the House of Hador, one of the three tribes of the Edain (S/147). This name is most likely Sindarin, simply hador “thrower” used as a name (Ety/KHAT).

Conceptual Development: In Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, this name appeared as N. Hádor and Hador with both long and short a (LR/146) and it appeared in the Etymologies with the derivation given above (Ety/KHAT).
