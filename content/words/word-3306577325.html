ᴺQ. !aumanca- v. “to sell, (lit.) trade away” (Category: to Sell)

ᴺQ. !aumanca-, v. “to sell, (lit.) trade away” [created by Sami Paldanius] (Category: to Sell)

A neologism for “sell” coined by Sami Paldanius in the 1000 Words project (1000W), a combination of au- “away from (the speaker)” and [ᴹQ.] manca- “trade”, inspired by homanca- “buy”.

Elements

Q. au- “away (from)”
ᴹQ. manka- “to trade”
