ᴹQ. Fanyamar, loc. “Cloudhome, Upper Air”

Region of the upper airs in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (SM/236), also appearing in The Etymologies as compound of fanya “cloud” and mar “home” (Ety/SPAN).
