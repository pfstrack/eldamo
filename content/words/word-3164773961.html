ᴱQ. Lunde Lótea pn. “May”

⚠️ᴱQ. Lunde Lótëa, pn. “May”
Q. Lótessë “May, (lit.) *Flower-ness”

A name for the month of May in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/56), a combination of lunde “month” and lótea “full of blossom”.
