N. Núron m. “Ulmo”

N. Núron, m. “Ulmo”

A Noldorin name for Ulmo appearing only in The Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/NU), apparently a masculinized form of nûr¹ “deep” and with the suffix -on¹.
