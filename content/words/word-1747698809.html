ᴺQ. !lingwimo n. “fisherman” (Category: Fisherman)

ᴺQ. !lingwimo, n. “fisherman” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Fisherman)

A neologism for “fisherman” coined by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of Q. lingwë “fish” and Q. mo “fish”. Fauskanger also used ᴺQ. raito for “fisher” (more literally “netter”), but I prefer lingwimo as it is more easily understood.

Elements

Q. #lingwë “fish”
Q. mo “one, anyone, someone, somebody”

      ᴺQ. !raito n. “fisher” (Category: Fisherman)

      See ᴺQ. !lingwimo for discussion.

      Elements

      Q. raita-¹ “to make network or lace; to catch in a net”
