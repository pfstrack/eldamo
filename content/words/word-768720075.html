S. Gaerdil, m. “*Sea-lover”

S. Gaerdil, m. “*Sea-lover”

Sindarin equivalent of Eärendil, also appearing as Gaerdilion and Gaerennil (PE17/19, 27). This name contains gaer “sea” and the suffix -dil “friend, lover”.
