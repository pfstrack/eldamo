ᴹQ. Koreldarin pn.

ᴹQ. Koreldarin, pn.

The shared language of the Lindar and Noldor when they both dwelt in Kôr in Tolkien’s linguistic writings from the 1930s (LR/195), a compound of that city name with the adjective Eldarin.
