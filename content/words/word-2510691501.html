ᴹQ. qáqínen adv. “*by all means” (Category: All, Every)

⚠️ᴹQ. quáquínen, adv. “*by all means” (Category: All, Every)
ᴺQ. !illinen “by all means”

The correlative ᴹQ. qáqínen appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/111), a combination of ᴹQ. qa(qe)- “all” and the instrumental suffix ᴹQ. -nen.

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. illinen “by all means” using il- “all”.

Reference ✧ PE23/111 ✧ “*by all means”

Elements

qa(qe)- “all, all the (with plurals), every; each, every”
-nen “instrumental”
qáqe “all, everyone, every person/thing” instrumental plural ✧ PE23/111 (qáqínen)
