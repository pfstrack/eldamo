ᴺQ. !verulóra adj. and n. “widowed; widow” (Category: Widow)

ᴺQ. !verulóra, adj. and n. “widowed; widow” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Widow)

A neologism for a “widow” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of veru “husband” and [ᴹQ.] -lóra “-less”, so literally “husband-less”.

Elements

Q. veru “husband”
ᴹQ. -lóra “without, less”
