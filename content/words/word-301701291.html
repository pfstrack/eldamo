Q. Heren Istarion pn. “Order of Wizards”

Q. Heren Istarion, pn. “Order of Wizards”

The Quenya name of the Order of Wizards to which Gandalf and Saruman belonged (UT/388). It is a compound of heren “order” and the genitive plural of Istar “Wizard”.
