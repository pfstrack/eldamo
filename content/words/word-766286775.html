Ilk. tiog adj. “thick, fat”

An adjective meaning “thick, fat” derived from primitive ᴹ✶tiu̯kā (Ety/TIW), where the primitive [iu] became [io] and the [k] voiced to [g] after the vowel, as was usual in Ilkorin.
