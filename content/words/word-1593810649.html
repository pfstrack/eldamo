      N. short [i], [u] became [e], [o] preceding final [a]; [-{ĭŭ}{C|CC}a] > [-{eo}{C|CC}a]

      GS/§4.95 WG/§68 @@@

      Order (01200)

      Before 02200 short final vowels vanished ON. pikina > N. pigen
      ON. ruska > N. rhosc             		Ety/PIK
      Ety/RŪSKA
      Before 02300 final [j] intruded into preceding syllable ᴹ✶gilya > N. geil
      ᴹ✶ulyā > œil > N. eil             		Ety/GIL
      Ety/ULU
      Before 04600 non-initial [h] vanished ON. nuhina > nohen > N. noen EtyAC/NUS
      Before 05000 [lð] became [ll] or [lt] ᴹ✶ulda > old > N. oll Ety/ULU
      Before 05100 [mb], [nd] became [mm], [nn] ᴹ✶tundā > tond > N. tonn Ety/TUN
      Before 05400 [mm] shortened ON. rimba > rhemb > N. rhem Ety/RIM
      Before 05500 [œ] became [e] ᴹ✶ulyā > œil > N. eil Ety/ULU
      Before 06700 initial [θl] sometimes became [fl] ᴹ√SLUS > thloss > N. floss Ety/SLUS
