S. hwinn n. “birch” (Category: Birch)

S. hwinn, n. “birch” (Category: Birch)

The ordinary non-mythological word for “birch” in notes from the mid-1960s, as opposed to brethil “silver-birch” for the subspecies associated with Elbereth (PE17/23).
