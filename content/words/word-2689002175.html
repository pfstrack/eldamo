⚠️ᴱ√MṆTYṆ root. “*memory”

An unglossed root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s with derivatives like ᴱQ. minti “memory” and ᴱQ. minty- “to remind” (QL/62). There are no signs of this root in Tolkien’s later writing.
