Q. Vinyamar loc. “New Dwelling”

Q. Vinyamar, loc. “New Dwelling”

Home of Turgon in Nevrast (S/115), translated “New Dwelling” by Christopher Tolkien (SI/Vinyamar). This name is probably a compound of vinya “new” and már “dwelling” (SA/bar).
