ᴺQ. !talmondo n. “foundation stone” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !talmondo, n. “foundation stone” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of Q. talma “foundation” and Q. ondo “stone”.

Elements

Q. #talma¹ “basis”
Q. ondo “stone (as a material), (large mass of) rock”
