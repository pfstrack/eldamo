ᴺQ. !cólolantë, n. “relief, (lit.) fall of the burden” [created by Delle] (Category: Emotion (other))

A neologism for “relief” coined by Delle posted on 2024-11-08 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of cólo “burden” and lanta “fall”.
