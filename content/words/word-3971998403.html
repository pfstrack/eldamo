ᴺQ. !falarië n. “splashing, splash” (Category: Splash, Spray)

ᴺQ. !falarië, n. “splashing, splash” (Category: Splash, Spray)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo, a gerund of the verb Q. ᴺQ. falarya- “to splash”, replacing the form ᴺQ. palastië from Helge Fauskanger’s Neo-Quenya New Testament that is a gerund of (similarly replaced) ᴺQ. palasta-.

Elements

^falarya- “to splash, ⚠️foam” gerund
