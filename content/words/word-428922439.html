ᴺQ. !tamala adv. “by the way, (lit.) beyond that matter” (Category: Conjunction)

ᴺQ. !tamala, adv. “by the way, (lit.) beyond that matter” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Conjunction)

A neologism for “by the way” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-09-27 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of tama “that matter” and lá “beyond”.

Elements

Q. tama “that matter”
Q. lá² “beyond, over, across, athwart”
