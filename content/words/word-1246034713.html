ᴱQ. Vendinuru f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Vendinuru, f.

Another name for ᴱQ. Urwen(di) (Arien) in the Gnomish Lexicon from the 1910s (GL/18), possibly a combination of variant form of wen(di) “maiden” with the root URU having to do with the Sun.
