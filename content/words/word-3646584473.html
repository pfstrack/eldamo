ᴺQ. !alon (alond-) n. “limestone, (lit.) grow-stone” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !alon (alond-), n. “limestone, (lit.) grow-stone” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism for “limestone” coined by Luinyelle in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of ala-¹ “grow” and ondo “stone”.

Elements

Q. ala-¹ “to plant, grow”
Q. ondo “stone (as a material), (large mass of) rock”
