ᴺQ. !forróna, adj. “northeastern” [created by Röandil] (Category: North)

A neologism coined by Röandil in 2023-08-31 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of ✶for- “north” with Q. (h)róna “eastern”.

Elements

✶for- “right-hand, north”
Q. (h)róna “eastern, east”
