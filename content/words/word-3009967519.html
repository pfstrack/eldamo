ᴺQ. !onya- v. “to be born, be begotten” (Category: to Bear (of Mother))

⚠️ᴺQ. !onya-, v. “to be born, be begotten” [created by Röandil] (Category: to Bear (of Mother))
Q. nosta- “to beget, [ᴱQ.] give birth to; [Q.] to be begotten, *be born [impersonal]; ⚠️[ᴱQ.] to cause”

A neologism coined by Röandil on 2023-07-30 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) from the root √ONO, with a half-strong past ononye or irregular strong past nónë; compare nóna “born”. I personally prefer to use a passive voice formation with the verb nosta- “to birth”: nosta ni “[it] birthed me” = “I was born”.
