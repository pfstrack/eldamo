ᴺQ. !mah- v. “to exchange” (Category: to Trade)

ᴺQ. !mah-, v. “to exchange” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Trade)

A neologism for “exchange” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), based on the root MBAKH of the same meaning. For example: mahuvan i yerna farma vinyan “I’ll exchange the old carpet for a new one”, mahin yo meldonya parmammali “my friend and I exchange some of our books”.
