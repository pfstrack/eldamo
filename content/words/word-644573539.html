S. Glanhír loc. “Boundary Stream”

S. Glanhír, loc. “Boundary Stream”

Sindarin name of the Mering Stream in Rohan, translated “boundary stream” (UT/318). This name is combination of glan(n) “border” and the lenited form of sîr “river”.
