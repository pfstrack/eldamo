ᴱQ. Kosartami coll.

⚠️ᴱQ. Kosartami, coll.

Qenya name for the people of Rog in an early name list (PE13/104), apparently a combination of kos “war” and the plural of artan “hammer”.

Reference ✧ PE13/104

Elements

kos “quarrel, dispute, the matter disputed, legal action”
artan “hammer” plural

Cognates

  • G. Thlim Gothodrum “*Folk of the Hammer” ✧ PE13/104
