ᴱQ. palasar (palasard-) n. “great table” (Category: Table)

⚠️ᴱQ. palasar (palasard-), n. “great table” (Category: Table)
ᴺQ. ^paluhta “table”

A noun in Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “great table”, apparently a combination of the root ᴱ√PALA having to do with flat things and a variant form of ᴱQ. sarno “table” (QL/71, 82).
