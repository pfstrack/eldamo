Q. lomentie n. “?away-journey, departure” (Category: to Go)

Q. lomentië, n. “?away-journey, departure” (Category: to Go)

An unglossed word in Late Notes on Verb Structure (LVS) from 1969 (PE22/16). It is perhaps the opposite of omentië “meeting = together-journey”, using an initial prefix lo- “away from” rather o- “together”, so meaning “away-journey = departure”.

Reference ✧ PE22/168

Related

  • lomentie “?away-journey, departure” vs. omentië “meeting (of pathways), (lit.) coming together of journey-path, meeting or junction of the directions of two people”

Elements

ló “(away) from; by agent [of]”
mentië “passage, journey, direction of travel”
✶lō¹ “ablative, from (a place within the world)” ✧ PE22/168
