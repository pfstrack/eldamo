N. Dolwethil f. “(Woman of) Secret Shadow”

N. Dolwethil, f. “(Woman of) Secret Shadow”

Noldorin equivalent of Ilk. Thuringwethil (Ety/THUR), a combination of doll “secret” with the lenited form of gwath¹ “shadow” where the [a] changed to [e] because of the following [i], ending with the feminine suffix -iel¹.
