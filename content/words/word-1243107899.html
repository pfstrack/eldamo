S. Lond Daer Enedh loc. “Great Middle Haven”

S. Lond Daer Enedh, loc. “Great Middle Haven”

A longer name for Lond Daer, translated “Great Middle Haven” (UT/264), a combination of lond “haven”, daer¹ “great” and enedh “middle”.
