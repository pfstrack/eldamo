S. calar n. “(portable) lamp” (Category: Lamp)

S. calar, n. “(portable) lamp” (Category: Lamp)

A noun for a “(portable) lamp” in the word calardan “lampwright” (PE17/96; RC/523, PE23/136), which appear as a plural in the name Rath Celerdain “Lampwrights’ Street” (LotR/768).

Conceptual Development: The Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s had G. dant “lamp”, but this word was deleted (GL/29).
