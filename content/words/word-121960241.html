Q. Úmaneldi, coll. “*Elves not of Aman”

Q. Úmaneldi, coll. “*Elves not of Aman”

A term for Elves who did not reach Aman, a longer form of Úmanyar (WJ/373). It is a combination of the negative prefix ú- and Amaneldi, the “Elves of Aman”.
