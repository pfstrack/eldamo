Q. lacaraitë adj. “impossible” (Category: Action)

Q. lacaraitë, adj. “*inactive; ⚠️impossible” (Category: Action)

A word appearing as {alákăraite >>} lắkăraite “impossible” in rough notes within Late Notes on Verb Structure (LVS), a negated form of karaite (PE22/156). The unnegated adjective was elsewhere glossed “active, busy” (PE22/155).

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I think it is safer to assume this adjective means “*inactive”, and that “impossible” is [ᴺQ.] alcárima, the negative form of cárima “possible”.
