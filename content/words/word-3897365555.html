Q. Nand’ Ondoluncava loc. “Stonewain Valley”

A Quenya name of the hidden Stonewain Valley in the Druadan Forest (LotR/833, PE17/28). It is a compound of the elided form nand’ of nan(do) “valley” and the possessive-adjectival form of ondolunca “stonewain”.
