ᴺQ. !findimaitar n. “barber, hairdresser” (Category: Hair (other))

ᴺQ. !findimaitar, n. “barber, hairdresser” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Hair (other))

A neologism for “barber, hairdresser” coined by Tamas Ferencz, a combination of Q. findë “hair” and Q. maitar “artist”.

Elements

Q. findë “hair (especially of the head), tress or plait of hair”
Q. maitar “artist; poet”
