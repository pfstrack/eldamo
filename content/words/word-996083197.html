G. nerthi n. “manhood, manliness; virtue” (Category: Male)

⚠️G. nerthi, n. “manhood, manliness; virtue” (Category: Male)
ᴺS. ^anwas “manhood, *manliness, masculinity”

A word in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “manhood, manliness; virtue”, an elaboration of G. nert “prowess, a feat, strength” (GL/60).
