S. Adanedhel m. “Elf-man”

S. Adanedhel, m. “Elf-man”

A name given to Túrin in Nargothrond for his noble, Elf-like nature, translated “Elf-man” (S/210). It is a compound of Adan “man” and Edhel “Elf” (SA/adan, edhel).
