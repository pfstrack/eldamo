ᴱQ. losille n. “rose”

⚠️ᴱQ. losillë, n. “rose”
ᴺQ. !merillë “rose”

A variant of ᴱQ. losse “rose” in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa of the 1910s (QL/56; PME/56). In later writings lossë was associated with white flowers, not roses.
