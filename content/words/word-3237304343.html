ᴺQ. !autulya- v. “to deport, (lit.) bring away” (Category: to Bring)

ᴺQ. !autulya-, v. “to deport, (lit.) bring away” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: to Bring)

A neologism for “to deport” coined by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of au- “away (from)” and tulya- “lead”, appearing primarily in its noun form ᴺQ. autulyalë “deportation”.

Elements

Q. au- “away (from)”
Q. tulya- “*to lead; to fetch”
