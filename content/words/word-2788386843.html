ᴺQ. !catassa n. “anus, arsehole, (lit.) behind-hole” (Category: to Void Excrement; Dung, Excrement)

ᴺQ. !catassa, n. “anus, arsehole, (lit.) behind-hole” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: to Void Excrement; Dung, Excrement)

A neologism coined by Tamas Ferencz for “anus, arsehole”, a combination of cata “behind” and [ᴹQ.] assa and hence more literally “behind-hole”.

Elements

Q. #ca(ta) “behind, at back of place”
ᴹQ. assa¹ “hole, perforation, opening, mouth”
