ᴹQ. hinima pron. “*of this kind (by us)” (Category: Pronoun)

⚠️ᴹQ. hinima, pron. “*of this kind (by us)” (Category: Pronoun)
Q. -itë “adjectival ending; [with verbs] capable of doing, generally (and naturally) doing”

A correlative combination appearing in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/107), a combination of ᴹQ. hi “this by you” and ᴹQ. -ima “kind”.

Reference ✧ PE23/107 ✧ “*of this kind (by us)”

Elements

hi “here, now (for both you and me)”
-ima “able to be done, -able, -ible”
