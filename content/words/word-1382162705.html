⚠️ᴱQ. lutto-, v. “to swill, wash” (Category: to Wash)
Q. #hwal- “to wash”

A verb appearing as {lutta- >>} lutto- “swill, wash” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√LUTU, but Tolkien ultimately rejected this verb (QL/57).
