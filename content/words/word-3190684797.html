ᴺQ. !penyalë n. “poverty, deprivation” (Category: Poor)

ᴺQ. !penyalë, n. “poverty, deprivation” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Poor)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo as a replacement for ᴱQ. ois(t)e “poverty”, based on the later word Q. penya “lacking”.

Elements

Q. penya “lacking, inadequate”

      ᴱQ. oise n. “poverty” (Category: Poor)
