ᴺQ. !arasso n. “hart, stag, (male) deer” (Category: Deer)

ᴺQ. !arasso, n. “hart, stag, (male) deer” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Deer)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2022 specifically for Eldamo, the Quenya equivalent of S. aras based on primitive ✶aras(sō) (UT/150; PE21/82). Boris Shapiro instead suggested ᴺQ. arassë in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, but this was before the primitive form was published in PE21.
