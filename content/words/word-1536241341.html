ᴺQ. !olmos (olmoss-), n. “chestnut tree, *chestnut” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Tree (other))
ᴱQ. mavoisi “chestnut”

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2023 specifically for Eldamo, inspired by G. omlos of the same meaning (GL/62).
