G. mugli n. “calf” (Category: Calf)

⚠️G. mugli, n. “calf” (Category: Calf)
ᴺS. !munneg “calf”

A word appearing as G. mugli “calf” in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s, apparently some kind of diminutive form of G. mû “ox” (GL/58).

Neo-Sindarin: For Neo-Sindarin I would adapt this word as ᴺS. munneg, a diminutive of later S. mund “bull”.
