S. Abonnen pn. “After-born”

S. Abonnen, pn. “After-born”

A term for Men as the second-born children of Ilúvatar, after the Elves, also attested in its plural form Eboennin (WJ/387). It seems to be a compound of ab “after” and onnen “born”.
