ᴺQ. !ramya- v. “to fly, sail; to wander” (Category: to Fly)

⚠️ᴺQ. !ramya-, v. “to fly, sail; to wander” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: to Fly)
ᴹQ. vil- “to fly, ⚠️[ᴱQ.] float, sail”
ᴹQ. ciryasta- “to sail [a ship], *make a ship go”
ᴹQ. ranya- “to stray, *wander”

A neologism for “fly, sail; wander” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s based on ᴹ✶ramya- > N. rhenio with the same glosses. All of these glosses have other attested verbs, so I would use those instead: [ᴹQ.] vil- “fly”, [ᴹQ.] ciryasta- “sail”, and [ᴹQ.] ranya- “wander”.
