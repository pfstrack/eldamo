          ᴱQ. Makarnisan pn. “Saturday”

          Name of the first Saturday in the Valinorean fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the god Makar and sana “day”.

          Reference ✧ PE14/22 ✧ “Saturday”

          Elements

          Makar “God of Battle”
          sana “day (24 hours)”

              ᴱQ. Sainen pn. “Saturday”

              Name of Saturday in the seven-day week of the Elves (otsola) in an early word list (PE14/21). The day was related to fire and metals, and was probably derived from sá “fire”.

              Reference ✧ PE14/21 ✧ “Saturday”
