ᴺS. [G.] ^cuinas n. “vitality, liveliness” (Category: to Live; Living; Life)

ᴺS. [G.] ^cuinas, n. “vitality, liveliness” (Category: to Live; Living; Life)
G. cuilogri “liveliness”
G. laus “juice, sap; energy, vitality; (aj.) fresh”
See G. cuibri for discussion.

Elements

N. cuin “alive”

      G. cuibri n. “vitality” (Category: to Live; Living; Life)

      A word for “vitality” in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s, a noun form of G. cuib “alive” (GL/27).

      Neo-Sindarin: I would adapt this word as ᴺS. cuinas “vitality, liveliness”, based on the later adjective [N.] cuin “alive”.
