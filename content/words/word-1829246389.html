ᴱQ. timpina adj. “fine, powdered, sprayed” (Category: Splash, Spray)

⚠️ᴱQ. timpina, adj. “fine, powdered, sprayed” (Category: Splash, Spray)
Q. vingë “foam, spindrift, spray, [ᴹQ.] wave crest, [ᴱQ.] froth, scud; ⚠️wave”

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “fine, powdered, sprayed”, an adjectival form of ᴱQ. timpine (QL/92).
