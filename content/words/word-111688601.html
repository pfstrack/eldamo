Ad. verb-classes grammar.

Ad. verb-classes grammar.

From Tolkien’s statements (SD/439), we know that there are three classes of Adûnaic verbs:

  • biconsonantal-verb: For example kan- “to hold”.
  • triconsonantal-verb: For example kalab- “to fall (down)”.
  • derived-verb: For example ugrudâ- “to overshadow”.
