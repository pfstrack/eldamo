N. Siriombar loc. “Sirion’s Haven”

N. Siriombar loc. “Sirion’s Haven”

Name of the settlement of Tuor and the remnants of Doriath at the mouth of the river Sirion appearing in a name list from the 1930s (LR/407), a combination of that river name and bâr “home”.
