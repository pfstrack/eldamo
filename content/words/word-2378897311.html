ᴺQ. !hruhirdië n. “dysentery” (Category: Sick; Sickness)

ᴺQ. !hruhirdië, n. “dysentery” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Sick; Sickness)

A neologism for “dysentery” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴺQ.] hirdë “bowels” with the prefix hru- “evil”.

Elements

Q. hru- “evil-”
!hirdë “entrails, bowels”
