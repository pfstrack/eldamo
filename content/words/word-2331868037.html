ᴺQ. !öamo, n. “owner” [created by Robert Reynolds, 1000W] (Category: to Own, Possess)

A neologism for “owner” coined by Robert Reynolds in 2018 as part of the 1000 Words project (1000W) as an agental form of öa- “own”. Ales Bican’s New Words (ABNW) from the early 2000s instead had harno “possessor” based on [ᴹQ.] harya- “possess”.

Elements

Q. öa- “to possess, own, keep”
