ᴱQ. Tetille f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Tetillë, f.

Name of a poppy-inhabiting fairy mentioned only in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/92; PME/92), apparently an elaboration of tetl “small flower”.
