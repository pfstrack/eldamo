ᴺQ. !cemellet (cemellept-) n. “asparagus, (lit.) earth-finger” (Category: Vegetables)

ᴺQ. !cemellet (cemellept-), n. “asparagus, (lit.) earth-finger” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Vegetables)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-05-23 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of cemen “earth” and a reduced form of lepetā “finger”.

Elements

Q. cemen “the earth; earth”
✶lepetā “thumb, ‘picker’; finger”
