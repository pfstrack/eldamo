ᴺQ. !ninteccon (ninteccond-) n. “chalk, (lit.) white writing rock” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !ninteccon (ninteccond-), n. “chalk, (lit.) white writing rock” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism for “chalk” coined by Luinyelle in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of ninquë “white”, [ᴹQ.] tek- “write” and ondo “stone”.

Elements

Q. ninquë “white; pallid, chill, cold”
ᴹQ. tek- “to write”
Q. ondo “stone (as a material), (large mass of) rock”
