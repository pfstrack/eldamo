ᴺQ. !enortalë n. “resurrection” (Category: to Bear (of Mother))

ᴺQ. !enortalë, n. “resurrection” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: to Bear (of Mother))

A neologism for “resurrection” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of en- “again” and an abstract noun form of orta- “rise”, hence “*rising again”. He also used ᴺQ. enontië base on Q. onta- “beget”, but I prefer enortalë.

Elements

Q. en- “re-, again”
Q. orta-¹ “to raise, cause to rise, lift (up)”
