Q. Atyamar loc. “*Second Home”

Q. Atyamar, loc. “*Second Home”
Name of a region in the vale of Anduin where the Eldar briefly settled during a break on their march to Valinor, in one chronology of the march written around 1959 (NM/51). It is a combination of Q. atya “second” and Q. már “home”, as suggested by Carl Hostetter (NM/56 note #12).
