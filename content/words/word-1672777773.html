Q. Írimon m.

Q. Írimon, m.
The given name of Tar-Meneldur (UT/219). The elements of this name are difficult to discern, but might be írima “desireable” combined with the masculine suffix -on¹.

References ✧ UT/219; UTI

Elements

írima “desirable, lovely, beautiful”
-on¹ “masculine suffix”
