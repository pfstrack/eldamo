N. caeleb adj. “bedridden, sick” (Category: Sick; Sickness)

N. caeleb, adj. “bedridden, sick” (Category: Sick; Sickness)
G. cômathol “diseased, ill”

A word in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “bedridden, sick”, an adjectival form of the noun N. cael “bedridden, sick” (Ety/KAY).
