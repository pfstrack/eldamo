ᴱQ. Solóre f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Solórë, f.

Another name for Ónen in early Valar name lists (LT1A/Ónen), likely derived from solor “surf, surge” as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Solosimpi).
