ᴺQ. !vor- v. “to endure, last, continue” (Category: to Last)

ᴺQ. !vor-, v. “to endure, last, continue” [created by Robert B Wilson, Luinyelle, Arael] (Category: to Last)

A neologism originally proposed by Robert B Wilson in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s with the sense “endure, survive” as the equivalent of N. brona- of the same meaning. In a conversation on 2025-06-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), Luinyelle and Arael instead suggested this verb should mean “last, continue” to be closer in sense to the basic root.
