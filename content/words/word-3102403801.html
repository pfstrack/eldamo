ᴺQ. !hwalmë, n. “bathing, bath” [created by Gilruin] (Category: to Wash)

A neologism for “washing, bathing” coined by Gilruin posted on 2024-10-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), based on √SWAL “wash”. I would use this word for the act of bathing, as opposed to ᴺQ. súnë for a “bath = place to bathe”.
