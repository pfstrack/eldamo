ᴺS. !io conj. “when” (Category: Conjunction)

ᴺS. !io, conj. “when” [created by Röandil] (Category: Conjunction)

A neologism coined by Röandil as io “ago” posted on 2024-03-19 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), the Sindarin equivalent of Q. yá “when”. In stressed positions, this conjunction might remain iaw. Both of these forms conflict with existing Noldorin words: [N.] io “ago” and [N.] iau “corn”.

Cognates

  • Q. yá “when”
