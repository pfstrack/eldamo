ᴺS. !iaw conj. “when” (Category: Conjunction)

ᴺS. !iaw, conj. “when” [created by Röandil] (Category: Conjunction)

A neologism coined by Röandil posted on 2024-03-19 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), the Sindarin equivalent of Q. yá “when”. In unstressed positions, this conjunction might reduce to io, but that conflicts with adverbial [N.] io “ago”. Note that iaw also conflicts with the noun [N.] iau “corn”, but I think a conflict between a conjunction and a noun is less problematic.

Cognates

  • Q. yá “when”

Derivations

  • < √YA “*there, over there; (of time) back, ago”
