ᴺQ. !laicelumir (laicelumír-) n. “malachite (lit.) green-flowing-jewel” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !laicelumir (laicelumír-), n. “malachite (lit.) green-flowing-jewel” [created by Valerie] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “malachite” coined by Valerie in 2022-07-05 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of laica “green”, celumë “flowing”, and mírë “jewel”.

Elements

Q. laica “green”
Q. celumë “flowing, flood (tide), stream”
Q. mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
