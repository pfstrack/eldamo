S. Tol Sirion loc. “*Island of Sirion”

An island in the river Sirion (S/120), a combination of tol “island” and the river name.

Conceptual Development: In Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, this name appeared as Tolsirion (LR/128).

References ✧ LT2I; SI; WJI

Elements

tol “island, (high steep-sided) isle”
Sirion “Great River”
