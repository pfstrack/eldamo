G. fung adj. “dark (esp. of colour)” (Category: Dark, Murky)

⚠️G. fung, adj. “dark (esp. of colour)” (Category: Dark, Murky)
S. morn “black, dark; ⚠️night”

An adjective in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “dark (esp. of colour)” derived from ᴱ√fuʒu (GL/36), but that was likely just the Gnomish variant of the early root ᴱ√ǶUẎU (QL/41).
