ᴺQ. !cuina- v. “to live, be alive” (Category: to Live; Living; Life)

⚠️ᴺQ. !cuina-, v. “to live, be alive” [created by ABNW] (Category: to Live; Living; Life)
Q. #coita- “[ᴹQ.] to live, be alive, have life”

A neologism for the verb “to live, be alive” appearing in Ales Bican’s New Words (ABNW) from the early 2000’s based on [ᴹQ.] kuina “alive”. I prefer using attested coita- for this purpose; this verb was published in 2015.
