S. Thinnedhel pn. “Grey-elf”

S. Thinnedhel, pn. “Grey-elf”

A Sindarin term for a “Grey-elf”, equivalent of Q. Sinda, a combination of thind “grey” and Edhel “Elf” (PE17/139).
