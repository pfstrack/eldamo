ᴺQ. !accasúcala, adj. “over-drinking” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: to Drink)

A neologism for “over-drinking” (adjective) coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴱQ.] akka “too” and the active participle of [ᴹQ.] suc- “drink”. @@@ dubious neologism, revisit
