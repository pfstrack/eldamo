Q. Alatar, m.

Q. Alatar, m.

One of the Ithryn Luin (Blue Wizards) who went to the East and South (UT/394). The meaning of his name is unclear, though the final element seems to be -tar “-lord” as seen in other names like Annatar and Sorontar.
