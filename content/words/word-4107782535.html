S. verbs grammar.
Verbs in Sindarin serve much the same function as they do in other languages, indicating the action of a phrase. Sindarin verbs are inflected for tense, number and person, but the “person” inflections come mostly in the form of pronominal subject suffixes, which are discussed under pronouns. Other details of verbal conjugation are discussed in the following entries under verbs.
