Q. Sinda [þ] pn. “Grey-elf”

Q. Sinda [þ], pn. “Grey-elf”

Quenya name for the Grey Elves (PM/223), usually appearing in its plural form Sindar (LotR/1137). It is simply sinda “grey” used as a name (SA/thin(d)).
