ᴱQ. histe- v. “to hiss” (Category: Snake)

⚠️ᴱQ. histe-, v. “to hiss” (Category: Snake)
ᴺQ. !surya- “to hiss”

A Qenya word mentioned in the Early Noldorin Dictionary of the 1920s with the gloss “hisses”, derived from primitive ᴱ✶sṣt- (PE13/163).
