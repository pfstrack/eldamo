ᴱQ. Kúne Malle loc. “Road of Arches”

⚠️ᴱQ. Kúne Mallë, loc. “Road of Arches”

Street name in an early name list (PE13/105), appearing nowhere else. It is a combination of kúne “arch” and malle “road”.
