ᴹQ. mallo adv. “whence” (Category: What)

ᴹQ. mallo, adv. “whence” (Category: What)
ᴹQ. manomello “*from what place”

A correlatives meaning “whence” appearing in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112), a combination of interrogative ᴹQ. ma and the ablative suffix ᴹQ. -llo. It also appeared in Quenya Verbal System (QVS) from this same period (PE22/124).
