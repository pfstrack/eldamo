?ᴱ√FAPA root.

⚠️?ᴱ√FAPA root.
√MAH “handle, manage, control, wield; ⚠️serve, be of use; [ᴹ√] hand; [ᴱ√] grasp”
√PĀ/APA “touch; after, behind of place”

A hypothetical early root to explain words in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s such as G. fab- “handle, touch, feel” and G. famp “handling, touching, feeling” (GL/33). There are no signs of this root in Tolkien’s later writing.

    Derivatives

    • > G. fab- “to handle, touch, feel”
      • ⇒ G. fabli “sense of touch” ✧ GL/33
      • > G. famp “handling, touching, feeling; stroking, caressing, ‘fuss’; fingering, appraising, assay”
        • ⇒ G. famfa- “to maul” ✧ GL/33
