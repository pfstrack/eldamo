Q. Maitimo m. “Well-shaped One”

Q. Maitimo, m. “Well-shaped One”

The mother-name of Maedhros (PM/353). It seems to be a compound of maitë “shapely” and the agental suffix -mo, translated “Well-shaped One” (PM/353, 366).
