ᴱQ. pirpiri- v. “to twirl, twizzle” (Category: to Spin)

⚠️ᴱQ. pirpiri-, v. “to twirl, twizzle” (Category: to Spin)

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “twirl, twizzle”, an elaboration of ᴱQ. piri- “spin, turn” (QL/74).
