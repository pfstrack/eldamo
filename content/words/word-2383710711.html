ᴱQ. Makar (Makarn-) m. “God of Battle”

⚠️ᴱQ. Macar (Macarn-), m. “God of Battle”

God of Battle in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/67). His name is derived from the root ᴱ√MAKA¹ “slay” (QL/57), but there was also a “popular etymology” of his name giving it the sense “Red Hand” (GL/18; PE14/13), as a combination of má “hand” and karne “red”.
