G. nert n. “prowess, a feat, strength” (Category: Strong, Mighty, Powerful)

⚠️G. nert, n. “prowess, a feat, strength” (Category: Strong, Mighty, Powerful)
N. bellas “bodily strength”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “prowess, a feat, strength” based on the early root ᴱ√NERE having to do with manliness (GL/60).
