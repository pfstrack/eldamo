Q. #attalya n. and adj. “biped, *(lit.) two-footed”

Q. #attalya, n. and adj. “biped, *(lit.) two-footed”

A noun or adjective meaning “two-legged”, attested only in its (noun) plural form attalyar (WJ/389). It is a combination of at(a)- “bi” and tál “foot”, with the adjective suffix -ya¹.

Conceptual Development: In the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s, there were similar forms: the noun ᴱQ. attalin and the adjective ᴱQ. attalaite (QL/88).
