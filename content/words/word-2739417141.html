Q. attalya adj. “biped, (lit.) two-footed”

An adjective meaning “two-legged” (WJ/389). It is a combination of atta “two” and tál “foot”, with the adjective suffix -ya¹.

Conceptual Development: In the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s, there was a similar form ᴱQ. attalin (QL/88).
