ᴺQ. !ályë interj. “go ahead” (Category: to Go)

ᴺQ. !ályë, interj. “go ahead” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Go)

A neologism for “go ahead” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), as a combination of imperative á and lye “you (polite)”. Familiar and plural variants átye and álde also exist.

Elements

Q. á “imperative particle”
Q. lye “you (polite)”
