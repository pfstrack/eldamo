ᴺQ. !öapsar- v. “to wipe away” (Category: to Rub)

ᴺQ. !öapsar-, v. “to wipe away” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: to Rub)

Helge Fauskanger coined this neologism as aupsar- “wipe away” for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of au- “away” and psar- “rub”. This neologism is rather dubious because normally a diphthong like au cannot occur before a consonant cluster like ps. I think it would be better to use öapsar- where the initial element is öa “away”.

Elements

Q. öa “away (of movement)”
Q. †psar- “to rub”
