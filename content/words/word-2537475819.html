ᴹQ. tolu-, v. (u-verb) “to stand up, get up, leave one’s seat” (Category: to Stand)

A verb appearing in the Quenya Verbal System (QVS) from 1948 based on the root ᴹ√TOL “stand”, given as an example of the inceptive [marking the beginning of an action] use of the verbal extension u (PE22/114).

Conceptual Development: In the Qenya Lexicon, ᴱQ. tolu- was glossed “roll up, furl” under the early root ᴱ√TOL+U of similar meaning, contrasted with ᴱQ. velu- “unfurl” (QL/94).
