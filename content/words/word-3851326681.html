ᴺQ. !hristomar (hristomard-), n. “church (building), Lord’s house” [created by Parmandil] (Category: Temple)
ᴺQ. !eruyána “building for the worship of Eru (church, synagogue, mosque)”

A neologism for “church (building)” coined by Parmandil posted on 2025-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of Hristo “Christ” and mar(da) “house”.
