S. Faramir m.

S. Faramir, m.

Brother of Boromir (LotR/657). The meaning of his name is unclear, but if it is a mixed name like his brother’s, the final element might be from Q. mírë “jewel”.

Conceptual Development: In Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s, this name first appeared as N. Falborn¹, revised to N. Faramir (WR/149).
