S. Sammath Naur loc. “Chambers of Fire”

S. Sammath Naur, loc. “Chambers of Fire”

The heart of Mount Doom, translated “Chambers of Fire” (LotR/942), a combination of the class-plural of sam “chamber” and the noun naur “fire”.
