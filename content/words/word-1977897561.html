S. Amon Sûl loc. “Weathertop, (lit.) Hill of the Wind”

S. Amon Sûl, loc. “Weathertop, (lit.) Hill of the Wind”

The Sindarin name of Weathertop (LotR/185), more literally “Hill of the Wind” (RC/778). It is a combination of amon “hill” and sûl “wind”.
