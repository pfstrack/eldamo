ᴱQ. Lotesse f.

A name for Erinti in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/55; PME/56), an elaboration of lóte “flower”. This name also appeared as Lotisse (QL/36)
