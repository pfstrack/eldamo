ᴺQ. !félula adj. “sensitive, (lit.) apt to feel” (Category: Feeling, Emotion)

ᴺQ. !félula, adj. “sensitive, (lit.) apt to feel” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Feeling, Emotion)

A neologism for “sensitive” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-11-03 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of [ᴺQ.] fel- “to feel” and -ula “apt to”, so more literally “apt to feel”.

Elements

!fel- “to feel (emotions)”
Q. *-ula “likelihood or aptitude”

Element In
