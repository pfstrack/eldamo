ᴺQ. !helléna n. “horizon, (lit.) sky-edge” (Category: Sky, Heavens)

ᴺQ. !helléna, n. “horizon, (lit.) sky-edge” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Sky, Heavens)

A neologism for “horizon” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-03-01 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of ᴹ√ƷEL “sky” (cf. [ᴹQ.] helle “sky”) and [ᴹQ.] réna “edge” with lr > ll. Thus its more literal meaning is “sky-edge”.

Elements

ᴹ√ƷEL “sky”
ᴹQ. réna “edge, border, margin”
