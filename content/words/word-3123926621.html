ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] pairemo n. “avenger” (Category: Penalty, Punishment)

ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] pairemo, n. “avenger” (Category: Penalty, Punishment)

Elements

!pairë “vengeance, revenge”

      ᴱQ. Pairemo m. “The Avenger”

      An otherwise unexplained name appearing in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s as a derivative of the root ᴱ√PAẎA “punish” (QL/72; PME/72). It also appeared as Paimar.
