N. ûn¹ n. “creature” (Category: Animal)

N. ûn¹, n. “creature” (Category: Animal)

A noun in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “creature” derived from the root ᴹ√ONO “beget” (Ety/ONO), perhaps from a primitive form *ōno with ancient ō becoming ū.
