    Phonetic Development

    ᴹAQ. voiceless stops were voiced before nasals gm < km ✧ PE19/50 (km > gm)
    ᴹAQ. voiced stops became nasals before nasals gm > ŋm ✧ PE19/50 (gm > ñm)
    ᴹAQ. voiced stops became spirants except after nasals and liquids g > ɣ ✧ PE19/51 (gy > ʒy); PE19/51 (gw > ʒw)
    ᴹAQ. [ɣ] between a vowel and [e] and became [j] Vɣe > Vje ✧ PE19/51 (g > y; before ḗ)
    ᴹQ. [ɣ] from [g] vanished ɣ > ø ✧ PE19/51 (lgw > lw); PE19/51 (rgw > rw)
