ᴺQ. !palallon n. “telephone, (lit.) far-sound” (Category: Tool)

ᴺQ. !palallon, n. “telephone, (lit.) far-sound” [created by ABNW] (Category: Tool)

A neologism appearing in Ales Bican’s New Words (ABNW) from the early 2000’s, a combination of palan “sound” and hlón(a) “sound”. The Neo-Quenya Wiki instead had ᴺQ. palanóma where the second element was óma “voice”, but in this case I prefer the older neologism.

Elements

Q. palan “far, wide, afar, distant”
Q. hlón(a) “noise, sound”
