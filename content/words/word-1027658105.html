Ed. Eilinel f.

Ed. Eilinel f.

Wife of Gorlim the Unhappy (S/162), the language and meaning of her name is unclear, but she was one of the Edain.

Conceptual Development: Her name was ᴱN. Eilinel when it first appeared in the Lays of Beleriand from the 1920s (LB/162) and remained N. Eilinel in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (LR/297), but at no point did Tolkien translate her name.
