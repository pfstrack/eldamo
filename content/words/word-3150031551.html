S. Gladhwen f. “Laughing Maiden”

S. Gladhwen, f. “Laughing Maiden”

The Sindarin equivalent of Lalwendë, a combination of gladh- “laugh” and the suffixal form -wen of gwend “maiden” (PM/343, 359 note #29).
