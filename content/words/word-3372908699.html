G. grondrui n. “oakwood” (Category: Oak)

⚠️G. grondrui, n. “oakwood” (Category: Oak)

A word in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “an oakwood”, a combination of G. grôn “oak” and G. drui “wood” (GL/30, 42).
