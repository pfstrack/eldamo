⚠️ᴱQ. fanórë, n. “day-dream” (Category: Dream)
ᴺQ. !cuilórë “day-dream, (lit.) awake-dream”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “a day-dream” under the early root ᴱ√FANA (QL/37).
