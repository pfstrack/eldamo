N. Elennor loc. “*Elf-land”

A Noldorin name for Ilk. Eglador appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/ELED), apparently a combination of Eledh “Elf” and (n)dôr “land”, with the dh lost because [ð] vanished before nasals in Noldorin.
