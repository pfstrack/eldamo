S. †Glinnel pn. “*Singer-elf”

S. †Glinnel, pn. “*Singer-elf”

An archaic Sindarin term for the Telerin people, equivalent to Q. Linda and used only in lore (WJ/378). It is a combination of a modified form of lind¹ “song” and †Ell “Elf”.
