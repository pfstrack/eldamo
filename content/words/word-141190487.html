Q. Calmacil m. “*Sword of Light, Shining Sword”

Q. Calmacil, m. “*Sword of Light, Shining Sword”

Tar-Calmacil was the Quenya name of 18th ruler of Númenor, also known as Ad. Ar-Belzagar (UT/222). This name seems to be a compound of cala “light” and macil “sword”.
