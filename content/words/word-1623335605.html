N. Narogardh loc. “realm of Narog”

N. Narogardh, loc. “realm of Narog”

Apparently an alternate name for Nargothrond appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/NÁRAK), a combination of the river name Narog and ardh “realm”.
