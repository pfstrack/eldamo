ᴱQ. Taruktarna loc. “Oxford”

⚠️ᴱQ. Taructarna, loc. “Oxford”

A Qenya name for Oxford appear in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s as a combination of tarukko “ox” and tarna “ford” (QL/89).
