N. Aran Dinnu pn. “King of Twilight”

N. Aran Dinnu, pn. “King of Twilight”

The Noldorin equivalent of Thingol’s title Ilk. Tor Tinduma, a combination of aran “king” and the lenited form of tinnu “twilight” (Ety/THIN, TIN).
