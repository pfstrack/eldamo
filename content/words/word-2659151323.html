S. Torhir Ifant m.

S. Torhir Ifant, m.

Author of Dorgannas Iaur (WJ/192). His first name seems to be a combination of [N.] taur² “mighty” and hîr “lord”, while his second name is simply iphant “aged” (as suggested by David Salo, GS/358).
