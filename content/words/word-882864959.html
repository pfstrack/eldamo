      Phonetic Development

      ᴱQ. short syllabic [ḷ] became [il], or [ul] before labials il < ḷ ✧ PE12/10 (ḷ short + k > il); PE12/10 (ḷ short + k̑ > il); PE12/10 (ḷ short + t > il)
      ᴱQ. short syllabic [ṛ] became [ar], [ir] before palatalized consonants or [ur] after [w] and [kʷ] irCʲ < ṛCʲ ✧ PE12/10 (ṛ short + k̑ > ir)
      ᴱQ. [jǝ], [wǝ] became [je], [wo] after a vowel eje < ejǝ ✧ PE12/12 (éı̯ǝ > ie); PE12/13 (eı̯ǝ́ > ié)
      ᴱQ. [j], [w] became [i], [u] after a vowel Vi < Vj ✧ PE12/14 (ī̆ > ı̯); PE12/20 (y̑ > i)
      ᴱQ. [ei] became [e] before [a], [o] or [u], but [i] before [e] ie < eie ✧ PE12/12 (éı̯e > ie); PE12/13 (eı̯é > ié)
