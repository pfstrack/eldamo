ᴱQ. †[ð]

⚠️ᴱQ. †[ð]

References ✧ PE12/16, 19-20

    Phonetic Development

    ᴱQ. voiced stops became spirants after vowels Vð < Vd ✧ PE12/16 (d > z; medial)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids rð < rθ ✧ PE12/19 (liquids + þ > rđ)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids lð < lθ ✧ PE12/19 (liquids + þ > lđ)
    ᴱQ. final voiceless spirants were weakened and voiced -ð < -θ ✧ PE12/20 (þ > đ)
    ᴱQ. intervocalic voiceless spirants became voiced VðV < VθV ✧ PE12/16 (þ > z; medial)
    ᴱQ. voiced spirants becames stops after liquids, nasals and [z] lð > ld ✧ PE12/19 (lđ > ld)
    ᴱQ. voiced spirants becames stops after liquids, nasals and [z] rð > rd ✧ PE12/19 (rđ > rd)
    ᴱQ. [z] became [r] z > r ✧ PE12/16 (z > r; medial); PE12/16 (z > r; medial)
    ᴱQ. [ð] became [z] ð > z ✧ PE12/20 (đ > z)
