Ed. Gorlim m.

Ed. Gorlim m.

Companions of Barahir who betrayed him to the forces of Morgoth (S/162-3). The language and meaning of his name are unclear.

Conceptual Development: This character was already named ᴱN. Gorlim was he first appeared in the Lays of Beleriand from the 1920s (LB/162), and remained N. Gorlim in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (SM/300, LR/282).
