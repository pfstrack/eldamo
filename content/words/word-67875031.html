ᴱQ. sal- v. “?to remain or remove” (Category: to Remain, be Left Over)

⚠️ᴱQ. sal-, v. “?to remain or remove” (Category: to Remain, be Left Over)

A verb in Early Qenya Word-lists of the 1920s with a past form salle glossed “remained”, though the gloss is difficult to read and might be “removed” instead as suggested by Patrick Wynne and Christopher Gilson (PE16/143).
