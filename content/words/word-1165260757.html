G. ho(n)- pref. “grandson of” (Category: Grandson)

⚠️G. ho(n)-, pref. “grandson of” (Category: Grandson)

A prefix for “grandson of” in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s related to G. sion “grandson”, derived from primitive ᴱ✶sı̯on where sy > χ > h (GL/49). Tolkien specified that it caused n-mutation as a prefix.
