Q. Manwendil m. “*Friend of Manwë”

Q. Manwendil, m. “*Friend of Manwë”
Third child of Elros, known only from a genealogy chart on UT/210. His name seems to be a compound of Manwë and -(n)dil “-friend”.

Reference ✧ UT/210

Elements

Manwë “Blessed Being”
-(n)dil “-friend, -lover; devotion, disinterested love”
