ᴺQ. !elengolmë, n. “astronomy” [created by NQ-Wiki] (Category: Star)

A neologism for “astronomy” appearing in the Neo-Quenya Wiki, a combination of Q. elen “star” and Q. n(g)olmë “lore”.

Elements

Q. elen “star”
Q. nolmë “knowledge, philosophy (including science), department of wisdom (science, etc.)”
