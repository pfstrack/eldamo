√YOD root. “fence, enclose”

√YOD root. “fence, enclose”
This root appears in a discussion of the possible origin of Sindarin -ion² “-land”, blended with the root √YON² “wide, extensive” on PE17/43. A more common variation of the latter root was YAN¹, making the status of √YOD uncertain. The derivatives of √YOD are similar to those of the much better attested √PEL, and the √PEL-derivatives should probably be preferred.
