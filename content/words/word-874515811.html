Q. Axantur m. “*Master of Law”

Third child of Nolondil and ancestor of Hallacar, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. His name seems to be a compound of axan “law” and -tur “master”, so perhaps: “Master of Law”.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0406

Elements

axan “law, rule, commandment”
-tur “master, lord, ruler, victor”
