ᴺQ. !cacátu-, v. “to design, shape or fashion slowly” [created by Sami Paldanius, 1000W] (Category: Form, Shape)

A neologism for “to design” coined by Sami Paldanius in the 1000 Words project (1000W), patterned after fifíru- “slowly fade away”, hence with the original meaning “shape or fashion slowly”.
