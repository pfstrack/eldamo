ᴺQ. !ciryapanda n. “haven, (lit.) ship-enclosure” (Category: Port, Harbor)

⚠️ᴺQ. !ciryapanda, n. “haven, (lit.) ship-enclosure” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Port, Harbor)
Q. londë “(land-locked) haven; ⚠️[ᴹQ.] road (in sea), fairway, entrance to harbour; gulf”

A neologism for “haven” coined by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, a combination of cirya “ship” and [ᴹQ.] panda “enclosure”, inspired by N. cirban “haven”. I would just used attested Q. londë “haven” instead.

Elements

Q. cirya¹ “(sharp-prowed) ship; swift gliding”
ᴹQ. panda “enclosure”
