ᴱQ. Inwilis (Inwiliss-) pn. “Faëry”

⚠️ᴱQ. Inwilis (Inwiliss-), pn. “Faëry”

A name of the land of “Fäery” in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/42), derived from the name of its people the Inwir.
