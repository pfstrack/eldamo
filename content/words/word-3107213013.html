Ad. azra-zâin n. “sea-lands, maritime regions” (Category: Sea)

Ad. azra-zâin n. “sea-lands, maritime regions” (Category: Sea)
A noun given as an example of a genitive compound (SD/429), translated “sea-lands, maritime regions” (SD/435) but literally meaning “*lands of the sea”.
