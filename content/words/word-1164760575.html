ᴱQ. †Ô pn. “The Sea, Ocean”

⚠️ᴱQ. †Ô, pn. “The Sea, Ocean”

A name for “The Sea” in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/85), marked archaic in the Qenya Lexicon where it was give as a derivative of the root ᴱ√’O’O (QL/70).
