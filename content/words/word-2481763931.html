ᴺQ. !emerro n. “shepherd, herdsman” (Category: Herdsman)

ᴺQ. !emerro, n. “shepherd, herdsman” [created by Damien Bador] (Category: Herdsman)
ᴱQ. mavar(do) “shepherd”
ᴱQ. moar(do) “shepherd”

A neologism coined by Damien Bador, the male equivalent of Q. emerwen “shepherdess”.

Elements

Q. #emer- “?to herd (sheep)”

      ᴱQ. lamandar n. “herdsman, shepherd” (Category: Herdsman)

      A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “herdsman”, based on ᴱQ. lama “head of cattle or sheep” (QL/50). It also appeared in the Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa with the gloss “(shep)herd”.
