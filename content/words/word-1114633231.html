ᴺQ. !relyávalda n. “fig-tree” (Category: Fig)

ᴺQ. !relyávalda, n. “fig-tree” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Fig)

A neologism for “fig-tree” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴺQ.] relyávë “fig” and alda “tree”.

Elements

!relyávë “fig”
Q. alda “tree”
