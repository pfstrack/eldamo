      N. Lhuien loc.

      Noldorin equivalent of ᴹQ. Lórien appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/LOS). It perhaps developed from a primitive form *✶Lōsien, where the intervocalic [s] became [h] and then this non-initial [h] vanished, as opposed to Quenya where the [s] became [r].

      See S. Lórien for further discussion.
