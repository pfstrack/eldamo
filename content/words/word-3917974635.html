ᴺS. !odh(o)r n. “parent (gender neutral)” (Category: Parent)

ᴺS. !odh(o)r, n. “parent (gender neutral)” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Parent)

A neologism for a gender-neutral term for “parent” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-06-03 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), derived from primitive ᴹ✶onrō. Luinyelle suggested it would be used mostly in its plural form edhyr “parents” referring to both a mother and father (or other pair of parents).
