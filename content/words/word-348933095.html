N. Eges-hirion loc. “Mouths of Sirion”

N. Eges-hirion, loc. “Mouths of Sirion”

Name for the mouth of the Sirion (LR/407), a combination of eges “mouth(s)” (attested only in this name) with the lenited form of the river’s name: hirion.
