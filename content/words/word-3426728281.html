ᴹQ. enyasse adv. “there far away, far away over there (out of sight)” (Category: Pronoun)

⚠️ᴹQ. enyassë, adv. “there far away, far away over there (out of sight)” (Category: Pronoun)
ᴹQ. entas(së) “away over there”

A correlative appearing in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/97, 111), a combination of ᴹQ. enya “that far away” and the locative suffix ᴹQ. -sse.
