Q. Ulumúri pn.

Q. Ulumúri, pn.

Name of the horns of Ulmo (S/27). The meaning of the name is unclear, but its initial element is probably related to the name of Ulmo.

References ✧ MR/202; MRI/Falarombar, Ulumúri; SI

      Q. Falarombar pn.

      A rejected name for the horns of Ulmo (MR/202). Its initial element seems to be derived from the root √PHAL “foam, splash”, with its second element the plural of the word romba “horn, trumpet”. It was replaced by Ulumúri, the name that appeared in the published version of The Silmarillion.
