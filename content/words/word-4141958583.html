S. Duin Dhaer loc.

S. Duin Dhaer, loc.

Another name for the river Gelion, appearing as both lenited and unlenited forms Duin Dhaer and Duin Daer (WJ/191, 336). This name is a compound of duin “river” and the adjective daer¹ “great”.
