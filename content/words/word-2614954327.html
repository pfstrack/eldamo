ᴱQ. Salkinóre loc. “Africa, *(lit.) Grass Land”

⚠️ᴱQ. Salcinórë, loc. “Africa, *(lit.) Grass Land”

Qenya name for Africa in the Qenya Lexicon and Poetic and Mythological Words of Eldarissa from the 1910s (QL/84; PME/84), a combination of salke (salki-) “grass” with nóre “land”.
