ᴺQ. !helmunquë n. “wrinkle, (lit.) skin-hollow” (Category: Skin, Hide)

ᴺQ. !helmunquë, n. “wrinkle, (lit.) skin-hollow” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Skin, Hide)

A neologism for “wrinkle” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴹQ.] helma “skin” and unquë “hollow”.

Elements

ᴹQ. helma “skin, fell”
Q. unquë “a hollow”
