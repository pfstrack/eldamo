ᴱQ. karpale n. “a plucking, tugging, wrenching; deceiving, tricking” (Category: Naked, Bare)

⚠️ᴱQ. carpalë, n. “a plucking, tugging, wrenching; deceiving, tricking” (Category: Naked, Bare)

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√KᴬRPᴬR [KṚPṚ], with glosses “a plucking, tugging, wrenching — deceiving, tricking” (QL/45).
