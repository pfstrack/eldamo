Q. tultaima adj. “*fetchable, summonable” (Category: to Call, Summon)

Q. tultaima, adj. “*fetchable, summonable” (Category: to Call, Summon)

An untranslated adjective in notes from 1969, a combination of tulta- “send for, summon, fetch” and the suffix -ima “-able” (PE22/156), so “*fetchable, summonable”.

Reference ✧ PE22/156

Elements

tulta- “to send (from point of view of receiver)” ✧ PE22/156 (tultā-)
-ima “-able, possibility” ✧ PE22/156
