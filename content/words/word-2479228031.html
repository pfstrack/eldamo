Q. Nerwen, f. “Man-maiden”

Q. Nerwen, f. “Man-maiden”

The mother-name of Galadriel (UT/229, PM/357), also appearing in the longer form Nerwendë (PM/346). This name is a compound of nér “man” and the suffixal form -wen of wendë “maiden”.
