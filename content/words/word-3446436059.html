S. Golodhbaeth pn. “*Noldor-speech”

S. Golodhbaeth, pn. “*Noldor-speech”

A Sindarin term for the speech of the Noldor, a combination of the Sindarin name of that tribe, the Golodh, with the lenited form of paeth “speech” (PE17/126).
