ᴺS. !torchal n. “shark or other large predatory fish” (Category: Fish)

ᴺS. !torchal, n. “shark or other large predatory fish” [created by Gábor Lőrinczi] (Category: Fish)

A neologism for a “shark or other large predatory fish” coined by Gábor Lőrinczi appearing in VinQuettaParma Wiki (VQP), a combination of N. taur “might” and N. hâl “fish” where the au became o in this polysyllable.

Elements

N. taur² “vast, mighty, overwhelming, awful, huge; high, sublime”
N. hâl “fish”
