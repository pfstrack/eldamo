ᴺQ. !hlarumir (hlarumír-) n. “(pair of) earrings” (Category: to Swim)

ᴺQ. !hlarumir (hlarumír-), n. “(pair of) earrings” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Swim)

A neologism for a “pair of earrings” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-10-06 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of dual hlaru “pair of ears” of hlas “ear” and mírë “jewel”. To refer to a single earring, one would say min hlarumíro “one of a pair of earrings”.

Elements

Q. hlas “ear” dual
Q. mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
