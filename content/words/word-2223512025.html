ᴺQ. !ilquaurë adv. “all the day” (Category: Whole)

ᴺQ. !ilquaurë, adv. “all the day” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Whole)
See ᴹQ. ilq(an)ar for discussion.

Elements

ᴹQ. ilqa “all the, the whole (situation); everything, all”
Q. aurë “day (as opposed to night), daylight, sunlight, morning”

      ᴹQ. ilq(an)ar adv. “all the day” (Category: Whole)

      A correlative combination in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/110), a combination of ᴹQ. ilqa “all the” and ᴹQ. -ar(yas) “day”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. ilquaurë “all the day”, since the later word for “day” is aurë.
