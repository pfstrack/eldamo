ᴺQ. !raiquë interj. “damn! (lit.) rage!” (Category: Various Cries)

ᴺQ. !raiquë, interj. “damn! (lit.) rage!” [created by Delle] (Category: Various Cries)

A word for “damn!” (as an interjection) suggested by Luinyelle and posted on 2024-08-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), inspired by the (untranslated) phrase Raiqe! Telkume kiryandon nolo. She suggested the initial word may be related to raiqa “angry”, which would mean raiqe is more literally “*rage!”.
