S. #hithui adj. “misty” (Category: Mist, Fog, Haze)

S. #hithui, adj. “misty” (Category: Mist, Fog, Haze)
An adjective form of hîth “mist”, constructed via the common adjective suffix -ui. It is translated in the early name N. Eredhithui “Misty Mountains” (TI/124), a precursor of S. Hithaeglir. It is attested in later writings as S. Hithui, the Sindarin name for November (lit. “*Misty-one”).

hîth “mist”
