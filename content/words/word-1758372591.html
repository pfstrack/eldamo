ᴱQ. tompo-tompo n. “noise of drums (or guns)” (Category: Noise, Thunder)

An onomatopoeic word for the “noise of drums (or guns)” in the Qenya Lexicon from the early root ᴱ√TOMPO (QL/94).

Neo-Quenya: I think this word might be salvaged in Neo-Quenya as a derivative of the later root √TOM for “resonant sounds” (PE17/138).
