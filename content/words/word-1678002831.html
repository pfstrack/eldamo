N. Maglavorn pn. “Black-sword”

⚠️N. Maglavorn, pn. “Black-sword”

A rejected variant name for Mormegil, a combination of magol “sword” and the lenited form of morn “black” (Ety/MAK, MOR; EtyAC/MAK, MOR).
