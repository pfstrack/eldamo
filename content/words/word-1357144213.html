Q. Hrávandil m. “*Wild Beast Friend”

Q. Hrávandil, m. “*Wild Beast Friend”

A Quenya name for Radagast appearing in notes from 1959 (NM/95), apparently a combination of Q. hravan “wild beast” and Q. -(n)dil “friend” as suggested by Carl Hostetter (NM/102 note #7).

Reference ✧ NM/95 ✧ “Radagast”

Elements

hravan “wild beast”
-(n)dil “-friend, -lover; devotion, disinterested love”
