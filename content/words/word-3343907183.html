Q. Lassemista pn. “Leaf-Grey”

Q. Lassemista, pn. “Leaf-Grey”
The name of a tree in Fangorn beloved of the Ent Bregalad (LotR/483). It is a compound of lassë “leaf” and mista “grey” (PE17/83).
