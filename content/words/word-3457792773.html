⚠️ᴺQ. !narassë, n. “pasture” [created by Boris Shapiro] (Category: Pasture)
ᴺQ. nesselë “pasture, mead[ow]; pasturage”

A neologism for “pasture” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, based on N. nadhras of the same meaning. I prefer instead to retain the early noun ᴱQ. nessele “pasture, mead[ow]; pasturage” for this purpose.

Cognates

  • N. nadhras “pasture”
