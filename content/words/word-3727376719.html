ᴺQ. !calta n. “picture, photography” (Category: Painting, Picture)

⚠️ᴺQ. !calta, n. “picture, photography” [created by ABNW] (Category: Painting, Picture)
Q. emma “picture, *image”
Q. #calta- “to kindle, [ᴹQ.] (cause to) shine, light up, [ᴱQ.] set light to”

A neologism for “picture, photography” appearing in Ales Bican’s New Words (ABNW) from the early 2000s, a blending of √KAT “shape” and √KAL “light”. I think it is better to use attested emma “*picture, image” which was published after the neologism was coined.
