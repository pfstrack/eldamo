ᴺQ. !levë n. “movement, motion” (Category: to Move)

ᴺQ. !levë, n. “movement, motion” [created by NQ-Wiki] (Category: to Move)

A neologism appearing in the Neo-Quenya Wiki based on [ᴱQ.] lev- “move”. Tamas Ferencz instead suggested menesta for “motion, movement” based on √MEN “go, move”, but I use Q. men- only for the sense “go = move (in a direction)”, not movement in general.
