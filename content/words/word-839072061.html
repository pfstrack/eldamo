ᴺS. !hardúnen adj. “southwestern” (Category: South)

ᴺS. !hardúnen, adj. “southwestern” [created by Röandil] (Category: South)

A neologism coined by Röandil in 2023-09-28 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of harn “south” with [ᴺS.] dúnen “western”.

Elements

S. harn “south, southern”
!dúnen “western”
