S. Lindis, f.

Wife of Dior (WJ/257). Her name appears to be a combination of lind¹ “song” and dîs “bride”.

Elements

lind¹ “song, chant, singing, air, tune; singer”
dîr¹ “man, adult male; agental suffix”
