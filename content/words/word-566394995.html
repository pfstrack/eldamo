ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] aurëa adj. “sunny, sunlit, (lit.) daylight-ish”

ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] aurëa, adj. “sunny, sunlit, (lit.) daylight-ish”

Related

  • aurëa “sunny, sunlit, (lit.) daylight-ish” implies both “warm and bright” versus simply “bright” as with Q. calina “light, bright, sunny, (lit.) illumined”

Elements

Q. aurë “day (as opposed to night), daylight, sunlight”
Q. #-a “adjectival suffix”
