ᴺQ. !parmasan (parmasamb-) [þ], n. “library [room]” [created by Ellanto] (Category: Literature)

A neologism for “library” coined by Ellanto posted on 2024-05-03 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of parma “book” and [ᴹQ.] sambe.

Elements

Q. parma “book, writing, composition, written document of some size”
ᴹQ. sambe “room, chamber”
