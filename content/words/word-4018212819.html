ᴱN. dathanc adj. “unsteady” (Category: to Fall)

⚠️ᴱN. dathanc, adj. “unsteady” (Category: to Fall)

An adjective for “unsteady” in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s, a combination of ᴱN. tanc “steady” with the negative prefix for adjectives ᴱN. dan- (PE13/141).
