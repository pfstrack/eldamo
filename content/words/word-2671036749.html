N. Ar Vanwe pn. “Day of Manwe, first day of the Valian week”

N. Ar Vanwe, pn. “Day of Manwe, first day of the Valian week”

The Noldorin name for the 1st day of the Valian 5-day week, a combination of the prefix ar-² “day” and the lenited form of Manwe (Ety/AR¹, LEP).
