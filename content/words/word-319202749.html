ᴺQ. !sovamorco n. “raccoon, (lit.) wash-bear” (Category: Animals (other))

ᴺQ. !sovamorco, n. “raccoon, (lit.) wash-bear” [created by Dírheron] (Category: Animals (other))

A neologism for “raccoon” coined by Dírheron on Discord in 2019, a combination of [ᴱQ.] sovo- “wash” and [ᴹQ.] morko “bear”, inspired by German “Waschbär”.

Elements

sov- “to wash, *bathe”
ᴹQ. morko “bear”
