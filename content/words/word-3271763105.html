ᴺQ. !rondarya-, v. “to solidify, become solid” [created by Αγωνιζῆς] (Category: Hard)

A neologism for “to solidify” coined by Αγωνιζῆς posted on 2025-01-30 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), an inceptive verb based on ronda “solid”, so actually “become solid”.
