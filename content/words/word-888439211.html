ᴱQ. †[ɣ]

⚠️ᴱQ. †[ɣ]

References ✧ PE12/16, 18, 20

    Phonetic Development

    ᴱQ. voiced stops became spirants after vowels Vɣ < Vg ✧ PE12/16 (g > -; medial)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids rɣ < rx ✧ PE12/18 (liquids + χ > rg)
    ᴱQ. voiceless spirants voiced after liquids lɣ < lx ✧ PE12/18 (liquids + χ > lg)
    ᴱQ. final voiceless spirants were weakened and voiced -ɣ < -x ✧ PE12/20 (h > ʒ)
    ᴱQ. intervocalic voiceless spirants became voiced VɣV < VxV ✧ PE12/16 (x > -; medial); PE12/18 (χ/h > ɣ)
    ᴱQ. between vowels and consonants [ɣ] vanished with compensatory lengthening VɣC > V̄øC ✧ PE12/18 (ʒ > ø)
    ᴱQ. final [ɣ] vanished with compensatory lengthening -Vɣ > -V̄ø ✧ PE12/20 (ʒ > -)
    ᴱQ. [ɣ] otherwise vanished ɣ > ø ✧ PE12/18 (ɣ > ø)
