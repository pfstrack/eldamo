Ilk. **Dilion loc.

Ilk. **Dilion loc.

A hypothetical name for the river N. Gelion, what the Ilkorin name would have been if it had the same origin as the Noldorin misinterpretion of the river’s actual Ilkorin name, Gelion (Ety/GYEL). It is an illustration of the rule that initial palatalized velars became dentals in Ilkorin.
