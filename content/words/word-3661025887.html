ᴱQ. (a)kainusta n. “wedding, marriage” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)

⚠️ᴱQ. (a)cainusta, n. “wedding, marriage” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)
ᴹQ. vestalë “wedding”
ᴹQ. vesta¹ “matrimony, [ᴱQ.] state of marriage”

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “wedding, marriage”, a noun form of the verb ᴱQ. (a)kainu- “to wed” (QL/46).
