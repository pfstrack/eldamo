ᴺQ. !tarolwen n. “sceptre, (lit.) royal branch” (Category: Adornment (other))

ᴺQ. !tarolwen, n. “sceptre, (lit.) royal branch” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Adornment (other))

A neologism coined by Tamas Ferencz as a combination of tar- “high, *royal” and [ᴱQ.] olwen “branch”.

Elements

Q. tar-¹ “high”
ᴱQ. olwe(n) “branch, wand, stick”
