ᴺQ. !andalango n. “giraffe, (lit.) long-neck” (Category: Animals (other))

ᴺQ. !andalango, n. “giraffe, (lit.) long-neck” [created by Dírheron] (Category: Animals (other))

A neologism for “giraffe” coined by Dírheron on Discord in 2019, a combination of anda “long” and lango “neck”.

Elements

Q. anda “long, far”
Q. lango “neck”
