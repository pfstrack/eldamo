Ad. [u]

A vowel that survived from Primitive Adûnaic into Classical Adûnaic (SD/422). It also arose in some circumstances from the consonant [w] (SD/419). Where circumstances or grammatical placed another vowel after [u], they were separated by a glide-consonant “w” (SD/424), sometimes spelled “v” (SD/434).
