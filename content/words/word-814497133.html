S. lissuin n. “a fragrant flower” (Category: Flower (other))

S. lissuin, n. “a fragrant flower” (Category: Flower (other))

Name of a sweet-smelling flower of Númenor (UT/189). Its initial element could be liss “?fragrant, sweet”, an otherwise unattested cognate to Q. lissë “sweet”.
