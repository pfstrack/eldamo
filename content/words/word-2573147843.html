ᴺQ. !(y)otulma, n. “coincidence, concurrence, simultaneity, synchronicity” [created by Röandil] (Category: to Happen)

A neologism coined by Röandil in 2023-08-29 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of Q. yo- or Q. o- “together” with ᴹQ. tulma “event”.

Elements

Q. #yo- “together (used in words describing the union of three or more things)”
ᴹQ. tulma “event”
