Q. rámannerte n. “ship-ennead” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

Q. rámannertë, n. “ship-ennead” (Category: Gem, Jewel)
Elendil’s fleet of the nine ships, a combination of ráman “great ship” and nertë “nine”, translated “ship-ennead”, where “ennead” = “group of nine”, originally from Greek.
