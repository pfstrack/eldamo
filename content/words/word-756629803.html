✶Ad. voiced-weak-continuants

✶Ad. voiced-weak-continuants
Tolkien used this category as a grabbag for various voiced continuants in Primitive Adûnaic. Most survived into Classical Adûnaic, but the velars [ɣ] and [ʔ] vanished.
