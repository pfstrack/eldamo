ᴺQ. !hondolauca adj. “warm-hearted” (Category: Heart)

ᴺQ. !hondolauca, adj. “warm-hearted” [created by Vyacheslav Stepanov] (Category: Heart)

A neologism for “warm-hearted” coined by Vyacheslav Stepanov posted on 2024-10-20 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of hondo “heart” and lauca “warm”; compare hondoringa “cold-hearted”.
