ᴹQ. qáqenna adv. “*to everywhere” (Category: All, Every)

ᴹQ. quáquenna, adv. “*to everywhere” (Category: All, Every)

The correlative ᴹQ. qáqenna appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112), a combination of ᴹQ. qa(qe)- “all” and the allative suffix ᴹQ. -nna.

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. ilinna “to everywhere” using il- “every”.

Reference ✧ PE23/112 ✧ “*to everywhere”

Elements

qa(qe)- “all, all the (with plurals), every; each, every”
-nna “to, towards; allative suffix” ✧ PE23/112
qáqe “all, everyone, every person/thing” allative ✧ PE23/112 (qáqenna)
