Q. Lungumá m. “Heavyhand”

The Quenya name of S. Mablung, a compound of lungu “heavy” and má “hand” (VT47/19). It also appeared in the form Lungumaqua where its second element was the direct equivalent of S. mâb “hand”, but elsewhere (VT47/7) Q. maqua was used as an alternate word for “five” (lit. “hand-full”), much as English “dozen” is an alternate word for “twelve”.
