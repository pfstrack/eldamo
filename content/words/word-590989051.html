ᴺQ. !fanorcarnë n. “sardonyx” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !fanorcarnë, n. “sardonyx” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “sardonyx” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), its second element appears to be carnë “red” but it is not clear what the first element is intended to be.
