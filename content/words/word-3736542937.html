ᴱQ. qamba adj. “unwell, sick” (Category: Sick; Sickness)

⚠️ᴱQ. quamba, adj. “unwell, sick” (Category: Sick; Sickness)
ᴺQ. quámëa “sick, *nauseous”

An adjective in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “unwell, sick” derived from the early root ᴱ√QAMA (QL/76).
