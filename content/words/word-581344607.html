Q. Arafinwë m. “*Noble Finwë”

Q. Arafinwë, m. “*Noble Finwë”

Father-name of Finarfin (PM/344) from which his Sindarin name was derived. It is a compound ar(a)- “high, noble” and the name of his father, Finwë.
