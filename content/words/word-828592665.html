ᴱQ. Laurundo m.

Qenya cognate of Glorund in the earliest Lost Tales (LT2/84). Its initial element is clearly laure “gold”. Its second element may be a cognate of G. gunn, which was glossed “dragon” in an Early Noldorin word list (PE13/144).
