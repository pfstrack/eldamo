S. Berennyn loc. “Brown Lands”

Sindarin name of the “Brown Lands”, appearing in Tolkien’s “Unfinished Index” of The Lord of the Rings (RC/343). The initial element is clearly the plural of baran “brown”, but the meaning of second element is unclear. It might be a plural form -yn of the suffix -(i)on “-land” seen in names like Eregion and Nanduhirion.
