Q. Cirion’s Oath

Q. Cirion’s Oath

Cirion’s oath to Eorl that bound Gondor and Rohan as allies in the later half of the third age. It is one of the longer prose texts in Quenya.

Reference ✧ UT/305

Elements

vanda sina termaruva Elenna·nóreo alcar enyalien “This oath shall stand in memory of the glory of the Land of the Star” ✧ UT/305.14005
ar Elendil Vorondo voronwë “and of the faith of Elendil the Faithful” ✧ UT/305.14075
nai tiruvantes i hárar mahalmassen mi Númen “in the keeping of those who sit upon thrones of the West” ✧ UT/305.15025
