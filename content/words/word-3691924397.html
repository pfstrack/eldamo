ᴱQ. Orodáni coll. “East Danes”

⚠️ᴱQ. Orodáni, coll. “East Danes”

Qenya name for the East Danes (LT2/306), a combination of ᴱQ. Dani “Danes” with a variant of ᴱQ. óre “East”.

Reference ✧ LT2/306 ✧ OroDáni “East Danes”
