N. Inias Veleriand pn. “Annals of Beleriand”

N. Inias Veleriand, pn. “Annals of Beleriand”

Noldorin title for the “Annals of Beleriand”, a combination of ínias “annals” and the lenited form of Beleriand (LR/202).

See S. Inias Beleriand for further discussion.
