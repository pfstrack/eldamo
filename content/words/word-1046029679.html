ᴺS. !tŷg n. “thigh” (Category: Leg)

ᴺS. !tŷg, n. “thigh” (Category: Leg)

A neologism for “thigh” coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo, the Sindarin equivalent of [ᴹQ.] tiuko “thigh” from the root ᴹ√TIW “fat, thick”.
