ᴺQ. !runyaril (runyarill-) n. “garnet, (lit.) fiery red radiance” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !runyaril (runyarill-), n. “garnet, (lit.) fiery red radiance” [created by Valerie] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “garnet” coined by Valerie in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of runya “fiery red” and [ᴹQ.] rille “brilliance”.

Elements

Q. runya “fiery red”
ᴹQ. ?rille “brilliance”
