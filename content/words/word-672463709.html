ᴺQ. !tanaurë adv. “on that day” (Category: That)

ᴺQ. !tanaurë, adv. “on that day” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: That)
See ᴹQ. tanar(yas) for discussion.

Elements

Q. ta¹ “that, there”
Q. aurë “day (as opposed to night), daylight, sunlight, morning”

Element In

  • ᴺQ. !tanaurëa “that old, that long lasting, (lit.) of that many days”

      ᴹQ. tanar(yas) adv. “*on that (past) day” (Category: That)

      The correlatives ᴹQ. tanar and tanaryas appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/109), a combination of ᴹQ. ta “that” and ᴹQ. -ar(yas) “day”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. tanaurë “on that day” using Q. aurë for “day”.
