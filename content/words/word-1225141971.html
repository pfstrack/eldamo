Ad. homorganic-series

Ad. homorganic-series

Like his other languages, Adûnaic phonemes can be organized into groups of homorganic series based on their place of articulation within the mouth (SD/418): On SD/416, Tolkien called these the labials (p-series), dentals (t-series) and velars (k-series).
