ᴺQ. !noremma n. “map” (Category: Painting, Picture)

ᴺQ. !noremma, n. “map” [created by Shihali] (Category: Painting, Picture)

A neologism for “map” suggested by Shihali in 2018 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of nórë “land” and emma “picture”. The Neo-Quenya Wiki had ᴺQ. nórihalmë “map” instead, where the second element was [ᴱQ.] halme “drawing”, but that is the wrong kind of “drawing”, meaning “drawing (pulling) wood”, not “a drawing of a picture”.

Elements

Q. nórë “land, country; †people, race, tribe”
Q. emma “*picture”
