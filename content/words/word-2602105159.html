Ilk. [w]

The semi-vowel [w] mostly survived from Primitive Elvish into Ilkorin, though initial [w] became [gw] (PE19/19, 24). The primitive semi-vowel [j] (“y”) disappeared, either vanishing or become the vowel [i].
