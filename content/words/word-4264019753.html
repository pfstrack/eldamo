Q. Ancalimë f. “*Brightest (f.)”

Q. Ancalimë, f. “*Brightest (f.)”
Tar-Ancalimë was the seventh ruler and first ruling queen of Númenor (UT/220). Her name seems to be a feminized form of ancalima “brightest”, the intensive form of calima “bright”.
