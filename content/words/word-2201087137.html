ᴺQ. !nendalaurë n. “golden water-flower, yellow iris” (Category: Flower (other))

ᴺQ. !nendalaurë, n. “golden water-flower, yellow iris” [created by Vyacheslav Stepanov] (Category: Flower (other))

A neologism for “golden water-flower, yellow iris” by Vyacheslav Stepanov posted on 2022-05-01 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of nenda “wet” and laurë “gold (colour)”, analogous to S. ninglor.

Elements

Q. nenda “wet”
Q. laurë “gold (light or colour)”

Cognates
