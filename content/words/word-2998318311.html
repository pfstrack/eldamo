ᴱQ. kaime n. “dwelling, home” (Category: Home)

⚠️ᴱQ. caimë, n. “dwelling, home” (Category: Home)
Q. már “home, dwelling, habitation; ⚠️[ᴹQ.] house; earth”

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “dwelling, home” under the early root ᴱ√KAYA “lie, rest; dwell” (QL/46).
