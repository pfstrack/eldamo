ᴱQ. Wingilla f.

⚠️ᴱQ. Wingilla, f.

Another name for Ónen in an early name list (PE14/14), perhaps a variation of wingil “sea nymph”, as suggested by the editors (Wynne, Gilson, Hostetter, Welden, Smith; footnote #20).

Reference ✧ PE14/14

wingil “(sea) nymph; *(lit.) foam-maiden”
