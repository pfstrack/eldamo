N. Bair Nestad loc. “Houses of Healing”

Noldorin name for the Houses of Healing appearing only in the Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s along with several variations (WR/379-380). It is a combination of the plural of bâr “home” and nestad “healing”.

Conceptual Development: An earlier, rejected form of this name was Edeb na Nestad (WR/380).

      N. Edeb na Nestad loc. “Houses of Healing”

      A rejected Noldorin name for the Houses of Healing (WR/380). It is a combination of the plural of adab “house”, the preposition na “of” and the noun nestad “healing”.

      See N. Bair Nestad for further discussion.
