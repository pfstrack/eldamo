ᴺQ. !ailindo n. “loon [bird], (lit.) lake-singer” (Category: Bird (other))

ᴺQ. !ailindo, n. “loon [bird], (lit.) lake-singer” [created by Valerie] (Category: Bird (other))

A neologism coined by Valerie posted on 2024-08-14 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of ailin “lake” and [ᴹQ.] lindo “singer”.

Elements

Q. ailin “a large lake”
ᴹQ. lindo “singer, singing bird”
