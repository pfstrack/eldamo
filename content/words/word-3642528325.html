G. tarog n. “ox” (Category: Ox, Steer)

⚠️G. tarog, n. “ox” (Category: Ox, Steer)
S. mund “bull”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s for an “ox”, cognate to ᴱQ. taruku- of the same meaning, clearly based on G. târ “horn” (GL/69).
