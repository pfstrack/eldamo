ᴹQ. otorno, n. “sworn brother, associate [m.]” (Category: Brother)

A noun in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “sworn brother, associate”, a combination of ᴹQ. toron “brother” with the prefix o- “together” (Ety/TOR, WŌ).
