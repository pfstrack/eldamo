N. Alflon loc. “*Swan-haven”

N. Alflon, loc. “*Swan-haven”

A Noldorin name for ᴹQ. Alqalonde appearing on the earliest Silmarillion maps (SM/250-1, 261). It appears to be a combination of alf “swan” and the lenited form of lhonn “haven”.
