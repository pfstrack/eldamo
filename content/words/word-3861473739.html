ᴺQ. !nehtanna n. “melliferous flower, any plant which supplies bees with a lot of nectar” (Category: Tree (other))

ᴺQ. !nehtanna, n. “melliferous flower, any plant which supplies bees with a lot of nectar” [created by Luinyelle, Fëastorno] (Category: Tree (other))

A neologism coined by Luinyelle and Fëastorno, posted on 2025-03-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of nehtë “honey” and anna “gift”.

Elements

Q. nehtë² “honey”
Q. anna “gift, (orig.) thing handed, brought or sent to a person”
