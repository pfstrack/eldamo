Q. Singimóre pn.

⚠️Q. Singimórë, pn.

Three variant names in a rejected paragraph in the second version of Tengwesta Qenderinwa (TQ2), illustrating how a medial ñ [ŋ] sound might be written in Quenya: g, ñ, or ng (PE18/107 note #163). Tolkien seems to have abandoned the idea almost immediately.
