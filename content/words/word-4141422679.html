ᴺQ. !novárilë n. “forenoon” (Category: Noon)

ᴺQ. !novárilë, n. “forenoon” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Noon)

A neologism for “forenoon” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of adjectival nöa “before” and an abstraction of árë “*noontide”.

Related

  • !novárilë “forenoon” is inspired by !apárilë “afternoon”

Elements

Q. nöa “former; yesterday; tomorrow”
Q. árë “sunlight, warmth (especially of the sun); day”
