S. Haudh-en-Arwen loc. “Ladybarrow”

S. Haudh-en-Arwen, loc. “Ladybarrow”
Grave of Lady Haleth translated “Ladybarrow” (S/147), a combination of haudh “mound”, en¹ “of the” and Arwen “Noble Maiden”, the last used as a title rather than a name.
