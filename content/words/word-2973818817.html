S. Ivonwin coll. “Maidens of Yavanna”

S. Ivonwin, coll. “Maidens of Yavanna”

Sindarin term for Elf-women in service to Yavanna (PM/404), a combination of her Sindarin name Ivann and the lenited plural form of gwend “maiden”.
