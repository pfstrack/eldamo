ᴺQ. !sardanar (sardanár-) n. “corundum, (lit.) hard sun” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !sardanar (sardanár-), n. “corundum, (lit.) hard sun” [created by Valerie] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “corundum” coined by Valerie in 2022-05-12 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of sarda “hard” and Anar “sun”.

Elements

Q. sarda “hard (*as stone)”
Q. Anar “Sun”
