Element In

    Phonetic Development

    Q. nasals and liquids often became syllabic after a consonant Car < Cr ✧ PE19/83 (r > ṛ > ar; after stops or aspirates); PE19/86 (pr > par); PE19/86 (tr > tar); PE19/86 (kr > kar); PE19/98 (mr > mar); PE19/98 (n-r > nar); PE19/102 (z-r > zar)
    AQ. syllabic nasals developed a preceding vowel of similar quality and- < ṇd- ✧ PE17/124 (ṇ > an)
