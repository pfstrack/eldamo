ᴱQ. Fairinor loc. “Land of Release”

⚠️ᴱQ. Fairinor, loc. “Land of Release”
Qenya cognate of G. Dor Faidwen in an early name list (PE15/7), likely a combination of faire (fairi-) “free” and nóre “land”.
