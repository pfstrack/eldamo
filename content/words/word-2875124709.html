ᴺQ. !illangiéla n. “nevertheless, (lit.) all-having-been-bypassed” (Category: Conjunction)

ᴺQ. !illangiéla, n. “nevertheless, (lit.) all-having-been-bypassed” [created by Arael, NotD] (Category: Conjunction)

A neologism for “nevertheless” by Arael posted on 2023-05-08 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of il- “all” and the past active participle of a verb *langa- “to (by)pass” based on langa “thing that crosses”.

Elements

Q. #il- “every, *all”
Q. langa “thing that crosses, ferry, ford, crossway, bridge, cross-bar”
