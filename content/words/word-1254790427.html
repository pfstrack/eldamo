Q. Ancalimon m. “*Brightest (m.)”

Q. Ancalimon, m. “*Brightest (m.)”
Tar-Ancalimon was the fourteenth ruler of Númenor (UT/221). His names seems to be a masculinized form of ancalima “brightest”, the intensive form of calima “bright”.
