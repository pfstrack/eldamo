ᴺQ. !etehat- v. “to throw out” (Category: to Leave, Relinquish)

ᴺQ. !etehat-, v. “to throw out” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: to Leave, Relinquish)

A neologism coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of et(e)- “out” and [ᴱQ.] hat- “hurl”.

Elements

Q. #et(e)- “*out”
hat- “[ᴱQ.] to hurl, fling, *throw”
