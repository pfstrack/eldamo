ᴱQ. Virtinoldor coll. “thrall-noldoli”

⚠️ᴱQ. Virtinoldor, coll. “thrall-noldoli”

Name for the enslaved Noldor in Tolkien’s “Creatures of the Earth” from the 1910s (PE14/9), a compound of virt “slave” and the plural of Noldo.

Reference ✧ PE14/9 ✧ virtinoldor “thrall-noldoli”

Elements

virt “servant, slave”
