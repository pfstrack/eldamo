ᴺQ. !ocaraitië n. “program, a set of activities” (Category: Action)

ᴺQ. !ocaraitië, n. “program, a set of activities” [created by Sami Paldanius, 1000W] (Category: Action)

A neologism for “program”, more exactly “a set of activities”, coined by Sami Paldanius in the 1000 Words project (1000W) as a combination of o- “together” and caraitië “activity”.

Elements

Q. o- “together”
Q. caraitië “activity, business”
