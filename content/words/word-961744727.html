ᴺQ. [ᴹQ.] ^riminna adv. “to many places” (Category: Much, Many)

ᴺQ. [ᴹQ.] ^riminna, adv. “to many places” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Much, Many)
See ᴹQ. hriminna for discussion.

Elements

^rim- “(very) many”
Q. -nna “(movement) to, towards, onto, at (arriving at a point); allative suffix”

      ᴹQ. hriminna adv. “*to many places” (Category: To, Towards)

      The correlative ᴹQ. hriminna appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112), a combination of ᴹQ. hrim- “many” and the allative suffix -nna.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would adapt this to ᴺQ. riminna “to many places” using rim- “many” to be more compatible with Tolkien’s later writings.
