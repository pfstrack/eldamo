?ᴱ√FUKU root. “*smoke, smoulder”

⚠️?ᴱ√FUKU root. “*smoke, smoulder”
√USUK “*reek, smoke; ⚠️dusk, evening”

A hypothetical early root to explain Gnomish words such as G. fug- “smoulder” and G. func “smoke” (GL/35). In Noldorin of the 1930s, it seems this root was replaced by ᴹ√USUK of similar meaning.
