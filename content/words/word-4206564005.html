ᴺQ. !AQ v. “P.S., postscriptum, abbreviation of an quetta” (Category: More)

ᴺQ. !AQ, v. “P.S., postscriptum, abbreviation of an quetta” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: More)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-05-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a Quenya abbreviation (pronounced á-quá) equivalent to “P.S. = postscriptum” for an addendum to a letter or document, and abbreviation of an quetta “a further word”.

Elements

Q. an “for, but, then, or; moreover, furthermore”
Q. quetta “word”
