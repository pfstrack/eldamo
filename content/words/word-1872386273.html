ᴱQ. ristar n. “sower” (Category: to Sow; Seed)

⚠️ᴱQ. ristar, n. “sower” (Category: to Sow; Seed)
ᴺQ. !rerindo “sower”

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “sower”, an agental form of ᴱQ. rista- “plant” (QL/80).
