S. Dor-en-Ernil loc. “Land of the Prince”

S. Dor-en-Ernil, loc. “Land of the Prince”

The Gondorian province ruled by the Prince of Dol Amroth, translated “Land of the Prince” (UT/245), a combination dôr “land”, en¹ “of the” and ernil “prince”.
