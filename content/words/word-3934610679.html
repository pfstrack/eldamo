      ᴱQ. pint (pimp-) n. “tail” (Category: Tail)

      A noun appearing as ᴱQ. pint (pimp-) “tail” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√PIPI “hang, trail” (QL/74).

      Neo-Quenya: Helge Fauskanger adopted this word as ᴺQ. pimpë “tail” in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), and I agree with this adaptation, since in later Quenya it was less likely final vowels were lost in disyllables. I would assume a stem form of pimpi- however.
