ᴱQ. Indorion m.

⚠️ᴱQ. Indorion, m.

A name of Earendel as a descendant of Indor, a combination of that name with the patronymic suffix -ion (LT2/217, LT2A/Indorion). It also appeared as Indorildo (LT2/217).
