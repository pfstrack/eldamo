ᴱ√KENYE root. “prick”

⚠️ᴱ√KENYE root. “prick”
√EK “(sharp) point, [ᴹ√] spear, thorn”

A root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “prick” (QL/46). There are no signs of this root in Tolkien’s later writing.
