ᴺQ. !hilistas, adv. “in turn” [created by Arael] (Category: to Follow)

A neologism for “in turn” coined by Arael posted on 2024-08-14 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), the short-locative form of ᴺQ. hilista “following (situation), succession, continuation”. It may also be used with possessive suffixes, such as hilistaryas “in his/her turn”.
