ᴺQ. !malmayul (malmayuld-) n. “lemonade, (lit.) lemon-drink” (Category: Fruit)

ᴺQ. !malmayul (malmayuld-), n. “lemonade, (lit.) lemon-drink” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Fruit)

A neologism for “lemonade” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-03-13 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of malma “lemon” and a reduction of yulda “drink”, so more literally “lemon-drink”.

Elements

!malma¹ “lemon”
Q. yulda¹ “drink, draught, thing drunk; cup”
