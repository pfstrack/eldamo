N. Gilbrennil f. “Varda”

N. Gilbrennil, f. “Varda”

A name for Varda appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/GIL), a combination of geil “star” and brennil “lady”. This name was probably replaced by Elbereth.
