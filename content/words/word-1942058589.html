ᴺQ. !cucuollë n. “turtledove” (Category: Bird (other))

ᴺQ. !cucuollë, n. “turtledove” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Bird (other))

A neologism for “turtledove” coined by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), an elaboration of ᴹQ. kukua “dove”.
