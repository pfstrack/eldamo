N. Sern Ubed loc. “[Stones of] Denial”

N. Sern Ubed, loc. “[Stones of] Denial”

Earlier name for the Argonath appearing in Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s (WR/132), a combination of the plural of sarn “stone” and ubed “denial”.
