ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] vaimata- v. “to [en]robe, *clothe; to get dressed, put on clothing” (Category: to Clothe, Dress)

ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] vaimata-, v. “to [en]robe, *clothe; to get dressed, put on clothing” (Category: to Clothe, Dress)
ᴺQ. !hap-² “to clothe, enfold”
See ᴱQ. vaimata- for discussion.

Elements

vaima “wrap, robe”

      ᴱQ. vaimata- v. “to robe” (Category: to Clothe, Dress)

      A word appearing as ᴱQ. vaimata- “robe” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s, a verb form of ᴱQ. vaima “wrap, robe” (QL/100).

      Neo-Quenya: Since I retain the noun ᴺQ. vaima “wrap, robe” for purposes of Neo-Quenya, I would also retain ᴺQ. vaimata- as a general verb for putting on clothing, both for yourself and others.
