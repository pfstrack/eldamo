ᴺQ. !russambos n. “robin” (Category: Bird (other))

⚠️ᴺQ. !russambos, n. “robin” [created by Tamas Ferencz, VQP] (Category: Bird (other))
ᴺQ. ^carniambos “robin, (lit.) red-breast”

A neologism for “robin” coined by Tamas Ferencz in the VinQuettaParma Wiki (VQP), a combination of russa “red-haired” and [ᴱQ.] ambos “breast”. I prefer adapting ᴱQ. karneambar “robin” as ᴺQ. carniambos.

Elements

Q. russa “red-haired”
ᴱQ. ambos “breast”
