Ad. [m]

The labial nasal survived from Primitive Adûnaic into Classical Adûnaic (SD/418), but it was unvoiced before [h] (SD/421) and assimilated to [p] before many consonants (SD/420); see the Adûnaic assimilation rules for further discussion.
