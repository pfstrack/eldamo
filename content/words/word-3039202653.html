ᴺQ. !nahtar n. “slayer, murderer” (Category: to Kill)

ᴺQ. !nahtar, n. “slayer, murderer” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: to Kill)

A neologism for “murderer, slayer” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), an agental form of nahta- “to slay”.

Elements

Q. nahta-¹ “to slay; to hurt, injure, wound”
