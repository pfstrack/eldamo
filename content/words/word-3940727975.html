      ᴱQ. pano n. “series, course, plan, arrangement” (Category: to Put, Place, Set, Lay)

      A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “series, course, plan, arrangement” under the early root ᴱ√PANA (QL/72).

      Neo-Quenya: Since ᴹQ. pano means “piece of shaped wood” in Tolkien’s later writings, I would use ᴺQ. panwë to mean “arrangement, plan”.
