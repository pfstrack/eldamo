S. gwinig n. “little-one, baby” (Category: Infant)

S. gwinig, n. “little-one, baby” (Category: Infant)
ᴺS. ^laes “babe”
N. lhaes “babe”
G. puthli “baby”

A word for a “baby” or “little one”, a combination of the root √WIN “young” with the diminutive suffix -ig (VT48/6), hence more literally “*little young one”.
