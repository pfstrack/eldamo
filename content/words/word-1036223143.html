N. cabor n. “frog” (Category: Animals (other))

N. cabor, n. “frog” (Category: Animals (other))

A noun for “frog” in The Etymologies of the 1930s, an agental form of the root ᴹ√KAP “leap” (Ety/KAP). This was initially glossed “a dog” (EtyAC/KAP).
