ᴺS. !dúvad n. “supper, dinner” (Category: Dinner)

ᴺS. !dúvad, n. “supper, dinner” (Category: Dinner)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo, a combination of S. dû “night” and ᴺS. mâd “meal”.

Elements

S. dû “night, dimness; dim, dark”
mâd “meal”

      G. dultha n. “supper, dinner” (Category: Dinner)

      A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “supper, dinner” related to G. dultha- “to dine”, possiblly based on a (hypothetical) early root ᴱ√DULU of similar meaning (GL/31).
