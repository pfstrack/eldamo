S. lebenedh n. “middle finger” (Category: Finger)

S. lebenedh, n. “middle finger” (Category: Finger)

A name for the middle finger in notes on Eldarin Hands, Fingers and Numerals from 1968, a combination of ✶lepe “finger” and enedh “middle” (VT48/5).
