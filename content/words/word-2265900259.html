ᴺQ. !vircantië n. “grid, lattice, (lit.) woven pattern” (Category: Wall)

ᴺQ. !vircantië, n. “grid, lattice, (lit.) woven pattern” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Wall)

A neologism for “grid, lattice” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-01-30 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination WIR “weave” and cantië “pattern”, so more literally “woven pattern”.

Elements

√WIR “weave, twine, weaving with cross-threads or withes”
Q. #cantië “pattern”
