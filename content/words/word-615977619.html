ᴹQ. hrímaro adv. “*for many reasons” (Category: Much, Many)

⚠️ᴹQ. hrímaro, adv. “*for many reasons” (Category: Much, Many)
ᴺQ. ^rímaro “for many reasons”

The correlative ᴹQ. {hríro, hrimiro >>} hrímaro appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112), a combination of ᴹQ. hrim- “many” and ᴹQ. -ro² “reasons”.

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would adapt this to ᴺQ. rímaro “for many reasons” using rim- “many” to be more compatible with Tolkien’s later writings.
