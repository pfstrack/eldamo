Q. verbal nouns and adjectives grammar.

In addition to the gerund, Quenya had a number of verbal noun formations that were sufficiently common that they are worth special discussion. Likewise, there are number of commonly-used verbal adjective that were not part of the conjugational system like participles, but are still worth discussion.
