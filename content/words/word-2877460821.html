ᴱQ. karpan n. “hank (of wool etc.)” (Category: Wool)

⚠️ᴱQ. carpan, n. “hank (of wool etc.)” (Category: Wool)

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “hank (of wool, etc.)” under the early root ᴱ√KᴬRPᴬR “pluck” (QL/45).
