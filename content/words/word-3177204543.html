ᴺQ. !moritöa n. “ebony wood” (Category: Wood, Timber)

ᴺQ. !moritöa, n. “ebony wood” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Wood, Timber)

A neologism coined by Tamas Ferencz for “ebony wood”, a combination of Q. morë (mori-) “black” and Q. töa “wood”.

Elements

Q. morë “dark, black; darkness, night”
Q. töa “wood (as material)”
