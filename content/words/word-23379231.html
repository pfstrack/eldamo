ᴱQ. mark n. “ripe juice, sap, ooze, moisture” (Category: Juice)

⚠️ᴱQ. marc, n. “ripe juice, sap, ooze, moisture” (Category: Juice)
ᴹQ. pirya “juice, syrup, *sap”

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “ripe juice, sap, ooze, moisture” and derived from the early root ᴱ√MṚKṚ (QL/63).
