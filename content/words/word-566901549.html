S. Lindedhel pn. “Nandorin Elf”

S. Lindedhel, pn. “Nandorin Elf”

A Sindarin term for the Nandor based on their own name for themselves: Nan. Lindi (WJ/385). This name is a combination of lind¹ “song” and Edhel “Elf”.
