ᴺQ. !nihtirya adj. “meticulous, (lit.) bit-watching” (Category: to Look)

ᴺQ. !nihtirya, adj. “meticulous, (lit.) bit-watching” [created by Orondil] (Category: to Look)

A neologism for “meticulous” coined by Orondil posted on 2024-09-21 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of ᴺQ. nihta “bit, piece” and an adjectival form of tir- “watch”, so more literally “bit-watching” as in paying attention to the little bits.

Elements

!nihta “piece, bit (of indeterminate size)”
Q. tir- “to watch (over), guard, heed; to look (at), gaze, observe”
