ᴺQ. !hyapóto n. “forepaw, front paw” (Category: Foot)

⚠️ᴺQ. !hyapóto, n. “forepaw, front paw” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Foot)
√KHY- “other”

A neologism for “forepaw” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-12-06 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of √KHYĀ “before” and póto “paw”. I find the root √KHYĀ problematic since it conflicts with “other” words based on √KHY- and is part of a directional paradigm with many experimental forms, replaced by a new paradigm later in the same set of documents (PE22/147, 168).

Elements

√KHYĀ “before, in front of place”
!póto “paw”
