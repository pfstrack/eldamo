G. tusta- v. “to inflame, kindle, set light to, burn (tr.)” (Category: to Light, Kindle)

⚠️G. tusta-, v. “to inflame, kindle, set light to, burn (tr.)” (Category: to Light, Kindle)
N. nartha- “to kindle, *ignite, inflame, set fire to”

A verb in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “inflame, kindle, set light to; burn, tr.” derived from primitive early root ᴱ√tudh- (GL/72), where ð became s before stops like t in Gnomish.
