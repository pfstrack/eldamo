Q. Eruion m. “*Son of God”

Q. Eruion, m. “*Son of God”

A name of Yésus (Jesus) as the Son of God in Tolkien’s Quenya translation of the Litany of Loreto (VT44/12). It is a compound of Eru “God” and the patronymic suffix -ion “-son”.
