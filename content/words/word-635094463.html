ᴺQ. !orhalda, adj. “dignified, (lit.) raised above” [created by Arael] (Category: Proud)

A neologism for “dignified” coined by Arael posted on 2024-12-22 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of or “above” and an adjectival form of the root √KHAL “raise”, so more literally “raised above”.

Elements

laptë “gluttonous eating, [ᴹQ.] †licking up (food or drink)”
√KHAL “raise; lift up or extend upwards”
