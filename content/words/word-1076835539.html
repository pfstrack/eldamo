Q. Súrion m. “*Wind-son”

Q. Súrion, m. “*Wind-son”

Tar-Súrion was the 9th ruler of Númenor (LotR/1038). His name seems to be a compound of súrë “wind” and the suffix -ion “-son”.

References ✧ LotRI/Tar-Súrion; UTI/Súrion, Tar-Súrion

Elements

súrë “wind, breeze”
-ion “-son, masculine patronymic”
