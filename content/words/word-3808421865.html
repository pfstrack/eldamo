N. Caleledh pn. “*Light Elf”

Noldorin form that appeared in a marginal note in the Etymologies from the 1930s along side a variant Celion and an unclear form Celelidh, possibly it plural (EtyAC/KAL). It appears to be a singular Noldorin equivalent ᴹQ. Kalaqendi “*Light Elves”, a combination of shortened form of ᴹ√KAL “shine” and Eledh “Elf”.
