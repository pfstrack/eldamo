ᴺQ. !ostoher n. “castellan, (lit.) lord of the castle” (Category: Master)

ᴺQ. !ostoher, n. “castellan, (lit.) lord of the castle” [created by Valarie] (Category: Master)

A neologism for “castellan” coined by Valarie and posted on 2024-08-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of osto “fortress” and her(u) “lord”. Compare with the name Ostoher.

Elements

Q. osto “fortress, stronghold, strong place”
Q. heru “lord, master”
