S. Achad Tarlang loc. “Tarlang’s Neck”

S. Achad Tarlang, loc. “Tarlang’s Neck”

The full Sindarin name of Tarlang’s Neck, a combination of achad “neck, rock ridge” and the name Tarlang (RC/537, PE17/92).
