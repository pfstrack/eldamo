Ilk. initial [j] vanished; [j-] > [ø-]

In Ilkorin an initial [j] (= “y”) vanished. This change is described in the Comparative Tables (PE19/19) and there are several examples in the Etymologies (see below).

      Phonetic Rule Elements

      [j-] > [ø-] ✧ PE19/019.1013 (j- > -)

      Phonetic Rule Examples

      jadme > adme j- > ø- ᴹ✶yatmā > Dor. aðum ✧ EtyAC/YAT.008
      jarna > arna j- > ø- ᴹ√YAR > Ilk. arn ✧ Ety/YAR.019
      jōr > ōr j- > ø- ᴹ√YAR > Ilk. ôr ✧ Ety/YAR.017
