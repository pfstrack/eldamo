      ᴱQ. valda n. and adj. “worth; worthy, dear” (Category: Dear, Expensive)

      An adjective (and noun?) appearing as ᴱQ. valda “worth, worthy, dear” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√VḶŘḶ [VḶÐḶ] (QL/102). It was mentioned in the contemporaneous Gnomish Lexicon in its stem form ᴱQ. vald- as the equivalent of G. bald (GL/23), elsewhere translated as “worthy, important; great, mighty” before being revised to G. {bald >>} bâl (GL/21).

      Neo-Quenya: I would retain ᴺQ. valda “worth; worthy, dear” for purposes of Neo-Quenya based on a Neo-Root ᴺ√BALAD.
