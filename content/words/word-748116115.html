ᴱQ. qinqelea adj. “languorous” (Category: Tired, Weary)

A word appearing as ᴱQ. qinqelea “languorous” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s, an adjective form of ᴱQ. qinqele “languor” (QL/77).
