ᴹQ. Aiwenóre pn. “Birdland, lower air”

Region of the lower airs in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (SM/236), also appearing in the Etymologies as compound of aiwe “bird” and nóre “land” (Ety/AIWĒ).
