Q. Ciriáran pn. “Mariner King, (lit.) *Ship King”

The title of the lord of the Teleri (PM/341), a compound of cirya¹ “ship” and aran “king”, with the y vocalized to i as it sometimes was in Quenya (for example Maiya > Maia).
