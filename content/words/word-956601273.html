Q. Ardamin m. “*World Tower”

Q. Ardamin, m. “*World Tower”
Tar-Ardamin was the 19th ruler of Númenor (UT/222). His name seems to be a compound of Arda “world” and min(don) “tower”.

References ✧ LotRI/Tar-Ardamin; UTI/Ar-Abattârik, Tar-Ardamin
