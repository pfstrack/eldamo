ᴹQ. Talmar Ambaren loc. “Roots of the Earth”

Another name for Martalmar in cosmological notes from the 1930s (SM/241), also appearing in the Etymologies as combination of the plural of talma “root” and the genitive of Ambar “world” (Ety/TAL).
