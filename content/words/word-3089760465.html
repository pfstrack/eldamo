S. Angmar loc. “*Iron Home”

The kingdom of the witch king (LotR/146). In the manuscript for Tolkien’s “Nomenclature of The Lord of the Rings”, he explained that it was corrupted Elvish, a combination of S. ang “iron” and már “home” (RC/20), hence: “*Iron Home”. The pure Sindarin form of the name would be Angbar.

Conceptual Development: In Lord of the Rings drafts from the 1940s, this name first appeared as N. Angmar (WR/334).
