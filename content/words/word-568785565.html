ᴱQ. mindl n. “piece of flesh” (Category: Flesh)

⚠️ᴱQ. mindl, n. “piece of flesh” (Category: Flesh)
Q. hrávë “flesh”

A word for a “piece of flesh” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s derived from the early root ᴱ√MIMI (QL/61). It seems to be an example of the Early Quenya phonetic rule whereby final nasal + [ḷ] became [ndḷ], so that -mḷ > -ndḷ (PE12/25).
