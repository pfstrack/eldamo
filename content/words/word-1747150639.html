ᴺQ. !muinas (muinass-) n. “hideout” (Category: Hidden, Concealed)

ᴺQ. !muinas (muinass-), n. “hideout” [created by Valerie, Luinyelle] (Category: Hidden, Concealed)

A neologism for “hideout” coined by Valerie and Luinyelle, posted on 2024-10-06 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as an abstract noun based on [ᴹQ.] muina “hidden, secret”; compare ainas “hallow” based on aina “holy”.
