ᴺS. !ylvon n. “chalice” (Category: Cup)

ᴺS. !ylvon, n. “chalice” [created by Elaran] (Category: Cup)

A neologism for a “chalice” = “large cup” coined by Elaran in 2018 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of S. ylf “drinking-vessel” with the augmentative suffix -on.

Elements

S. ylf “drinking-vessel, *cup”
S. -on² “augmentative suffix”

Cognates

  • ᴺQ. !yulmon “chalice”
