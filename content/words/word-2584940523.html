ᴺQ. !pucco (puccu-), n. “cunnus, vagina” [created by Fiona Jallings] (Category: Womb)

A neologism coined by Fiona Jallings, based on the Early Noldorin word ᴱN. huch of the same meaning derived from primitive ᴱ✶pukku- (PE13/163).
