Ad. [n]

The dental nasal survived from Primitive Adûnaic into Classical Adûnaic (SD/418), but it was unvoiced before [h] and assimilated in many cases to a following consonant (SD/420-1); see the discussion of the Adûnaic assimilation rules for further discussion.
