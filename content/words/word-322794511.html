S. Ennorath loc. “(All) the Middle-lands”

S. Ennorath, loc. “(All) the Middle-lands”

A variant form of Ennor with the class-plural suffix -ath added, meaning “lands of Middle-earth” or “(All) the Middle-lands” (LotR/1115, PE17/25-6).
