Ad. [ē] ‹ê›

Ad. [ē] ‹ê›

In Classical Adûnaic, [e] only appeared as a long vowel, having developed from the short primitive diphthongs ✶[ai] (SD/423).

    Phonetic Development

    Ad. short diphthongs [ai] and [au] monophthongized to [ē] and [ō] ē < ăĭ ✧ SD/423 (ai > ē)
    Ad. [ōu] and [ēi] became over-long [ō] and [ē] ē < ēi ✧ SD/424 (ÊI > Ê)
