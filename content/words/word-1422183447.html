ᴱN. bodamrost n. “resurrection” (Category: to Bear (of Mother))

⚠️ᴱN. bodamrost, n. “resurrection” (Category: to Bear (of Mother))

A word for “resurrection” in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s, a combination of ᴱN. bod- “back” and ᴱN. amrost “rising” (PE13/137, 159).
