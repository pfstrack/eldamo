S. Goenor m. “Fell Fire”

S. Goenor, m. “Fell Fire”

The literal Sindarin translation of Q. Aicanáro, as opposed to the form he actually used: Aegnor. It is a combination of goe “terror, great fear” and naur “fire” (PM/363).
