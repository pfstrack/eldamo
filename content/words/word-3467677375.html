ᴹQ. Eldacan, m. “Elf-bold”

A name appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s with the gloss “Ælfnoþ” = Old English for “Elf-bold(ness)” (Ety/KAN). It seems to be as compound of Elda “elf” and the root ᴹ√KAN.
