Q. Úmaiar coll. “Evil Spirits”

Q. Úmaiar, coll. “Evil Spirits”

A term for the corrupted Maiar who served Morgoth (MR/79, 165). It is a combination of the negative prefix ú- and the plural of Maia.

References ✧ MR/79, 165; MRI
