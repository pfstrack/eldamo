S. Falathrim, coll. “Elves of the Falas, Wave-folk”

The Elves of the coast of Beleriand (S/58, PM/386), a combination of falas “shore” and the class-plural suffix -rim (SA/falas), undergoing the phonetic change: medial [s] became [θ] before [l], [r] .
