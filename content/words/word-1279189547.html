Ad. [s]

The voiceless sibilant sound [s] survived from Primitive Adûnaic into Classical Adûnaic (SD/418). In speech it could be partly voiced towards [z] before a voiced consonant, but without the buzz of a full Adûnaic [z] (SD/421).
