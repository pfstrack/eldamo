ᴱQ. rista- v. “to plant” (Category: to Sow; Seed)

⚠️ᴱQ. rista-, v. “to plant” (Category: to Sow; Seed)
Q. ala-¹ “to grow (of plants) [intr. and trans.], plant; *to thrive, flourish (of other creatures)”
ᴹQ. rista- “to cut”

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “plant” under the early root ᴱ√RIẎI “scatter” (QL/80).
