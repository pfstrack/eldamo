ᴺQ. !súlendil n. “kite, (lit.) friend of the wind” (Category: Sail)

ᴺQ. !súlendil, n. “kite, (lit.) friend of the wind” [created by Delle] (Category: Sail)

A neologism coined by Delle posted on 2023-12-15 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of súlë “wind” and -(n)dil “friend”.

Elements

Q. súlë “breath; (movement of) spirit, emission of power (of will or desire)”
Q. -(n)dil “-friend, -lover; devotion, disinterested love”
