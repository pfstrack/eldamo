ᴱ√GWEÐE¹ root. “*bend, loop”

⚠️ᴱ√GWEÐE¹ root. “*bend, loop”
√LOK “bend, loop, [ᴱ√] twine, twist, curl; [ᴹ√] great serpent, dragon”

A unglossed root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s with Qenya and Gnomish derivatives having to do with “bending” and “looping” (QL/103; GL/46). There are no signs of it in Tolkien’s later writing.
