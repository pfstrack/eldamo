ᴹQ. Solonyeldi coll. “*Surf Singers”

ᴹQ. Solonyeldi, coll. “*Surf Singers”

Proper Quenya form of the name of the Sea Elves in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s: ᴹT. Soloneldi, though only the Telerin form appeared in the tales (LR/214). The Quenya form appeared only in the Etymologies, where it was given as compound of solor “surf” and nyello “singer” (Ety/NYEL, SOL).

See Q. Falmari for further discussion.

      ᴱQ. Solosimpe pn. “Shore Piper”

      Name for the third tribe of Elves (later called the Teleri) in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/16). In the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s it is glossed “Shore Piper” and given as a combination of solor “surf, surge” and some derivative of the root ᴱ√SIPI “whistle, pipe” (QL/35, 85).

      See Q. Falmari for further discussion.
