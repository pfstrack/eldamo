Q. Elendil’s Oath

Elendil’s oath made upon arriving in Middle-earth, repeated by Aragorn when he assumed kingship of Gondor (LotR/967).

Elements

et Eärello Endorenna utúlien “Out of the Great Sea to Middle-earth I am come”
sinomë maruvan ar Hildinyar tenn’ Ambar-metta “In this place will I abide, and my heirs, unto the ending of the world”
