ᴱN. tlî n. “butter” (Category: Butter)

⚠️ᴱN. tlî, n. “butter” (Category: Butter)
ᴺS. mang “butter”

A word for “butter” in Early Noldorin Word-lists of the 1920s from primitive ᴱ✶t’lḗpe (PE13/154), a temporary repurposing of forms usually used for “silver”, which in this document had the forms ᴱN. celeb from ᴱ✶kelekwé (PE13/140) and (deleted) ᴱN. tlub (PE13/154).
