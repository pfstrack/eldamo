ᴹQ. hrímina adj. “of many kinds, of many sorts” (Category: Much, Many)

⚠️ᴹQ. hrímina, adj. “of many kinds, of many sorts” (Category: Much, Many)
ᴺQ. !rimítë “of many kinds”

The correlative ᴹQ. {rhímina >>} hrímina “of many kinds” appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/107), a combination of ᴹQ. hrim- “many” and ᴹQ. -ima “-kind”.

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. rimítë “of many kinds” using rim- “many” and the later suffix -itë, as in taitë “of that sort”.
