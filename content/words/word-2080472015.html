S. Forlond loc. “North Haven”

A name for the “North Haven” of Lindon (LotR/1050), a combination of the prefixal form for- of forn “north” and lond “haven”.

Conceptual Development: On the draft maps for Lord of the Rings from the 1940s, this name appeared as N. Forlorn, with a fina (lenited) element of N. lhorn¹ “haven” instead of lond (TI/301).
