ᴺQ. !hilië n. “following (act of)” (Category: to Follow)

ᴺQ. !hilië, n. “following (act of)” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: to Follow)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2022 specifically for Eldamo, the gerund (verbal noun) of [ᴹQ.] hilya- “to follow”. Petri Tikka instead suggested ᴺQ. hilmë “following” in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, an abstract noun formation based on the root √KHIL “follow”, but I think the gerund is more straightforward.
