ᴱN. tlais n. “splashing, flapping noise” (Category: Splash, Spray)

⚠️ᴱN. tlais, n. “splashing, flapping noise” (Category: Splash, Spray)

A noun glossed “splashing, flapping noise” in the Early Noldorin Dictionary of the 1920s with variants tlais and tlass, related to ᴱN. tlach “a clap, clash” (PE13/165).
