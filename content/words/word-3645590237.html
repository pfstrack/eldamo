ᴱQ. Kópas Alqalunte(n) loc. “Haven of the Swanships”

⚠️ᴱQ. Kópas Alqualunte(n), loc. “Haven of the Swanships”

Earliest name of the swan have of the Sea Elves, appearing both with (LT1/164) and without (LT2/82) the genitive suffix -n¹.

See Q. Alqualondë for further discussion.
