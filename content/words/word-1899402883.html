ᴺQ. !ospor n. “chaff” (Category: Grain (generic, British Corn))

ᴺQ. !ospor, n. “chaff” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Grain (generic, British Corn))

A neologism for “chaff” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), probably a combination of the roots ᴹ√OS “around” and ᴹ√POR having to do with flour and meal, so literally something like “*around grain”.

Elements

ᴹ√OS “round, about”
ᴹ√POR “*meal, flour”
