ᴺQ. !laimaril n. “emerald” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !laimaril, n. “emerald” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “emerald” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of laica “green” and [ᴹQ.] maril “crystal”. I prefer this over Tamas Ferencz’s neologism laiquaril which uses the older form laiqua for “green”.

Elements

Q. laica “green”
ᴹQ. maril “glass, crystal”
