S. Dol Tarlang loc. “Tarlang’s Head”

S. Dol Tarlang, loc. “Tarlang’s Head”

A mountain near the pass of Tarlang’s Neck translated “Tarlang’s Head” (RC/536), a combination of dol(l) “head, hill” with the name of the pass.
