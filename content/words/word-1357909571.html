Q. Atanamir m. “*Man Jewel”

Q. Atanamir, m. “*Man Jewel”
Tar-Atanamir was the 13th ruler of Númenor (UT/221). His name seems to be a compound of Atan “Man” and mírë “jewel” (SA/mîr, PE17/24).
