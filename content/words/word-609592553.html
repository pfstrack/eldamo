S. Bar-en-Nibin-noeg, loc. “House of the Petty Dwarves”

The original name of the house of Mîm, translated “House of the Petty Dwarves” (UT/100). The initial element of the name is bar “house” followed by en¹ “of” and the plural Nibin-noeg of the name Niben-nog “Petty Dwarf”.
