ᴺQ. !éta adv. “even” (Category: Also)

ᴺQ. !éta, adv. “even” [created by Chaered, Luinyelle] (Category: Also)

A neologism for “even” coined by Chaered and Luinyelle, posted on 2025-08-22 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of é “indeed” and ta “and so”. It can be used in sentences like ilquen tulle, éta yára Túro “everyone came, even old Túro”.

Elements

ᴹQ. é “indeed”
Q. ta³ “so, like that, also; and also, then; etcetera”
