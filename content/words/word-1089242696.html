ᴺS. !caron n. “actor, agent, doer, maker” (Category: Doer, Agent)

ᴺS. !caron, n. “actor, agent, doer, maker” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Doer, Agent)

A combination of the verb car- “do, make” with the agental suffix -on, an alternative for [N.] ceredir. This neologism arose in a private conversation with Elaran in 2018.
