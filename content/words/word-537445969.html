Q. Oiencarmë pn. “Perpetual Production”

Q. Oiencarmë, pn. “Perpetual Production”

A term for Eru’s management of the drama or story of the world, translated “Perpetual Production” (MR/329). Most likely it is a combination of oi(o) “ever”, en- “re-” and carmë “*creation”.
