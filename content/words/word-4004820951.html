ᴱQ. etta n. “cousin” (Category: Cousin)

⚠️ᴱQ. etta, n. “cousin” (Category: Cousin)
ᴺQ. rendo “male cousin, kinsman, *relative”
ᴺQ. ressë “female cousin, kinsman, *relative”

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “cousin” based on the early root ᴱ√ETE “cling together” (QL/36).
