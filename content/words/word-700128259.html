S. Tol Morwen loc. “*Island of Morwen”

S. Tol Morwen, loc. “*Island of Morwen”

The island where the bodies of Morwen and her children rested after the drowning of Beleriand (S/230), a combination of tol “island” with her name.

tol “island, (high steep-sided) isle”
Morwen “Dark Maiden”
