ᴺQ. !maxarma n. “software” (Category: Tool)

ᴺQ. !maxarma, n. “software” [created by NQ-Wiki] (Category: Tool)

A neologism for “software” in the Neo-Quenya Wiki, a combination of [ᴹQ.] maksa “soft” with the singular of [ᴹQ.] armar “goods”. It was given as maxárma on the wiki, but a long á cannot occur before a cluster, so the form should be maxarma.

Elements

ᴹQ. maksa “soft, pliant”
ᴹQ. armar “goods”
