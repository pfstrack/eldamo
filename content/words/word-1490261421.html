ᴺQ. !lorta- v. “to faint, swoon, become dazed” (Category: to Sleep; Sleep)

ᴺQ. !lorta-, v. “to faint, swoon, become dazed” (Category: to Sleep; Sleep)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo to replace the “go dazed, swoon” senses of the Early Qenya verb ᴱQ. fanta- (QL/37).

Element In

  • ᴺQ. !lortalë “swoon, faint, dizziness”

Cognates

  • ᴺS. lortha- “to put to sleep, send to sleep”

Derivations

  • < √(O)LOR “dream, vision”

      ᴱQ. fanta- v. “to fall asleep, go dazed, swoon” (Category: to Sleep; Sleep)

      A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “fall asleep, go dazed, swoon” and derived from the early root ᴱ√FANA having to do with dream and visions (QL/37). The verb reappeared without a gloss in Early Qenya Word-lists of the 1920s (PE16/143).
