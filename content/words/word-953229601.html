Q. Eldandil m. “Elf-friend”

Q. Eldandil, m. “Elf-friend”

A term meaning “Elf-friend”, but implying an interest in them as a subject of lore (WJ/412). This name is a compound of Elda “Elf” and the suffix -(n)dil “-friend”.

References ✧ WJ/410, 412; WJI
