ᴺQ. !tixipi n. “ladybug, (lit.) insect of [many] dots” (Category: Insect)

ᴺQ. !tixipi, n. “ladybug, (lit.) insect of [many] dots” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Insect)

A neologism for a “ladybug” coined by Luinyelle on 2023-05-29 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of [ᴹQ.] tixe (tixi-) “dot” and pí “small insect”, hence literally “insect of [many] dots”.

Elements

ᴹQ. tikse “dot, tiny mark, point”
Q. pí “small insect, fly”
