    Phonetic Development

    OS. voiceless stops were voiced before nasals dn < tn ✧ VT42/26 (tn > dn)
    OS. voiceless stops were voiced before nasals dm < tm ✧ VT42/26 (tm > dm)
    OS. [z] vanished before [d] lengthening preceding vowel V̄d < Vzd ✧ PE19/91 (zd > d; with lengthening of preceding vowel); PE19/101 (zd > ´d)
    OS. [bm], [dn] became [mm], [nn] dn > nn ✧ VT42/26 (dn > nn)
    S. voiced stops became spirants after vowels Vd > Vð ✧ PE19/101 (´d > đ); VT42/26 (dm > ðm)
