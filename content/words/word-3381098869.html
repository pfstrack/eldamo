ᴺQ. !helissar n. “amethyst” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !helissar, n. “amethyst” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “amethyst” coined by by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴱQ.] helin “violet” and sar “stone”.

Elements

ᴱQ. helin “pansy”
Q. #sar “stone (small)”

      ᴺQ. !mirumírë n. “amethyst, (lit.) wine-jewel” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

      A neologism for “amethyst” coined by Valerie on 2022-05-12 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of [ᴱQ.] miru “wine” and mírë “jewel”. At the time Valerie coined this word, Helge Fauskanger’s neologism ᴺQ. helissar was mislabeled “hyacinth” rather than “amethyst”. After I discovered my mistake, I started recommending Fauskanger’s older neologism rather than Valerie’s.

      Elements

      miru “wine”
      Q. mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
