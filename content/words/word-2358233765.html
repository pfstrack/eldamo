ᴺQ. !asquétima [þ] adj. “easy to say” (Category: Easy)

ᴺQ. !asquétima [þ], adj. “easy to say” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Easy)

A neologism for “easy to say” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of as(a)- “easy”, quet- “say” and the suffix -ima “-able”.

Elements

Q. as(a)- “easily”
Q. quet- “to say, speak, tell”
Q. -ima “-able, possibility”
