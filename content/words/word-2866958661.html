G. gwarest n. “cousinhood, -ship” (Category: Cousin)

⚠️G. gwarest, n. “cousinhood, -ship” (Category: Cousin)
ᴺS. !rennas “kinship, relation”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “cousinhood, -ship”, a combination of G. gwa- “together” and G. rest “kinship” (GL/44).
