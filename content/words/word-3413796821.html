ᴺQ. !immenya adj. “parallel, (lit.) of the same direction” (Category: Straight)

ᴺQ. !immenya, adj. “parallel, (lit.) of the same direction” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Straight)

A neologism for “parallel” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-09-21 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of Q. im- “same” and an adjectival form Q. men “way, direction”, so more literally “of the same direction”.

Elements

Q. im- “same, self”
Q. men “way”
