Q. #naiqua- v. (weak-verb) “*to sin” (Category: Sin, Impiety)

⚠️Q. #naiqua-, v. (weak-verb) “*to sin” (Category: Sin, Impiety)
Q. #úcar- “*to trespass, do wrong, sin”
@@@ the editors of VT43 analyze this as an adjective used as a plural noun, but since this is a replacement of the verb phrase i naici nar “*who are sinners”, it seems likelier to me that it is also a verb: i naiquear “*who sin”. @@@ Alexander Zapragajev: this “verb” predates this present-tense formation
