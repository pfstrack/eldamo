ᴺQ. !surya-, v. “to hiss” (Category: Snake)

A neologism for “hiss” coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo, based on the root ᴹ√SUS of the same meaning, serving as a replacement for 1920s ᴱQ. histe-.

      ᴱQ. histe- v. “to hiss” (Category: Snake)
