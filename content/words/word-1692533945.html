ᴺQ. !hyallë adv. “otherwise, another way, differently” (Category: Different, Other)

ᴺQ. !hyallë, adv. “otherwise, another way, differently” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Different, Other)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-01-31 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of hya “other” and the suffix -llë; compare sillë “like this” and tallë “like that”.

Elements

Q. hya² “other thing”
Q. *-llë⁴ “like, in the same way/manner”

Element In

  • ᴺQ. tulmar tuller hyallë “things turned out differently, (lit.) events came differently”
