ᴱN. Gormagli m. “Great Bear”

⚠️ᴱN. Gormagli, m. “Great Bear”

A name of Túrin appearing in the Noldorin word list of the 1920s (PE13/149), a combination of gorod¹ “chief” and magli “bear” = “honey eater”.
