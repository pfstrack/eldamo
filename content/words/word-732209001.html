Ilk. final [mb], [mm] became [m]; [-m{bm}] > [-m]

In Ilkorin, it seems that final [-mb] and [-mm] reduced to [-m], the former probably developing as [-mb] > [-mm] > [-m]. The clearest example of this change is ᴹ✶turúmbē > trumb > Ilk. trum (Ety/TURÚM). A similar changed occurred in Noldorin, but unlike Noldorin, this change did not occur medially in Ilkorin, as indicated by Ilk. umboth.

      Phonetic Rule Elements

      [-mb] > [-m]
      [-mm] > [-m]

      Phonetic Rule Examples

      dimb > dim -mb > -m ᴹ✶dimbē > Ilk. dim ✧ Ety/DEM.009
      dimb > dim -mb > -m ᴹ✶dembē > Ilk. dim ✧ EtyAC/DEM.023
