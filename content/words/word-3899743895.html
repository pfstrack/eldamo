Q. Nurtalë Valinóreva pn. “Hiding of Valinor”

Q. Nurtalë Valinóreva, pn. “Hiding of Valinor”

The time when the Valar hid Valinor from the attacks of Morgoth (S/102). This name is a combination of nurtalë “hiding” and the possessive-adjectival form of Valinórë.
