⚠️ᴱQ. hón(a), n. “bone” (Category: Bone)
Q. axo¹ “bone”

Quenya cognates of G. hûn “bone” given as Q. hōn- or hōna- in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s (GL/49), perhaps related to the early root ᴱ√HONO “*heart” (QL/40).
