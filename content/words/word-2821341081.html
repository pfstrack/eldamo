Ad. noun-numbers grammar.

Ad. noun-numbers grammar.
Like English, Adûnaic has separate forms for singular and plural nouns, and like Quenya it has an additional form used for pairs of nouns: the dual (SD/427). Markers for these numbers are:
  • Singular: the unmodified form of the noun.
  • Plural: uses the plural inflection -î-.
  • Dual: uses the dual suffix -at.
