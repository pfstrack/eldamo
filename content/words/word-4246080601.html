Q. Lóminórë loc. “*Echoing Land”

Quenya name of Dor-lómin (WJ/145). It is probably a combination of the North Sindarin word NS. lómin “echoing” with Q. nórë “land”. The Quenya word for “echoing” is elsewhere given as lámina (Ety/LAM).
