S. Falathar m.

S. Falathar, m.

A companion of Eärendil (S/248). The meaning of this name is unclear, but it appears to contain a variant of falas “shore” (SA/falas).

Conceptual Development: In Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, his name was also N. Falathar (LR/324).
