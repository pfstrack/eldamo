ᴱQ. Tulkassan pn. “Thursday”

⚠️ᴱQ. Tulcassan, pn. “Thursday”
Q. Eärenya “Sea-day, *Thursday”

Name of the first Thursday in the Valinorean fortnight (PE14/22), a combination of the name of the god Tulkas and sana “day”.
