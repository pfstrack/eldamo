ᴺQ. !aralussë, n. “aorist, (lit.) beside-tense” [created by Luinyelle, Orondil, Röandil] (Category: Verb Inflection)

A neologism coined by Luinyelle, Orondil and Röandil on 2023-08-10, a combination of ar(a) “beside” and [ᴺQ.] lussë “(verb) tense”.

Elements

Q. ar(a) “beside, next [to]; out”
!lussë “(verb) tense”
