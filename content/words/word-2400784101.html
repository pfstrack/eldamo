ᴺQ. !linquemir n. “hyacinth [jewel]” (Category: Gem, Jewel)

ᴺQ. !linquemir, n. “hyacinth [jewel]” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Gem, Jewel)

A neologism for “hyacinth” as a jewel coined by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of linquë “hyacinth (plant?)” and mírë “jewel”.

Elements

Q. linquë¹ “(leaf of a) hyacinth”
Q. mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
