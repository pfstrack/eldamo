Dan. ealc n. “swan”

A noun for “swan” that developed from primitive ᴹ✶alk-wā (Ety/ÁLAK). It is an example of how [w] vanished after medial velars in Ossiriandic and of how [a] broke into the diphthong [ea] before the liquid [l].
