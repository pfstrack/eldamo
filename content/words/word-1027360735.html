ᴺQ. !maxata- v. “to knead” (Category: to Knead)

ᴺQ. !maxata-, v. “to knead” [created by Petri Tikka, Eddin Najetovic, PPQ] (Category: to Knead)

A neologism for “to knead” created by Petri Tikka, Eddin Najetovic in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, a causative verb formation based on the root ᴹ√MASAG that was the basis for “dough” words (Ety/MASAG).

Derivations

  • < ᴹ√MASAG “knead, make soft by rubbing, kneading”
    • < ᴹ√M(B)AS “knead”
