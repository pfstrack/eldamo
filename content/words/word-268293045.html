Ad. [θ] ‹th›

The Adûnaic voiceless dentant spirant was represented by “th” in Tolkien’s writing (SD/419), and was mostly derived from a primitive aspirated “t”: [tʰ] (SD/418). It is pronounced as the “th” in English “thin”.
