S. gwathren adj. “shadowy, dim” (Category: Shade, Shadow)

S. gwathren, adj. “shadowy, dim” (Category: Shade, Shadow)
G. mortha “dim”

A word meaning “shadowy, dim” appearing in The Rivers and Beacon-hills of Gondor of the late 1960s, the adjectival form of gwath “shadow” (VT42/9).
