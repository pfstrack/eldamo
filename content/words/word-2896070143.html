ᴺS. !randis n. “wanderer (f.)” (Category: to Wander, Stray)

ᴺS. !randis, n. “wanderer (f.)” [created by Fiona Jallings, FJNS] (Category: to Wander, Stray)

A feminine equivalent of randir “wanderer” coined by Fiona Jallings using the feminine agental suffix [N.] -dis.

Related

  • !randis “wanderer (f.)” is a feminine form of S. randir “wanderer, wandering man, pilgrim”

Elements

√RAN “wander, stray, meander, go on an uncertain course, go aside from a course (commanded or self-chosen); err”
N. #-dis “feminine agent”
