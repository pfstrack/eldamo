Q. Anarríma, pn. “*Sun Border”

Q. Anarríma, pn. “*Sun Border”

Name of a constellation (S/48). It seems to be a compound of Anar “Sun” and (ᴹQ.) ríma “border”, hence: “*Sun Border”.

References ✧ MRI; SI

Elements

Anar “Sun”
ᴹQ. ríma “edge, hem, border”
