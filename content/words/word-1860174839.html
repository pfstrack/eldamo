Q. Morinehtar m. “Darkness-slayer”

Q. Morinehtar, m. “Darkness-slayer”

A later name of one of the S. Ithryn Luin “Blue Wizards” (PM/384). It is a compound of morë (mori-) “darkness” and nehtar “slayer”.
