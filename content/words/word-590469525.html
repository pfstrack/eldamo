ᴱ√AQA root. “*seize”

⚠️ᴱ√AQA root. “*seize”
√RAPH “seize, grab, [ᴹ√] snatch”
√NAP “take (hold), pick up, grasp, seize quickly (with fingers)”

A root in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s, apparently with the sense “*seize”. Various later forms have similar meanings, making it unlikely this root remained valid.
