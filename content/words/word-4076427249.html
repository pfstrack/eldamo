ᴺQ. !eritë adj. “singular” (Category: Alone, Only)

ᴺQ. !eritë, adj. “singular” (Category: Alone, Only)
An adjective for “singular”, a combination of er “alone” and the suffix -itë. Also used as a grammatical term.

Elements

Q. er “one, alone”
