ᴹQ. Korolambe pn. “Tongue of Kôr”

ᴹQ. Korolambë, pn. “Tongue of Kôr”

Another name for Kornoldorin (ancient Noldorin) in Tolkien’s linguistic writings from the 1930s (LR/174), a compound of Kôr and lambe “language”.
