G. tinta- v. “to dance” (Category: to Dance)

⚠️G. tinta-, v. “to dance” (Category: to Dance)
ᴺS. ^liltha- “to dance”

A noun glossed “dance” in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s (GL/70), clearly based on the early root ᴱ√TṆTṆ (QL/93), probably with short syllabic [ṇ] becoming [in]. It had a past form {tinti >>} tanti showing the development of long syllabic [ṇ̄] to [an].
