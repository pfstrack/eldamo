ᴺQ. !páselanne [þ] n. “linen-cloth” (Category: Flax, Linen)

ᴺQ. !páselannë [þ], n. “linen-cloth” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Flax, Linen)

A neologism for “linen-cloth” coined by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴺQ.] pásë “linen” and [ᴹQ.] lanne “cloth”.

Elements

!pásë “linen”
ᴹQ. lanne “tissue, cloth”
