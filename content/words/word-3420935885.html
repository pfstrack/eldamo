ᴱQ. Helusion m. “Hendwine, Hlúdwine”

⚠️ᴱQ. Helusion, m. “Hendwine, Hlúdwine”

Quenya name of a son of Eriol in notes from the 1910s, equated to Old Germanic “Hendwine” (PE15/17) or “Hlúdwine” (PE15/18), with variant form Helúsion. It is probably a phonetic adaptation of the Germanic form.
