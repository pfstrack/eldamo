ᴹQ. hapas(se) adv. “together in the same place (as far as the pair or group is concerned, and in distinction to others outside it)” (Category: Like, Similar)

ᴹQ. hapas(së), adv. “together in the same place (as far as the pair or group is concerned, and in distinction to others outside it)” (Category: Like, Similar)

A correlative combination in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/100, 111), a combination of ᴹQ. hap- “about the same” and the locative suffix ᴹQ. -sse.
