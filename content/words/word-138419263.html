Ad. draft-instrumental grammar.

Ad. draft-instrumental grammar.
The draft instrumental inflection was formed with the suffix -ma or -mā (SD/439). The dual suffix -t was added directly to the instrumental inflection. In the plural instrumental, a special plural form of the instrumental suffix was added to the plural form of the noun, either -main or -men.

Examples (draft-instrumental)
bāruma ← bār ✧ SD/439
sōbēthumā “assent-with” [← sâibêth] ✧ SD/311
