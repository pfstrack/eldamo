S. Curunír ’Lân m. “Saruman the White”

S. Curunír ’Lân, m. “Saruman the White”

Sindarin translation of “Saruman the White” (UT/390), a combination of his Sindarin name Curunír and lenited form ’lân of the (otherwise unattested) adjective glân¹ “white”.
