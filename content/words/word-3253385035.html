ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] ^patacë n. “clatter” (Category: Noise, Thunder)

ᴺQ. [ᴱQ.] ^patacë, n. “clatter” (Category: Noise, Thunder)
See ᴱQ. patake for discussion.

Element In

  • ᴺQ. ^patahta- “to clatter; *to clap (+ instrumental of hands)”

      ᴱQ. patake n. “clatter” (Category: Noise, Thunder)

      A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “clatter”, a (frequentative?) elaboration of ᴱQ. pata- “rap, tap” (QL/72).

      Neo-Quenya: I would salvage this word as ᴺQ. patacë “clatter” for purposes of Neo-Quenya.
