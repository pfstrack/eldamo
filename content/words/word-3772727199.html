G. sanc n. “jag, tooth” (Category: Tooth)

⚠️G. sanc, n. “jag, tooth” (Category: Tooth)
S. carch “fang, ⚠️[N.] tooth”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “jag, tooth” based on the (Gnomish) root *sag- (GL/66). It was most likely tied to the early root ᴱ√SṆKṆ “rend, tear” (QL/85). In the Gnomish Lexicon, the word sanc was marked with an “X”, indicating it was probably rejected.
