ᴺS. !gwilchair n. “airplane” (Category: to Fly)

ᴺS. !gwilchair, n. “airplane” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Fly)

A neologism coined by Delle posted on 2024-09-14 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of [G.] vil- “fly” and cair “ship” as a thing that cuts swiftly through the air.

Elements

gwil- “to fly, ⚠️sail, float”
S. cair “ship”
