ᴱQ. Earendilyon m. “Son of Eärendel”

⚠️ᴱQ. Earendilyon, m. “Son of Eärendel”

A term for a sailor as a Son of Earendel in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/35), a compound of that name with the suffix -ion “son”.
