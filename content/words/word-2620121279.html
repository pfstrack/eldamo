ᴺS. !rosg n. “fox” (Category: Fox)

⚠️ᴺS. !rosg, n. “fox” [created by Ryszard Derdzinski, PPW] (Category: Fox)
S. rusc “fox”

A neologism for “fox” coined by Ryszard Derdzinski in Parf Pith ’Wain (PPW) from the early 2000s, based on Q. rusco. However, the actual Sindarin form S. rusc as published in Vinyar Tengwar #41 (VT41/10).

Cognates

  • Q. rusco “fox”
