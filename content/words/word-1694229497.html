ᴺS. [N.] ^rim n. “cold pool or lake (in mountains)”

ᴺS. [N.] ^rim, n. “cold pool or lake (in mountains)” [vetted by HSD]

      N. rhim² n. “cold pool or lake (in mountains)”

      this word provides an etymology for N. Mithrim “*Grey Lake”, but in later writings Tolkien explained it differently, deriving the name from the Elves that lived around the lake
