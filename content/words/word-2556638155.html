N. Elethon m. “Ælfwine”

A rejected Noldorin name for Ælfwine (“Elf-friend”) appearing in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (EtyAC/SON), a combination of Eledh “Elf” and the suffixal form of (rejected) thond “friend”.
