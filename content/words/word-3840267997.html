ᴱQ. Palisor loc.

⚠️ᴱQ. Palisor, loc.

Region where the Elves first awoke in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/114). It seems to be an elaboration on palis(te) “sward, lawn” as suggested by Christopher Tolkien (LT1A/Palúrien).
