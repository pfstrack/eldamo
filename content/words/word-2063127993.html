Q. Arda Hastaina pn. “Arda Marred”

A term for the state of Arda after evil was created by the actions of Morgoth, corrupting the original work of the Valar (MR/254). Tolkien had several other Quenya transalations of this term, including Arda Sahta (MR/405), Arda Úvana and Arda χarina (read Harina?) (PE17/150).
