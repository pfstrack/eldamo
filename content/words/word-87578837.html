Q. hrón n. “matter, substance, flesh; body”

⚠️Q. hrón, n. “matter, substance, flesh; body”
Q. hroa “body, bodily form, flesh; physical matter”
Q. erma “(physical) matter”
This form initially appeared as hrón (sometimes rhón) with the sense “body”, but it was altered >> hrondo, later >> hroa (MR/231). The sense of hrón was changed to the “substance, matter” of Arda, be this was later changed >> orma (MR/218), which was elsewhere erma.
