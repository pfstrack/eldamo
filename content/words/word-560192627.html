S. Glanduin loc. “Border-river”

S. Glanduin, loc. “Border-river”

River marking the southern border of Eriador (LotR/1039), translated “Border-river” (UT/261, VT42/7). This name is combination of glan(n) “border” and duin “river” (VT42/7-8).
