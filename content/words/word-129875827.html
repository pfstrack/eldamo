Q. Erólamaitë pn. “uniconsonantal”

Q. Erólamaitë, pn. “uniconsonantal”
A term for the uniconsontal roots of Primitive Elvish (PE18/33, 84). It is a compound of er “one” and ólamaitë “consonantal”.
