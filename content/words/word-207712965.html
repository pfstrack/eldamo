ᴺQ. !yanen adv. “whereby, by which” (Category: Relative Pronouns)

ᴺQ. !yanen, adv. “whereby, by which” [created by Helge Fauskanger, PPQ] (Category: Relative Pronouns)
See ᴹQ. ínen for discussion.

Elements

Q. ya “which, that (relative pronoun)”
Q. -nen “instrumental”

      ᴹQ. ínen adv. “*whereby, by which” (Category: Relative Pronouns)

      The correlative ᴹQ. ínen appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/111), a combination of the relative pronoun ᴹQ. i and the instrumental suffix ᴹQ. -nen.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. yanen “whereby, by which” (originally coined by Helge Fauskanger in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s) using the relative pronoun ya more commonly used in declined forms in later writings, such as Q. yassë “where [relative]”.
