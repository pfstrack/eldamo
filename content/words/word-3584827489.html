Ilk. [dl] became [gl]; [dl] > [gl]

This phonetic rule explains the development of Dor. Egla from primitive ᴹ✶edel- (Ety/ELED), and is also the Comparative Tables from the 1930s (PE19/21). A similar development occurred in Noldorin and Gnomish.
