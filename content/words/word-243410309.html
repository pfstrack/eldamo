S. Handir m.

S. Handir, m.

The 5th chieftain of the Haladin (S/195), his name is a combination of (N.) hand “intelligent” and dîr¹ “man” (Ety/KHAN).

Conceptual Development: This name appeared as N. Handir in Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s (SM/129, LR/139), and in The Etymologies it appeared with the derivation given above.
