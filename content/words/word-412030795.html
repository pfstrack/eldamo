ᴺQ. !céquen n. “candidate, (lit.) opportunity-person” (Category: Social (Other))

ᴺQ. !céquen, n. “candidate, (lit.) opportunity-person” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Social (Other))

A neologism for “candidate” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-03-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of √KE “have an opportunity” and quén “person”, so more literally “opportunity-person”.

Elements

√KE/EKE “may (be); have chance, opportunity or permission; it is open”
Q. quén “person, individual, man or woman; one, somebody”

Element In

  • ᴺQ. !céquenus(se) “candidacy”
  • ᴺQ. !céquenusta- “to apply, become a candidate”
