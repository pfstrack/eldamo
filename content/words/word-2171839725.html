Q. Nelólamaitë, pn. “triconsonantal”

Q. Nelólamaitë, pn. “triconsonantal”

A term for the triconsontal roots of Primitive Elvish (PE18/33, 84). It is a compound of (ᴹQ.) nel- “tri-” and ólamaitë “consonantal”.
