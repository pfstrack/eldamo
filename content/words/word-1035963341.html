ᴹQ. tambaro n. “woodpecker, (lit.) knocker” (Category: Bird (other))

ᴹQ. tambaro, n. “woodpecker, (lit.) knocker” (Category: Bird (other))

A noun in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “woodpecker, knocker”, an agental form of ᴹQ. tamba- “knock” (Ety/TAM).
