Q. Rombaras pn. “Horn of Oromë”

Q. Rombaras, pn. “Horn of Oromë”
The earliest name of the Horn of Oromë (MR/7). The exact meaning is unclear, but is most likely some derivation of the root √ROM “horn noise”. It was soon changed to Valaróma.
