Q. Eruhantalë pn. “Thanksgiving of Eru”

Q. Eruhantalë, pn. “Thanksgiving of Eru”

The name of a festival in Númenor (UT/166, UT/214). It is a compound of Eru “God” and hantalë “thanksgiving”.
