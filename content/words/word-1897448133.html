Ilk. usc n. “smoke”

A noun for “smoke” developed from primitive ᴹ✶us(u)k-wē (Ety/USUK). In this example, the loss of the second [u] was probably very early, since it appears in all child languages. This word is a good example of how [w] vanished after medial velars in Ilkorin, as noted by Helge Fauskanger (AL-Ilkorin/usc).
