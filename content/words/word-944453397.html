Q. Caliondo m.

The son of Eärendur and grandson of Tar-Elendil, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. His name might be a combination of cala “light” and the masculine agental suffix -ndo.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0504

Elements

cala “light”
-ndo “masculine agent”
