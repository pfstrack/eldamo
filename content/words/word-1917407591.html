ᴱQ. purya- v. “to set fire to” (Category: to Light, Kindle)

⚠️ᴱQ. purya-, v. “to set fire to” (Category: to Light, Kindle)
ᴹQ. narta- “to kindle, *ignite, inflame, set fire to”

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “set fire to” and derived from the early root ᴱ√PUŘU [PUÐU] “consume by fire” (QL/75).
