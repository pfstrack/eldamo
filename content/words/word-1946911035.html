S. Amon Tirith loc. “Hill of Guard”

S. Amon Tirith, loc. “Hill of Guard”

The hill where Minas Tirith was built, translated “Hill of Guard” (RC/772). It is a combination of amon “hill” and tirith “watch, guard”.

Reference ✧ RC/772 ✧ “Hill of Guard”

Elements

amon “hill, mountain with steep sides; lump, clump, mass” ✧ RC/772
tirith “watching, guarding, watch, ward, guard” ✧ RC/772
