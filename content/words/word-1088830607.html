Q. Cormacolindor coll. “Ring-bearers”

Q. Cormacolindor, coll. “Ring-bearers”
A title of Frodo and Sam as bearers of the One Ring (LotR/953). It is a compound of corma “ring” and the plural form of colindo “bearer”.
