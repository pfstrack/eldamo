N. Celebrond m. “Silver-mace”

N. Celebrond, m. “Silver-mace”

A name appearing in The Etymologies from the 1930s glossed “Silver-mace”, a combination of celeb “silver” and the lenited form of grond “club” (Ety/RUD).
