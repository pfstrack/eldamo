S. Gwathuirim coll. “Dunlendings”

S. Gwathuirim, coll. “Dunlendings”

Sindarin name of the Dunlendings (PM/330), apparently a combination of an otherwise unattested adjective gwathui “*shadowy” and the class-plural suffix -rim.
