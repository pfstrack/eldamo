References ✧ PE18/44; PE19/34-35, 40, 43, 46

Order (03100)

After 02400 [x], [s] unvoiced following [j], [w]
After 02600 syllabic nasals developed a preceding vowel of similar quality
After 03000 voiced stops became spirants except after nasals and liquids
Before 04300 initial [ŋ] vanished
Before 04500 initial nasals plus stops reduced to nasals

Related

  • initial nasals plus stops reduced to nasals would be a more straightforward rule if it occurred before dentalization, but that can’t be the case because of how initial isolated [ŋ] vanished after dentalization; velars were dentalized before [j]
