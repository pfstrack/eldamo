N. Delduthling f. “Ungoliant, (lit.) *Horror Night Spider”

Another name for Ungoliant appearing on in the Etymologies from the 1930s, a combination of del “horror”, dû “night” and thling “spider” (Ety/DYEL, DOƷ, SLIG).
