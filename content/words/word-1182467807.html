Q. Ercantië pn. “Great Pattern”

Q. Ercantië, pn. “Great Pattern”

A term for the great pattern of existence set in motion by Eru in some notes from 1959 (NM/287), a combination of er “one” and cantië “pattern”.

Reference ✧ NM/287 ✧ Erkantië “Great Pattern”

Elements

er “one, alone”
#cantië “pattern”
