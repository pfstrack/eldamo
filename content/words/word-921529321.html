Q. Vardilmë f. “?Friend of Varda”

Second child of Tar-Vardamir, known only from a geneology chart on UT/210. The first element of her name is likely Varda, which also appears in the name of her father. Helge Fauskanger suggested (QQ/Vardilmë) the final element might be a contraction of -ndilmë, a feminine form of -(n)dil, so perhaps: “*Friend of Varda”.

Reference ✧ UT/210.0302

Elements

Varda “Exalted, Lofty, Sublime”
-(n)dil “-friend, -lover” feminine
