G. hauthwaid n. “consort, wife or husband” (Category: Husband)

⚠️G. hauthwaid, n. “consort, wife or husband” (Category: Husband)
ᴺS. ^bethron “spouse (m.)”
ᴺS. ^bethril “spouse (f.)”

A word for “consort”, that is either a “wife or husband”, appearing as {hauthwad (hauthod) >>} hauthwaid in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s, a combination of G. haus “bed” and G. gwaid “companion” (GL/48).
