?ᴱ√MUKU² root. “*silent”

⚠️?ᴱ√MUKU² root. “*silent”
S. dîn “silence; silent, quiet”

A hypothetical early root based on words like G. mug- “keep silent, say nothing (about)” and G. maug “silent” (GL/57-58). In later writings Tolkien used words like S. dîn for “silence” in Sindarin.

    Derivatives

      • > G. mug- “to keep silent, say nothing (about)”
        • ⇒ G. mugol “taciturn” ✧ GL/58
          • ⇒ G. mugwen “secret” ✧ GL/58
