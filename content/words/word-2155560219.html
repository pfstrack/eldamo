Q. mamo pron. “who, what person” (Category: What)

Q. mamo, pron. “who, what person” (Category: What)

A pronominal form of ma “what” in notes 1969, with the glosses “who, what person” (PE23/135).

Conceptual Development: Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 had ᴹQ. mane “who, which” as the pronominal form of ᴹQ. ma, with masculine and feminine variants mano and {mare >>} manie (PE23/80, 102).

Reference ✧ PE23/135 ✧ “who, what person”

Elements

ma² “interrogative particle”
mo “one, anyone, someone, somebody”
