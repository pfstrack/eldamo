ᴺQ. !tinwerúmë n. “starling, (lit.) abundance of stars” (Category: Bird (other))

ᴺQ. !tinwerúmë, n. “starling, (lit.) abundance of stars” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Bird (other))

A neologism for “starling” coined by Tamas Ferencz, a combination of Q. tinwë “star” and Q. -úmë “large, of quantity”, hence more literally “abundance of stars”.

Elements

Q. tinwë “spark, [apparent] star”
Q. -úmë “[large], of quantity”
