ᴺQ. !rimperocco n. “zebra, (lit.) stripe-horse” (Category: Horse (generic))

ᴺQ. !rimperocco, n. “zebra, (lit.) stripe-horse” [created by Dírheron] (Category: Horse (generic))

A neologism for “zebra” coined by Dírheron on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord server in 2019, a combination of [ᴱQ.] rimpë “stripe” and Q. rocco “horse”, inspired by G. raibrog with similar elements.

Elements

ᴱQ. rimpe “stripe, strip”
Q. rocco “horse”

Cognates

  • ᴺS. ^raebroch “zebra”
