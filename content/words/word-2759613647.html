ᴺQ. !hausta, n. “nest” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Bird)

A neologism for “nest” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), based on ᴹ✶khau̯-stā “rest-ing”.
