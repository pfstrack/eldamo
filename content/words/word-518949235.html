S. Rochon Methestel pn. “Rider of the Last Hope”

S. Rochon Methestel, pn. “Rider of the Last Hope”

A song about the ride of Borondir Udalraph translated “Rider of the Last Hope” (UT/313), a combination of rochon “rider”, meth “end; last” and estel “hope”.
