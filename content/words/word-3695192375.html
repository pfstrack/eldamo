Dan. [ei], [ui] became [ī], [ū] in Primitive Danian; [ei|ui] > [ī|ū]

In Primitive Danian, the diphthongs [ei] and [ui] simplified to long vowels [ī] and [ū] (PE19/25-6). A similar change of [ei] > [ī] occurred in Proto-Germanic (ref/@@@).
