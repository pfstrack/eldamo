ᴺQ. !telcohan (telcohamp-), n. “skirt, (lit.) half-robe” [created by Luinyelle, NotD] (Category: Trousers)

A neologism coined by Steve - Ríon post on 2023-08-10, a combination of telco “leg” with [ᴺQ.] hampë “garment”.

Elements

Q. telco “leg; stem”
