ᴺQ. !telpetan n. “silversmith” (Category: Smith)

ᴺQ. !telpetan, n. “silversmith” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Smith)

A neologism coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of telpë “silver” and the suffixal form -tan of tamo “smith”.

Elements

Q. telpë “silver”
Q. tamo¹ “smith, builder, wright, artificer”
