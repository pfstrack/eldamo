ᴱQ. Koreldar coll. “Elves of Kôr”

⚠️ᴱQ. Koreldar, coll. “Elves of Kôr”

Term for the Elves who had lived in Kôr in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/143, LT2/276), a compound of that city name with the plural of Elda “Elf”. This term also appeared in the earliest Silmarillion drafts from the late 1920s (SM/21), but not in later versions of the narratives.
