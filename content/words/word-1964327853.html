S. Radhruin m.

S. Radhruin, m.

One of the twelve companions of Barahir (S/155). The language and meaning of his name is unclear.

Conceptual Development: In Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, this character was first named N. Radros (SM/319), later revised to N. Radruin >> Radhruin (LR/147, 282).
