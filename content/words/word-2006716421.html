ᴺQ. !celwë n. “spring, source” (Category: Spring, Well)

⚠️ᴺQ. !celwë, n. “spring, source” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Spring, Well)
ᴺQ. ^celu “source, origin”

A neologism for “spring, source” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, equivalent to N. celw. I prefer ᴺQ. celu as an adaptation of ᴱQ. qelu.

Cognates

  • N. celw “spring, source”

Derivations

  • < √KEL “flow (down or away), run (of water or rivers), go away”
