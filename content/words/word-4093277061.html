ᴱQ. nang- v. “I have a cold” (Category: to Cough)

⚠️ᴱQ. nang-, v. “I have a cold” (Category: to Cough)
ᴺQ. nungwë “cold (in the nose or head)”

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “I have a cold” from the early root ᴱ√NṆGṆ of the same meaning (QL/66), where the syllabic ṇ became an [aŋ].
