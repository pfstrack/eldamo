S. Ginglith loc.

S. Ginglith, loc.

A river in Beleriand flowing into the Narog (S/169). The meaning of this name is unclear.

Conceptual Development: The name ᴱN. Ginglith first appeared in Lays of Beleriand from the 1920s (LB/66), and N. Ginglith reappeared in Silmarillion maps and drafts from the 1930s (SM/221, LR/152).
