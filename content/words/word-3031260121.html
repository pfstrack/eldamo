ᴺQ. !lempetil n. “columbine, (lit.) five-points” (Category: Flower (other))

ᴺQ. !lempetil, n. “columbine, (lit.) five-points” [created by Valerie] (Category: Flower (other))

A neologism for “azalea, rhododendron” coined by Valerie posted on 2025-03-13 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of lempë “five” and tildë “point”, so more literally “five-points”.

Elements

Q. lempë “five”
Q. tildë “a fine sharp point”
