Q. Lórellin loc. “*Dream Pool”

Q. Lórellin, loc. “*Dream Pool”

The lake in Lórien where Estë sleeps by day on an island (S/28). This name seems to be a compound of lórë “dream” and a derivative of the root √LIN¹ “pool”.
