S. orodben n. “mountaineer, one living in the mountains” (Category: Hill, Mountain)

S. orodben, n. “mountaineer, one living in the mountains” (Category: Hill, Mountain)

A word for “a mountaineer, one living in the mountains”, a compound of S. orod “mountain” and S. pen “person” (WJ/376).
