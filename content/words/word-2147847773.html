ᴺQ. !hlócihonda adj. “greedy, (lit.) serpent-hearted” (Category: Stingy, Avaricious)

ᴺQ. !hlócihonda, adj. “greedy, (lit.) serpent-hearted” [created by Fëastorno] (Category: Stingy, Avaricious)

A neologism for “greedy” coined by Fëastorno posted on 2025-01-25 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of hlócë “serpent” and honda “hearted”, so more literally “serpent-hearted”. It is more metaphorical than milka “greedy”.

Elements

Q. hlócë “reptile, snake, serpent, worm”
Q. #honda “hearted”
