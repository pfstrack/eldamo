ᴺQ. !endótë n. “mean, (lit.) middle-number” (Category: Middle)

ᴺQ. !endótë, n. “mean, (lit.) middle-number” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Middle)

A neologism for “mean” as the central tendency of a range of numbers (a term in statistics for “average”), coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-06-03 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of √ENED “middle” and [ᴹQ.] nóte “number”.

Elements

√ENED “centre, middle; three”
ᴹQ. nóte “number”
