S. Tawar-in-Drúedain loc. “Drúadan Forest”

S. Tawar-in-Drúedain, loc. “Drúadan Forest”

A Sindarin name for the Forest of the Drúedain (UT/319), a combination of tawar “forest”, the plural in of the definite article i¹ and the plural of Drúadan “Wose”.
