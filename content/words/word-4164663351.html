N. Gondothrimbar loc.

N. Gondothrimbar loc.

Another name for the city Gondolin appearing in Etymologies from the 1930s, a combination the name for its people, the Gondothrim, with bâr “home” (Ety/GOND).

Conceptual Development: It appeared as G. Gondothlimbar in the earliest Lost Tales (LT2/158), revised when G. -lim became N. -rim.
