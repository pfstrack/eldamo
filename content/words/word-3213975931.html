Q. cesta-¹ v. (weak-verb) “to seek, search for” (Category: to Seek)

⚠️Q. cesta-¹, v. (weak-verb) “to seek, search for” (Category: to Seek)
Q. cesta-² “to ask”
Q. ces- “to search, examine (in order to find something)”

A verb in notes from 1964 glossed “to seek, search for”, a long form of ces- of similar meaning (PE17/156).

Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya, I prefer to use cesta- to mean “to ask” and ces- for “to search”.
