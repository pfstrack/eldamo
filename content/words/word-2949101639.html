Ad. draft-dual grammar.

Ad. draft-dual grammar.

The draft dual seems to have been formed mainly with a suffixal -t (SD/438). This dual suffix seems to have been added to other case endings; there are a number of difficult-to-interpret variations.
Examples (draft-dual)
bārut ← bār ✧ SD/438.3805
bārusit ← bār draft-dative ✧ SD/439.0203
bārōt ← bār draft-genitive ✧ SD/439.0103
bārumat ← bār draft-instrumental ✧ SD/439.0303
bārut ← bār draft-subjective ✧ SD/438.3903
