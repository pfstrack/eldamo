ᴺQ. !onquilë n. “tone, timbre, (lit.) voice-color” (Category: Sound)

ᴺQ. !onquilë, n. “tone, timbre, (lit.) voice-color” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Sound)

A neologism for “tone, timbre” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2025-03-01 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of √OM having to do with voices and [ᴱQ.] quilë “color”, so more literally “voice-color”.

Elements

√OM “of resonant sounds”
quilë “colour, hue”
