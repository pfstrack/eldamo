Q. Telumendil pn. “*Sky-lover”

The name of a constellation (S/48), a compound telumë “roof, canopy” = “sky” and the suffix -(n)dil “-friend, -lover”.

References ✧ MRI; SI

Elements

telumë “roof, canopy; dome (of heaven)”
-(n)dil “-friend, -lover”
