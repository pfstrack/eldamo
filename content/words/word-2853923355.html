Ad. [k]

The voiceless velar stop survived from Primitive Adûnaic into Classical Adûnaic (SD/418). It also developed in many cases from the velar nasal [ŋ] followed by a consonant (SD/420); see the Adûnaic assimilation rules for further discussion.
