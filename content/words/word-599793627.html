ᴹQ. entas(se) adv. “away over there” (Category: That)

ᴹQ. entas(së), adv. “away over there” (Category: That)
ᴹQ. enyanomë “*that (remote) place”
ᴹQ. enyassë “there far away, far away over there (out of sight)”
ᴹQ. yassë “there [yonder]; once upon a time”

A correlative appearing in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/97, 111), a combination of ᴹQ. enta “that yonder” and the locative suffix ᴹQ. -sse.
