G. faiglion adj. “having long hair” (Category: Hair)

⚠️G. faiglion, adj. “having long hair” (Category: Hair)
S. finnel “head of hair, fax, mass of long hair; having fine or beautiful hair; ⚠️[N.] (braided) hair; [G.] tress”

A word in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “having long hair”, an adjectival form of G. faigli “hair” (GL/33).
