Q. Yáranoldorin pn. “Ancient Noldorin”

Q. Yáranoldorin, pn. “Ancient Noldorin”

A term for Old Noldorin appearing in linguistic notes from the 1930s and early 1950s (PE18/26, 76), a compound of yára “old” and Noldorin.
