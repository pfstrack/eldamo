ᴺQ. !ucésima [þ] adj. “unsearchable” (Category: to Seek)

ᴺQ. !ucésima [þ], adj. “unsearchable” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: to Seek)

A neologism given as [ᴺQ.] alacestima “unsearchable” by Helge Fauskanger in his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), based on the verb cesta- “search (for)”. I prefer ucésima based on the shorter verb ces-.

Elements

Q. ú- “no, not, un-, in-; hard, difficult, bad, uneasy; hardly, with difficulty, ‘badly’”
Q. ces- “to search, examine (in order to find something)”
