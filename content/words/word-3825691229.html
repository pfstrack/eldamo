ᴺQ. !aumenta- v. “to dismiss, divorce, (lit.) away-send” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)

ᴺQ. !aumenta-, v. “to dismiss, divorce, (lit.) away-send” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Wedding, Marriage)

A neologism for “to dismiss or divorce” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of menta- “to send” with the prefix au- “away”, hence more literally “to send away”.

Elements

Q. au- “away (from)”
Q. menta-¹ “to send, cause to go (in a desired direction)”

Element In

  • ᴺQ. !aumentalë “dismissal, divorce”
