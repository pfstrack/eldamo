ᴹQ. ampende n. “upward slope” (Category: Slope, Cliff)

ᴹQ. ampendë, n. “upward slope” (Category: Slope, Cliff)
ᴱQ. róma “upward path, mountain side, (mountain) slope, alp”

A noun meaning “upward slope” in The Etymologies of the 1930, a combination of am- “up” and pende “slope” (Ety/PEN).
