ᴱQ. maktya- v. “to kill” (Category: to Kill)

⚠️ᴱQ. mactya-, v. “to kill” (Category: to Kill)
Q. nahta-¹ “to slay, [ᴱQ.] slay cruelly; ⚠️[Q.] to hurt, injure, wound”

A verb for “kill” in the Early Qenya Grammar of the 1920s, a causative form of ᴱQ. maka- “die” (PE14/58).
