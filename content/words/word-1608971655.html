ᴺQ. !imanen adv. “by identical means” (Category: Equal, Alike)

ᴺQ. !imanen, adv. “by identical means” (Category: Equal, Alike)
See ᴹQ. onainen for discussion.

Elements

Q. im- “same, self”
Q. -nen “instrumental”

      ᴹQ. onainen adv. “*by identical means” (Category: Equal, Alike)

      The correlative ᴹQ. onainen appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/111), a combination of ᴹQ. on- “identical” and the instrumental suffix ᴹQ. -nen.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. imanen “by identical means” using im- “same”.

      Reference ✧ PE23/111 ✧ “*by identical means”

      Elements

      on- “the same (oneness, uniqueness), identical” ✧ PE23/111 (onainen)
