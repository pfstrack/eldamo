ᴺQ. !mustalan (mustalam-) n. “mattress, (lit.) soft flat space” (Category: Bed)

ᴺQ. !mustalan (mustalam-), n. “mattress, (lit.) soft flat space” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Bed)

A neologism for “mattress” coined by Luinyelle and posted on 2024-08-10 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of Q. mussë “soft” and Q. talan “flat space”.

Elements

Q. mussë “soft”
Q. talan “flat space, platform”
