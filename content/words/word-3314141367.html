ᴺS. !mírad n. “treasury, hoard” (Category: Treasure)

ᴺS. !mírad, n. “treasury, hoard” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Treasure)
ᴱN. aus “hoard”
G. fuis “hoard”
G. fûn “hoard”
G. fô(tha) “hoard”
G. fund “hoard”

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo as a replacement for various “hoard” words from the 1910s and 20s. It is based on mîr “a precious thing”.

Elements

S. mîr “jewel, precious thing, treasure”
