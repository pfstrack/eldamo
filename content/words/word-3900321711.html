Q. Formenos loc. “Northern Fortress”

Q. Formenos, loc. “Northern Fortress”
Stronghold of Fëanor in the north of Valinor (S/71). It is a compound of formen “north” and the suffixal form -os of osto “city, fortress” (SA/formen, os(t)).
