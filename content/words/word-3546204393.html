Q. Mar Vanwa Tyaliéva, loc. “Cottage of the Lost Play”

The Early Qenya name for the “Cottage of the Lost Play” from the 1920s reappeared (without accents) in some late notes from the 1950s (PE17/64). It is a combination of mar “house”, vanwa “lost” and the possessive-adjectival form of tyalië “play”. It is remarkable that this early name remained the same in Late Quenya as it was conceived after Tolkien finished The Lord of the Rings.
