S. Ithryn Luin pn. “Blue Wizards”

S. Ithryn Luin, pn. “Blue Wizards”

Sindarin title of the Blue Wizards (UT/390), a combination of the plural of ithron “wizard” and the adjective luin “blue”, whose singular and plural forms are the same.
