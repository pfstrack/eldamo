Q. sa¹ pron. and conj. “it (inanimate)” (Category: Pronoun)

Q. sa¹, pron. and conj. “it (inanimate)” (Category: Pronoun)
@@@ in Qenya living things as trees etc. are never regarded as neuter (PE14/44) Note that most vegetable objects (esp. trees) are rarely classed as neuter or “inanimate” in Qenya - while all things may in nonce-use be personified (PE14/73)
