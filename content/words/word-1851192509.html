ᴺQ. !nívë n. “face” (Category: Face)

⚠️ᴺQ. !nívë, n. “face” [created by Petri Tikka, PPQ] (Category: Face)
ᴺQ. anta “[ᴹQ.] face, *front of the head, [ᴱQ.] cheek; ⚠️[Q.] jaw”

A neologism for “face” coined by Petri Tikka in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, derived from the root ᴹ√NIB “face, front”, but I see no reason not to use attested [ᴹQ.] anta or cendelë “face” for that purpose for that purpose.
