Q. Hallacar m. “*Tall Helm”

Q. Hallacar, m. “*Tall Helm”
Spouse of Tar-Ancalimë, the first ruling queen of Númenor (UT/211). His name seems to be a compound of halla “tall” and the suffixal form -car of carma¹ “helm”.

References ✧ UT/210; UTI

Elements

halla “tall”
carma¹ “helm”
