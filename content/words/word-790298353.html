ᴱQ. Lunde Timpínea pn. “April”

⚠️ᴱQ. Lunde Timpínëa, pn. “April”
Q. Víressë “April, *Freshness”

A name for the month of April in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/56), a combination of lunde “month” and timpínea “showery” (i.e. “April showers”).

Reference ✧ QL/56 ✧ (Lunde) timpīnea “April”

Elements

lunde “(lunar) month; (lit.) period” ✧ QL/56
timpínea “made of spray, full of fine showers, showery (of weather)” ✧ QL/56 (timpīnea)
