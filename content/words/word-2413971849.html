ᴱQ. tumpo n. “shed, barn” (Category: Barn)

A noun appearing as ᴱQ. tumpo “shed, barn” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s, derived from either ᴱ√TṂPṂ or ᴱ√TUPU (QL/93, 95).

Neo-Sindarin: Since √TUP “cover” survived in Tolkien’s later writings, I think this word can be salvaged as ᴺQ. tumpo “shed, barn” referring to an external, non-residential building, though it does conflict with [ᴹQ.] tumpo “hump, lump”.
