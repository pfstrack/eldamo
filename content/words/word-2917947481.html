Ad. ûriyat nîlô “sun and moon”

Ad. ûriyat nîlô “sun and moon”

An example of a dual phrase with two different words. The first word of the pair is declined as a dual. It contains the related pair ûri “sun” and nîlu “moon” (SD/428).
