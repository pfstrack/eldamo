ᴺQ. !hyarnúmen n. “southwest” (Category: South)

ᴺQ. !hyarnúmen, n. “southwest” [created by Röandil] (Category: South)

A neologism coined by Röandil in 2023-08-31 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of Q. hyar- “south” with Q. númen “west”.

Elements

Q. hyar- “left-hand, south”
Q. númen “west, direction or region of the sunset, occident, (lit.) going down”
