ᴹQ. aikasse n. “mountain peak” (Category: Peak, Summit)

ᴹQ. aicassë, n. “mountain peak” (Category: Peak, Summit)
ᴱQ. colman “peak, summit”
ᴱQ. tormë “mountain (peak)”

A word in The Etymologies of the 1930s glossed “mountain peak”, an abstract noun formation of ᴹQ. aika “sharp” (Ety/AYAK).
