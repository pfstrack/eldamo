ᴱQ. Ponóre loc. “Norway”

⚠️ᴱQ. Ponórë, loc. “Norway”

Qenya name for Norway in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/74), a combination of root ᴱ√PO having to do with the north, and nóre “land”.
