ᴺQ. !yánë n. “space (as a physical dimension)” (Category: Place)

ᴺQ. !yánë, n. “space (as a physical dimension)” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Place)

A neologism for “space (as a physical dimension)” based on yána “vast, wide”. For example: “space and time” = yáne ar lúme. Paul Strack coined this neologism specifically for Eldamo to replace [ᴺQ.] nairë from the Neo-Quenya Wiki based on naira “vast, wide, empty”, since that adjective based on a probably-abandoned root √ÑGAY.

Elements

Q. yána “wide, vast, huge”
