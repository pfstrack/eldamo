ᴱ√HUHU² root. “*pine”

⚠️ᴱ√HUHU² root. “*pine”
✶thŏno “pine”

A root in the Qenya Lexicon for words like ᴱQ. hóre “pine”, but Tolkien erased the root without coming up with a replacement (QL/41). There are no signs of this root in Tolkien’s later writing.
