ᴱQ. ettani n. “female cousin” (Category: Cousin)

⚠️ᴱQ. ettani, n. “female cousin” (Category: Cousin)
ᴺQ. ressë “female cousin, kinsman, *relative”

A noun for a female cousin in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s with variants ettani and ettaresse, feminized forms of ᴱQ. etta (QL/36), the second of which incorporates ᴱQ. resse “kinswoman, cousin” (QL/79).
