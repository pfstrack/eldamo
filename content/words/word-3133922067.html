S. Taur-na-Chardhîn loc. “Forest of Southern Silence”

Another name for Taur-im-Duinath appearing in revisions to the Silmarillion maps from the 1950s-1960s, translated “Forest of Southern Silence” (WJ/185). This name a combination of taur¹ “forest”, na² “of”, the soft-mutation char- of harn¹ “southern” and the soft-mutation dhîn of dîn¹ “silence”.
