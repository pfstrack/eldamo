ᴺS. !aetha- v. “to narrow (intr.); to besiege [with o(h)]” (Category: Attack)

ᴺS. !aetha-, v. “to narrow (intr.); to besiege [with o(h)]” [created by Ellanto] (Category: Attack)

A neologism coined by Ellanto posted on 2024-05-15 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), based on ᴹ√AK “narrow”. Ellanto further proposed that when used with o(h) “around” it can have the sense “besiege”, as in aethasser oh i-ost “they besieged the city, (lit.) became narrow around the city”.

Element In

  • ᴺS. !aethad(a)- “to (make something) narrow”
  • ᴺS. !aethas “siege”

Cognates

  • ᴺQ. !ahta- “to narrow (tr. and intr.); ⚠️to besiege [with os]”

Derivations

  • < ᴹ√AK “narrow, confined”
