ᴺQ. !armen- v. “to accompany” (Category: to Meet)

ᴺQ. !armen-, v. “to accompany” [created by Röandil] (Category: to Meet)

A neologism coined by Röandil posted on 2024-05-28 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of ar- “beside” and men- “go”.

Elements

Q. ar- “by-, beside, near”
Q. men- “to go, proceed, move (generally); to come, arrive”
