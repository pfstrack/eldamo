G. gwegweth n. “male sex, manhood (state)” (Category: Male)

⚠️G. gwegweth, n. “male sex, manhood (state)” (Category: Male)
ᴺS. ^anwas “manhood, *manliness, masculinity”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “male sex, manhood (state)”, an elaboration of G. gweg “man” (GL/44).
