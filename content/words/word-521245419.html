✶Ad. homorganic-series

✶Ad. homorganic-series

The consonants of Primitive Adûnaic can be grouped into four homorganic series based on their place of articulation within the mouth (SD/416). Tolkien called these the p-series (labials), t-series (dentals), c-series (palatals) and k-series (velars).
