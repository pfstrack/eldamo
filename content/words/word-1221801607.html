ᴺQ. !orpano, n. “ridgepole” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Beam)

A neologism for “ridgepole” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, a combination of OR “raise” and [ᴹQ.] pano “piece of shaped wood”.
