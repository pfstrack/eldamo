ᴺQ. !árasar (árasár-) n. “age (of a person), (lit.) day-tally” (Category: Age)

ᴺQ. !árasar (árasár-), n. “age (of a person), (lit.) day-tally” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Age)

A neologism for “age (of a person)” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-12-22 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of [ᴹQ.] ára “day” and sár “tally”, so more literally “day-tally”.

Elements

ᴹQ. ára “dawn, day”
!sár “(incised) mark, notch, tally; (generalized) score (of game or contest)”
