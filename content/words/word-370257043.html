Ilk. tóril n. “queen” (Category: Queen)

Ilk. tóril n. “queen” (Category: Queen)

A noun for “queen”, a combination of tôr “king” with the feminine suffix -il, though according to Tolkien it was used only as a title for Melian (Ety/TĀ).
