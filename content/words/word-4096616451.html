ᴺQ. !ítaquanta-, v. “to charge (an electrical device), (lit.) to fill with lightning” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Swim)

A neologism for a “to charge (an electrical device)” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-10-06 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of íta “flash, *lightning” and quanta- “fill”.

Elements

Q. íta¹ “flash”
Q. mírë “jewel, gem, precious thing, treasure; precious”
