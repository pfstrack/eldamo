ᴺQ. !macca n. “article (for exchange), ware, thing” (Category: Market (place))

ᴺQ. !macca, n. “article (for exchange), ware, thing” [created by Boris Shapiro, PPQ] (Category: Market (place))

A neologism for “article (for exchange), ware, thing” created by Boris Shapiro in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, inspired by [N.] bach of the same meaning and based on the root ᴹ√MBAKH “exchange”. Tamas Ferencz instead suggested ᴺQ. mangwa “article for trading, goods” in VinQuettaParma Wiki (VQP), based on MBAKH+mā.
