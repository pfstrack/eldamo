ᴺQ. !lihta- v. “to seal” (Category: Candle)

ᴺQ. !lihta-, v. “to seal” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Candle)

A neologism for “to seal” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a verb form of the neologism ᴺQ. lihta “seal” which was based on líco “wax”.

Elements

Q. líco “wax”

Element In

  • ᴺQ. !lihtaina “sealed”
