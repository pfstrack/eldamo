ᴺQ. !nenungol, n. “octopus, (lit.) water-spider” [created by Delle] (Category: Animals (other))

A neologism for “crocodile” by Delle posted in 2023-05-08 on the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of nén (nen-) “water” and *ungol “spider”.

Elements

Q. nén “water”
*ungol “(monstrous) spider”
