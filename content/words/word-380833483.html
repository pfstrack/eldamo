ᴺQ. !pímótar n. “ant, (lit.) insect-worker” (Category: Insect)

ᴺQ. !pímótar, n. “ant, (lit.) insect-worker” [created by Ellanto] (Category: Insect)

A neologism for a “ant” coined by Ellanto on 2023-05-29 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) as a combination of pí “small insect” and [ᴺQ.] mótar(o) “worker”, hence literally “insect-worker”.

Elements

Q. pí “small insect, fly”
!mótaro “worker”
