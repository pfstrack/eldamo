⚠️ᴱQ. cupta-, v. “to hump up, look lumpy” (Category: Lame)

A verb in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “to hump up, look lumpy, etc.” under the early root ᴱ√KUPU “hump” (QL/49).
