Q. Tainacolli pn. “Signifier”

Q. Tainacolli, pn. “Signifier”

Name of a star translated as “Signifier”, a compound of tanna¹ “sign” and a (feminine?) form of the adjective colla “borne” (MR/385), perhaps literally meaning “*Sign Bearer”.
