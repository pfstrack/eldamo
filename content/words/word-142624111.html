ᴺQ. !hasaino [þ] n. “patient, (lit.) medically treated one” (Category: to Cure, Heal)

ᴺQ. !hasaino [þ], n. “patient, (lit.) medically treated one” [created by Sami Paldanius] (Category: to Cure, Heal)

A neologism for “patient” coined by Sami Paldanius, a nominalized form of the passive participle hasaina “treated” of the verb Q. hasa- “to treat (medically)”, hence literally “*one who is treated medically”.

Elements

Q. hasa- “to treat (medically), (help to) cure; to treat kindly, make easy”
