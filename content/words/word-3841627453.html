Dan. [y]

A vowel that appeared in Danian from the i-mutation of [u], in words such as lygn and yrc, the plural of urc (Ety/LUG², ÓROK). Presumbably it is pronounced like Sindarin/Noldorin [y]: similar to French “u” in “lune”.
