ᴺQ. !cirihtor n. “reaper” (Category: to Mow, Reap)

ᴺQ. !cirihtor, n. “reaper” (Category: to Mow, Reap)

A neologism for coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), an agental form of [ᴺQ.] cirihta- “to reap”. He also used cerihtando “reaper” (perhaps intended to be *cirithando), but I think cirihtor is better.

Elements

!cirihta- “to reap, harvest”
