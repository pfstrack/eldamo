ᴺQ. !ehtipolca, n. “porcupine, (lit.) spike-pig” [created by Dírheron] (Category: Animals (other))

A neologism for “porcupine” coined by Dírheron on Discord in 2019, a combination of [ᴹQ.] ehte “spear” and [ᴱQ.] polca “pig”.
