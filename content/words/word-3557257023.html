ᴱQ. limpalasse n. “much roaring” (Category: Noise, Thunder)

⚠️ᴱQ. limpalassë, n. “much roaring” (Category: Noise, Thunder)

A noun in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “much roaring” (QL/53), a combination of ᴱQ. li(n)- “many” and ᴱQ. palasse “splashing”, so likely referring to the roar of waves on the beach.
