ᴺQ. !látacar (látacard-) n. “pub, public house, (lit.) open-house” (Category: House (other))

ᴺQ. !látacar (látacard-), n. “pub, public house, (lit.) open-house” [created by NQ-Wiki] (Category: House (other))

A neologism for a “pub, public house” appearing in the Neo-Quenya Wiki, a combination of láta “open” and [ᴹQ.][] car (card-) “house”, so more literally “open house”.

Elements

Q. láta “open (not closed)”
ᴹQ. kar¹ “building, house”
