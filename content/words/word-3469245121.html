G. hauthwais n. “matrimony, conjugal rights” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)

⚠️G. hauthwais, n. “matrimony, conjugal rights” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)
ᴺS. !best “marriage, matrimony, wedlock, ⚠️wedding”

A noun in the Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s appearing as G. {hauthwas (hauthos) >>} hauthwais “matrimony, conjugal rights”, a combination of G. haus “bed” and G. gwais “relation” (GL/48).
