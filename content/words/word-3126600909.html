Q. Elmo, m.

Q. Elmo, m.

Younger brother of Elwë and Olwë, and grandfather of Celeborn¹ in one version of that character’s history (UT/233, WJ/350). The meaning of this name is unclear, but it could be a combination of él “star” and the suffix -mo “person”.
