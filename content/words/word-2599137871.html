ᴺQ. !vinyanóna adj. “new-born” (Category: Infant)

ᴺQ. !vinyanóna, adj. “new-born” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT] (Category: Infant)

A neologism for “new-born” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of vinya “new” and nóna “born”.

Elements

Q. vinya “young, new”
Q. #nóna “born”
