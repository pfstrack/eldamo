S. Úmarth m. “Ill-fate”

S. Úmarth, m. “Ill-fate”

A pseudonym Túrin gave for his father’s name while in Nargothrond, translated “Ill-fate” (S/210), a combination of the negative prefix ú- and amarth “fate” (SA/amarth).
