ᴺQ. !arinwat n. “breakfast, (lit.) morning-meal” (Category: Breakfast)

ᴺQ. !arinwat, n. “breakfast, (lit.) morning-meal” [created by Helge Fauskanger, NQNT, Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Breakfast)

A neologism for “breakfast” coined by Helge Fauskanger for his Neo-Quenya New Testament (NQNT), a combination of [ᴹQ.] arin “morning” and [ᴹQ.] mat “meal”, inspired by [ᴹQ.] ahtumat “supper = maker’s meal” (PE21/32).

Elements

ᴹQ. arin “morning”
ᴹQ. mat “food, meal”
