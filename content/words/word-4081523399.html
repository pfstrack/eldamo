ᴱQ. Sankossi coll. “Goblins”

⚠️ᴱQ. Sancossi, coll. “Goblins”

A collective name for Goblins in the Qenya Lexicon from the 1910s (QL/85), apparently a combination of sanke “hateful” with hosse¹ “band” (LT2A/Glamhoth).
