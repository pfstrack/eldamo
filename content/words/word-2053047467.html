ᴱQ. Turondo m.

⚠️ᴱQ. Turondo, m.

Qenya name for Turgon in the earliest Lost Tales (LT1/115), perhaps a combination of tur “king” and ondo “stone”.

See Q. Turucáno for further discussion.
