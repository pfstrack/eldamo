Q. si¹ pron. “this, here” (Category: This)

Q. si¹, pron. “this, ⚠️here” (Category: This)
ᴱQ. enqui “this (emphatic)”
ᴱQ. hya¹ “this by us”
ᴱQ. icë “this”

The demonstrative “this” in Quenya, as in carnen si “I did this”. See the entry on demonstrative pronouns for further discusion.
