S. Glornan loc. “Valley of Gold(en Light)”

S. Glornan, loc. “Valley of Gold(en Light)”

Sindarin equivalent of the original Nandorin name of Lórien: Lórinand (UT/253), a combination of glaur “gold” and nan(d) “valley”.
