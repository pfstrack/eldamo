ᴺS. !tírad(a)- n. “to correct, straighten, rectify” (Category: Straight)

ᴺS. !tírad(a)-, n. “to correct, straighten, rectify” [created by Echuidor and Vyacheslav Stepanov] (Category: Straight)

A neologism coined by Echuidor and Vyacheslav Stepanov, a verb form based on [N.] tîr “straight, right”, the equivalent of ᴺQ. térata-.

Elements

N. tîr “straight, right”

Cognates

  • ᴺQ. !térata- “to straighten”
