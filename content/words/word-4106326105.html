ᴺQ. !virsúre n. “gauze, muslin, (lit.) woven wind” (Category: Cloth)

ᴺQ. !virsúrë, n. “gauze, muslin, (lit.) woven wind” [created by Fëastorno] (Category: Cloth)

A neologism for “gauze, muslin” coined by Fëastorno posted on 2024-12-28 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of √WIR “weave” and súrë “wind”, so more literally “woven wind”.

Elements

√WIR “weave, twine, weaving with cross-threads or withes”
Q. súrë “wind, breeze”
