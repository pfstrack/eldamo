Dan. [j] was lost after initial dentals and [s]; [{ttʰdnls}j-] > [{ttʰdnls}-]

Initial palatalized dentals became simple dentals in Danian, as they did in Noldorin (PE19/22). Unlike Noldorin, the [j]-sound was also lost after initial [s] (PE19/21).
