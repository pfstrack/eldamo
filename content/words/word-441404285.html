ᴺQ. !roccë n. “mare” (Category: Mare)

ᴺQ. !roccë, n. “mare” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Mare)

A neologism coined by Paul Strack in 2022 specifically for Eldamo to replace ᴱQ. lopsi “mare”, based on primitive ✶rokkē “mare” from the Common Eldarin: Noun Structure as a feminine form of ✶rokkō “horse” (PE21/82). Though I haven’t seen this word used by anyone else prior to adding it to Eldamo, it’s a rather obvious neologism and one involving very little creativity on my part.

Derivations

  • < ✶rokkē “mare”
    • < ᴹ√ROK “run on foot”

      ᴱQ. lopsi n. “mare” (Category: Mare)

      A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “mare”, a feminine variant of ᴱQ. lópa “horse”, both words appearing under the early root ᴱ√LOPO (QL/56).
