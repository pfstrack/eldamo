S. Gil-Orrain pn. “Star of High Hope”

The earliest form of Gil-Estel with the same translation “Star of High Hope” (WJ/246). The second element might be a combination Or- < ar(a)- “high” with rain¹, though the second element is usually translated “wandering” which does not match the translation.
