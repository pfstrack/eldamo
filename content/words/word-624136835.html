ᴺQ. !rácumbar n. “aconitum, wolfsbane, (lit.) wolf-doom” (Category: Flower (other))

ᴺQ. !rácumbar, n. “aconitum, wolfsbane, (lit.) wolf-doom” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Flower (other))

A neologism coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-05-07 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of [ᴹQ.] ráca “wolf” and umbar “doom”.

Elements

ᴹQ. ráka “wolf”
Q. umbar “fate, doom, curse”
