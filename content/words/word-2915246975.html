G. nithla n. “candlestick” (Category: Candle)

⚠️G. nithla, n. “candlestick” (Category: Candle)
ᴺS. !lígu “candle”

A noun appearing as {nithlafast >>} nithla “candlestick” in Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s, based on G. {nithla >>} nitha “candle” (GL/60). The second element of the deleted form was the (also deleted) word G. fast² “holder, stand” (GL/33).
