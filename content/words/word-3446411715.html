ᴺQ. !seruinon (seruinond-) n. “sedimentary rock, (lit.) settled-rock” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !seruinon (seruinond-), n. “sedimentary rock, (lit.) settled-rock” [created by Echuidor] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism coined by Echuidor posted on 2024-03-17 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), the passive participle seruina “settled” of the verb [ᴹQ.] seru- “settle on”, combined with n(g)olmë “knowledge”.

Elements

Q. nolmë “knowledge, philosophy (including science), department of wisdom (science, etc.)”
Q. ondo “stone (as a material), (large mass of) rock”
