S. Tumhalad, loc.

The field or valley between the rivers Ginglith and Narog (S/212). The meaning of this name is unclear, but it seems to contain tum “valley” (SA/tum).

Conceptual Development: In Silmarillion drafts from the 1930s, this name appeared as N. Tum-halad (LR/139).
