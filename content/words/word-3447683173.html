S. Certhas Daeron pn. “Daeron’s Runes”

S. Certhas Daeron, pn. “Daeron’s Runes”

The runic alphabetic developed by Daeron, a combination of certhas “runes-rows, runic alphabet” with his name (LotR/1123). It was also called the Angerthas Daeron.
