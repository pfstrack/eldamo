ᴺS. !yll n. “draught” (Category: to Drink)

⚠️ᴺS. !yll, n. “draught” [created by Ryszard Derdzinski, PPW] (Category: to Drink)
ᴺS. ^suith “draught, [G.] a drink”

A neologism for “draught” coined by Ryszard Derdzinski in Parf Pith ’Wain (PPW) from the early 2000s, based on Q. yulda. I prefer ᴺS. suith “draught, a drink”, but ᴺS. yll might be preferable if you think Tolkien abandoned the root √SOK/SUK “drink”.
