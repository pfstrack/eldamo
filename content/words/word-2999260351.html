ᴱQ. tessara adj. “maiden, maidenly” (Category: Girl)

⚠️ᴱQ. tessara, adj. “maiden, maidenly” (Category: Girl)

A word in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s glossed “maiden, maidenly”, an adjectival form of ᴱQ. tessa “maiden” (QL/90).
