ᴱQ. akaire (akairi-) n. “‘living beside’, wedded life” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)

⚠️ᴱQ. acairë (acairi-), n. “‘living beside’, wedded life” (Category: Wedding, Marriage)
ᴹQ. vesta¹ “matrimony, [ᴱQ.] state of marriage”

A noun for “wedded life” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s, more literally “living beside”, based on the early root ᴱ√KAYA “lie, rest; dwell” (QL/46).
