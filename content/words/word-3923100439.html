S. Gorothress loc.

⚠️S. Gorothress, loc.
A rejected and hard-to-read name, possibly referring to Ered Gorgoroth, appearing in Silmarillion map revisions from the 1950s-60s (WJ/183, 188). The letter after th was marked through and illegible, and might have been an r according to Christopher Tolkien.
