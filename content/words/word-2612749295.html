ᴺQ. !ildomë pron. “everywhere, every place” (Category: All, Every)

ᴺQ. !ildomë, pron. “everywhere, every place” [created by Vyacheslav Stepanov] (Category: All, Every)
ᴱQ. oma “everywhere”
ᴱQ. omarimni “everywhere about”

A neologism coined by Vyacheslav Stepanov posted on 2023-08-21 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of il- “every” and nómë “place”, with ancient ln becoming ld. I adopted this over my own neologism ilinomë.

Elements

Q. #il- “every, *all”
Q. #nómë “place”

      ᴹQ. qaqenome adv. “*all places” (Category: All, Every)

      The correlative ᴹQ. qaqenome appeared in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/112), a combination of ᴹQ. qa(qe)- “all” and ᴹQ. nome “place”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. ildomë “everywhere, every place” using il- “all”.

      Reference ✧ PE23/112 ✧ “*all places”

      Elements

      qa(qe)- “all, all the (with plurals), every; each, every”
