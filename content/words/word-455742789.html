ᴱQ. Makarnea m. “Red-handed”

⚠️ᴱQ. Macarnëa, m. “Red-handed”

The “popular” name of the god Makar, appearing in a rejected section in the Gnomish Lexicon from the 1910s (GL/18), a combination of má “hand”, karne “red” and the adjective suffix -a.
