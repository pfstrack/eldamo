Q. Hyarantar loc. “?Southern Height”

Q. Hyarantar, loc. “?Southern Height”

The earliest name of Hyarmentir (MR/105, 291), perhaps a compound of hyarna¹ “southern” and the element tar “high”, meaning “*Southern Height”.
