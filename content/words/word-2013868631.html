ᴱQ. loa n. “life” (Category: to Live; Living; Life)

⚠️ᴱQ. loa, n. “life” (Category: to Live; Living; Life)
Q. loa “(seasonal) year, (lit.) (time of) growth, blooming”
Q. coivië “life, [ᴱQ.] liveliness; ⚠️awakening”

A noun for “life” in the Qenya Lexicon of the 1910s under the early root ᴱ√LAWA, apparently an alternate name of ᴱQ. Vána (QL/52).
