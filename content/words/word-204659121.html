ᴺQ. !(n)ungwar-, v. “to undermine, weaken, hinder, sabotage, sap, (lit.) to under/down-fret” [created by Röandil] (Category: to Destroy)

A neologism for “undermine, weaken, hinder, sabotage, sap” coined by Röandil posted on 2023-04-09 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of Q. nu “under” and ✶ñgwar- “fret”, so more literally “under-fret”.

Elements

Q. nu “under, beneath”
✶ñgwar- “fret, wear away”
