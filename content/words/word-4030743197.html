Ilk. aspirates became voiceless spirants; [{ptk}ʰ] > [{fθh}]

These developments are similar to those of Old Noldorin. This change for initial or medial aspirates is documented in the Comparative Tables (PE19/18, 21), though the medial developments are obscured because later medial voiceless spirants voiced after vowels (PE19/23).
