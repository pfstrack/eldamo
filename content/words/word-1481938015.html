ᴺQ. !ratta n. “street; course, river-bed” (Category: Street)

⚠️ᴺQ. !ratta, n. “street; course, river-bed” [created by Boris Shapiro, Petri Tikka, PPQ] (Category: Street)
Q. ratta “track”
ᴹQ. mallë “street, (made or stone) road”

A neologism created by Boris Shapiro and Petri Tikka in Parma Penyanë Quettaron (PPQ) from the early 2000s, based on S. and N. rath of the same meaning. I think it is better to use ratta with its attested meaning “track”, first published in 2021.

Cognates

  • S. rath “street”

Derivations

  • < ᴹ√RAT “walk, go in a line (as a road)”
