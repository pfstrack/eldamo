Q. Eä n. “Creation, the (Material) Universe”

Q. Eä, n. “Creation, the (Material) Universe”
In Elvish cosmology, this is the created or material universe, so named after Eru’s command in the Ainulindalë: Eä “Let it be”. It does not contain Eruman “Heaven”.
