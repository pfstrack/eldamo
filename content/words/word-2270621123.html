ᴺQ. !ilqualumë adv. “the whole time, continuously” (Category: Whole)

ᴺQ. !ilqualumë, adv. “the whole time, continuously” [created by Paul Strack] (Category: Whole)
See ᴹQ. ilqan(an) for discussion.

Elements

ᴹQ. ilqa “all the, the whole (situation); everything, all”
Q. lúmë¹ “time, period of time, hour”

      ᴹQ. ilqan(an) adv. “all the time, the whole time, continuously” (Category: Whole)

      A correlative combination in Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 (PE23/110), a combination of ᴹQ. ilqa “the whole” and ᴹQ. -n(an) “time”.

      Neo-Quenya: For purposes of Neo-Quenya I would update this to ᴺQ. ilqualumë “the whole time, continuously”, since I think -n(an) was abandoned.
