ᴺQ. !amboraxosta n. “rib cage” (Category: Bone)

ᴺQ. !amboraxosta, n. “rib cage” [created by Delle] (Category: Bone)

A neologism coined by Delle posted on 2024-01-17 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of [ᴺQ.] ambos (ambor-) “breast, *chest” and [ᴺQ.] axosta “skeleton = collection of bones”.
