Ilk. initial [s] was lost before consonants; [sC-] > [øC-]

This rule is described on the Comparative Tables along with the unvoicing effects of the initial [s] (PE19/20-21), but in the Etymologies the only examples are [st-] > [θ-] (see below).

Order (2500)

After 2400 initial [s] spirantalized following voiceless stops ᴹ✶stalrā > Ilk. thall Ety/STAL
