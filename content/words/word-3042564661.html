ᴺQ. !lúmesar (lúmesár-) n. “age (of long-lasting objects), (lit.) time-tally” (Category: Age)

ᴺQ. !lúmesar (lúmesár-), n. “age (of long-lasting objects), (lit.) time-tally” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Age)

A neologism for “age (of a person)” coined by Luinyelle posted on 2024-12-22 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of lúmë “time” and sár “tally”, so more literally “time-tally”.

Elements

Q. lúmë¹ “time, period of time, hour”
!sár “(incised) mark, notch, tally; (generalized) score (of game or contest)”
