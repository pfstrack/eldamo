      [ṇ̄] > [an] ✧ PE12/010.2604 (long nasals + k̑ > añ); PE12/010.2605 (long nasals + t > an)

      [ṇ̄kʷ] > [aŋkʷ] ✧ PE12/010.2602 (long nasals + q > aȵ)
      [ṇ̄p] > [amp] ✧ PE12/010.2606 (long nasals + p > am)
      [-ṇ̄] > [-ān] ✧ PE12/011.1301 (ṇ̄ > -ān)
