ᴺS. !lígu n. “candle” (Category: Candle)

ᴺS. !lígu, n. “candle” (Category: Candle)

A neologism for “candle” coined by Paul Strack in 2018 specifically for Eldamo, the equivalent of Q. lícuma “candle”.

Elements

!lîg “wax”

Cognates

  • Q. lícuma “taper, candle”

      G. nitha n. “a candle” (Category: Candle)

      A noun appearing as {nithla >>} nitha “a candle” in Gnomish Lexicon of the 1910s, an elaboration of G. nith “wax” (GL/60). According to the editors, the deletion of the l might have been a smudge, but this word was followed by another one: G. {nithlafast >>} nithla “candlestick”. This supports the notion that “candle” was revised to nitha.
