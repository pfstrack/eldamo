N. Galadlevnar pn. “Week of the Trees”

N. Galadlevnar, pn. “Week of the Trees”

Name of a holiday week in the Etymologies from the 1930s (Ety/LEP), a combination of galadh “tree” and the lenited form of lhevnar “week” (EtyAC/LEP).
