Q. simo pron. “*this person” (Category: This)

Q. simo, pron. “*this person” (Category: This)
ᴹQ. hinë “this by you and me (not him)”

A pronominal form of si “this” in notes 1969 (PE23/135), so likely meaning “*this person”.

Conceptual Development: Demonstrative, Relative, and Correlative Stems (DRC) from 1948 had ᴹQ. sine “this” as the pronominal form of ᴹQ. si (PE23/102, 107), apparently used of both things and persons. The Early Qenya Grammar (EQG) of the 1920s had ᴱQ. qinqe for pronominal “this” (PE14/55).
