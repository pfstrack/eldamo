ᴺQ. !loc-, v. “to loop, curl, ⚠️bend” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: to Bend)

A neologism for “to bend” coined by Luinyelle in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS) on 2023-01-11 based on the root √LOK “bend, loop”. I prefer to use attested Q. cúna- “bend”, but would retain loc- for “to loop, curl”.
