ᴺQ. !undalasar (undalasarn-) n. “stalactite, (lit.) down-growing stone” (Category: Rock, Stone)

ᴺQ. !undalasar (undalasarn-), n. “stalactite, (lit.) down-growing stone” [created by Luinyelle] (Category: Rock, Stone)

A neologism for “stalactite” coined by Luinyelle in a 2022 Discord discussion of words for stones, a combination of undu “down”, ala-¹ “grow”, and sar (sarn-) “stone”.

Elements

Q. undu “down, under”
Q. ala-¹ “to plant, grow”
Q. #sar “stone (small)”
