ᴺQ. !marrum- v. “to move = change residence” (Category: Home)

ᴺQ. !marrum-, v. “to move = change residence” [created by Delle, Luinyelle] (Category: Home)

A neologism coined by Delle and Luinyelle, posted on 2024-01-14 in the Vinyë Lambengolmor Discord Server (VLDS), a combination of már “home” and rum- “shift, move”.

Elements

Q. már “home, dwelling, habitation”
Q. rum- “to shift, move, heave (of large and heavy things)”
