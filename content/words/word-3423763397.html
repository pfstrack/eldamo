Q. Annatar m. “Lord of Gifts”

Q. Annatar, m. “Lord of Gifts”

A name used by Sauron while he deceived the Elves of Eregion into crafting the rings of power (S/287, UT/254). It is a compound of anna “gift” and the affix tar “lord” (SA/anna, tar).
