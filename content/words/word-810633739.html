Q. Lalwendë f. “Laughing Maiden”

Q. Lalwendë, f. “Laughing Maiden”

The mother-name of the youngest daughter of Finwë, from which her Sindarin name was derived (PM/343). It is a compound of lala- “to laugh” and wendë “maiden”.
