S. Nogotheg n. “Dwarflet” (Category: Dwarf)

S. Nogotheg, n. “Dwarflet” (Category: Dwarf)

Another Sindarin names for Petty Dwarves (WJ/388), a combination of Nogoth “Dwarf” and the diminutive suffix -eg¹.
