S. Felagon m. “*Fair-minded Lord”

S. Felagon, m. “*Fair-minded Lord”

A “Sindarized” interpretation of the name Felagund, a combination of fael² “fair-minded” and the suffix -gon “lord” (PM/352). See the entry for Felagund for further discussion.
