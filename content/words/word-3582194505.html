ᴹQ. Úrien¹ f. “Sun-maiden”

ᴹQ. Úrien¹, f. “Sun-maiden”

An earlier name for Q. Arien (SM/97, LR/243). Its initial element seems to be Ûr, an early name Tolkien used for “Sun”.

See Q. Arien for further discussion.
