⚠️ᴱQ. initial and intervocalic [xʷ] became [f]; [xʷ-|VxʷV] > [f-|VfV]

References ✧ PE12/17-18

Order (01300)

After 01200 [xʷu] became [xu]
Before 02000 intervocalic voiceless spirants became voiced

      [xʷ-] > [f-] ✧ PE12/17 (x͡w > f)
      [VxʷV] > [VfV] ✧ PE12/17 (x͡w > f; between vowels)
