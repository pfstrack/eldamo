ᴺQ. !rassemunda n. “rhinoceros, (lit.) horn-snout” (Category: Animals (other))

ᴺQ. !rassemunda, n. “rhinoceros, (lit.) horn-snout” [created by Dírheron] (Category: Animals (other))

A neologism for “rhinoceros” coined by Dírheron on Discord in 2019, a combination of rassë “horn” and [ᴹQ.] mundo “snout”.
