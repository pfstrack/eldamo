ᴺQ. !minomë adv. “instead, in place of, in exchange of” (Category: Place)

ᴺQ. !minomë, adv. “instead, in place of, in exchange of” [created by Tamas Ferencz] (Category: Place)

A neologism coined by Tamas Ferencz, a combination of mi and nómë “place”. He also suggested ᴺQ. menessë as the locative of [ᴹQ.] men “place”, but in Tolkien’s later writings men usually means “way”.

Elements

Q. mi “in”
Q. #nómë “place”

      ᴺQ. !menessë adv. “instead, (lit.) in place” (Category: Place)

      See ᴺQ. !minomë for discussion.

      Elements

      ᴹQ. men “place, spot” locative
