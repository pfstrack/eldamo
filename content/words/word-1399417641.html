S. Arodreth pn. “Noble Endurance”

S. Arodreth, pn. “Noble Endurance”
A Sindarin name from a list of Sindarin worlds from around 1959 (PE17/182). It is a compound of either arod “noble” and reth- “endure”, hence “Noble Endurance”.
