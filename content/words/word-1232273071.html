ᴺQ. !sauluppo (sauluppu-) n. “cancer (disease) ” (Category: Sick; Sickness)

ᴺQ. !sauluppo (sauluppu-), n. “cancer (disease) ” [created by Shihali] (Category: Sick; Sickness)

A neologism for “cancer” coined by Shihali in 2018, a combination of Q. luppo “lump” with the prefix Q. sau-, so literally “*unhealthy lump”.

Elements

Q. sau- “very badly”
Q. luppo “clumsy piece or lump”
