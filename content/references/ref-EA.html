Encyclopedia of Arda (EA)

  • Mark Fisher; 1997-present; http://www.glyphweb.com/arda/‎

A website describing Tolkien’s created worlds. Although the Tolkien Gatewary is generally more complete, this site was written by a single author, making it more coherent. I found it helpful in my analysis and description of Elvish names. References are to individual articles by hyperlink.
