Plotz Letter (Plotz)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien; 1966-7, copyright Tolkien Estates 1989, published in VT06

A letter from Tolkien to Dick Plotz outlining the declension of Quenya nouns. The citations in this lexicon refer to the reproduction of this letter in Vinyar Tengwar #06. The chart describes written or “classical” Quenya. The notes in the letter indicate that long vowels are shortened in the final position in spoken or “modern” Quenya.

References are by line number in the chart, with the decimal value being the word position.
