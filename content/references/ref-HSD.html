Hiswelókë’s Sindarin Dictionary (HSD)

— Unfinished; Didier Willis; 1999-2008; http://www.jrrvf.com/hisweloke/sindar/online/sindar/dict-sd-en.html

The most extensive list of Sindarin words available on the internet. Unfortunately, it does not yet include Sindarin words from PE17. I generally check Sindarin entries in this lexicon against this list.

References are by word entry, for example: HSD/anu.
