Minor Documents (Minor-Doc)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, various dates

Various minor documents such as letters published by auction houses and other sources. The list below links to the original document, if it is available. References are by the date the document was originally written by Tolkien or (where this cannot be determined) the date of discovery or publication. The reference cites only relevant Elvish text.

  • 1955-CT - 1955 inscription in Christopher Tolkien’s copy of The Return of the King.
  • 1963-12-18 - a letter to Jonathan Hepworth.
  • 1964-03-05 - a letter to Eileen Elgar.
  • 1973-05-30 - a letter to Phillip Brown.
  • 2015-11-09 - notes on a draft map for Pauline Baynes, probably made around 1970 and rediscovered in 2015.
