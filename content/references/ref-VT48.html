Vinyar Tengwar #48 (VT48)

— J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Patrick Wynne; December 2005

The forty-eighth issue of Vinyar Tengwar. This issue has the second in a series of articles on the Eldarin Hands, Fingers and Numbers, most likely written in 1968. The article in this issue focuses primarily on the numbers in various Eldarin languages. The article includes a couple of related documents discussing D/L variation in Common Eldarin and various theories on how Sindarin Lhûn might have developed.

References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.
