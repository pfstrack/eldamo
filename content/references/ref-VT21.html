Vinyar Tengwar #21 (VT21)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Carl Hostetter; January 1992

The twenty-first issue of Vinyar Tengwar. It publishes the phrase sí man i yulmar n(g)wen enquantuva which appeared as an 1954 inscription by Tolkien in the 1st edition of The Lord of the Rings owned by Richard Manney.
