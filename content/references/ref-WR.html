War of the Ring (WR)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Christopher Tolkien; 1990

The eighth book in the History of Middle-earth series. It discusses the development of the Two Towers through the first half of the Return of the King (books III-V). Given the early nature of this writing, its words are best classified as Noldorin and (Middle) Qenya, even though they are the precursors to the Sindarin and (Late) Quenya words of The Lord of the Rings.
