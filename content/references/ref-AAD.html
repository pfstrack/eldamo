An Adunaic Dictionary (AAD)

  • Carl Hostetter, Patrick Wynne; September 1992; Vinyar Tengwar #25, pp. 8-26

An article in Vinyar Tengwar #25 analyzing all the Adûnaic vocabulary published up through Sauron Defeated. Despite its age, the article is pretty comprehensive, missing only a few Adûnaic names published later in Peoples of Middle-Earth. References are to page numbers in VT25.
