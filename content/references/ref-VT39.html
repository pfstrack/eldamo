Vinyar Tengwar #39 (VT39)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Carl Hostetter; July 1998

The thirty-ninth issue of Vinyar Tengwar. It contains a pair of linguistic articles related to Tolkien’s Quendi and Eldar essay (WJ/359-424). The first article, “From Quendi and Eldar, Appendix D” (Carl Hostetter, VT39/4-20), includes some linguistic notes that Christopher omitted from the Quendi and Eldar essay published in War of the Jewels. The second article, “Ósanwe-kenta” (Carl Hostetter, VT39/21-34), is a related essay by Tolkien on mental communication.
