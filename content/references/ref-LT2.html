Book of Lost Tales Part Two (LT2)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Christopher Tolkien; 1984

The second book in the History of Middle Earth series. It discusses the earliest versions of the tales that would eventually become The Silmarillion.

References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.

Its appendix of names (LT2A) is referenced separately.
