Silmarillion (S)

— J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Christopher Tolkien; 1977

The Quenta Silmarillion. Names in the main text are not included unless there is additional linguistic information. Other names are cited from the Silmarillion Index.

References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.

Its appendix (SA) of linguistic elements is cited separately.
