Parma Eldalamberon #12 (PE12)

  • J.R.R. Tolkien, (editors) Christopher Gilson, Carl Hostetter, Patrick Wynne, Arden Smith; 1998

The twelfth issue of Parma Eldalamberon. This issue publishes two documents written in the 1910s that were part of a larger work called the Qenyaqetsa: the so-called Qenya Phonology (PE12/3-28) and Qenya Lexicon (QL, PE12/29-106); Tolkien himself labeled these documents the “Sounds of Qenya” and the “Qenya Dictionary”. The Qenya Lexicon has its own set of references in Eldamo (QL), so the PE12 entries refer only to the Qenya Phonology.
