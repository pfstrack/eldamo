Vinyar Tengwar #06 (VT06)

— J.R.R. Tolkien, (editor) Dick Plotz; July 1989

The sixth issue of Vinyar Tengwar. It is one of the places in which J.R.R. Tolkien’s letter to Dick Plotz was first published. The other was the Tolkien fanzine Beyond Bree, in March 1989. Citations in this lexicon are to the Plotz Letter itself.
