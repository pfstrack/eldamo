Verbs, Syntax, Hooray! (VSH)

  • Carl Hostetter, Patrick Wynne; July 1992; Vinyar Tengwar #24, pp. 14-38

An article in Vinyar Tengwar #24 analyzing the Adûnaic texts and grammar appearing in Sauron Defeated. As this book is the primary source for Adûnaic grammar, the article is very comprehensive despite its age. References are to page numbers in VT24.
