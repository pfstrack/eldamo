Letters of J.R.R. Tolkien (Let)

— J.R.R. Tolkien, (editors) Humphrey Carpenter, Christopher Tolkien; 1981

Collected letters written by J.R.R. Tolkien. The letters contain considerable linguistic information. References are by page number; the first 2 digits of the decimal value are the line number, and the last 2 digits are the word position in the line.
