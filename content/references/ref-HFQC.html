Helge Fauskanger’s Quenya Course (HFQC)

  • Unfinished; Helge Fauskanger; 2003; https://folk.uib.no/hnohf/qcourse.htm‎

The original version Helge Fauskanger’s popular introductory (Neo) Quenya course. Though thorough for its time, this course is rather dated now. It does not necessarily reflect Mr. Fauskanger’s current use of Quenya in later Neo-Quenya works like NQNT (Neo-Quenya New Testament). A “secret” (not publically linked) beta update is available at http://folk.uib.no/hnohf/lessons.rtf, but the last time I checked is not substantially different from the original.
