Nalyë Melda

by Vyacheslav Stepanov

Nalyë melda ’r vanima lá maiya.
Ter quana ré
Luë nin i vániel vahaiya
Ar anda né.
Quiquië nin quetil, nás alassë
Ve virya nén.
Ilyë márë felmi hondonyassë
Antainë lyén.

You are more sweet and beautiful than an angel.
Through the whole day
It is heavy on me that you have gone far away
And long ago.
Whenever you speak to me, that is joy
Like fresh water.
All good feelings in my heart
Are given to you.
