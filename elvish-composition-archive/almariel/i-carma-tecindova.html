I Carma Tecindova

by Almáriel

sarte meldon
i alhanquente tenca
i nolwe úquétima
tucier i aina-parmasse
quilini tengwar
i carmanen tecindova
i carmanen tecindova

lepin tecilinya
ar i parmalasse luhta nin
cacarra! cacarra!

sanwi olea móro
ar i móro olea quettar
mal lá rie quettar
Siryar sule, lúce, ar vanie
i carmallo tecindova
i carmallo tecindova

The Scribe’s Tool

a note to a friend
an unanswered letter
the unsayable secret
sketches in the holy book
adorned tengwar
With the scribe’s tool
With the scribe’s tool

I pick up my pen
and the page enchants me
continue! continue!

thoughts become ink
ink becomes words
but not only words
spirit, enchantment, and beauty flows
Out of the scribe’s tool
Out of the scribe’s tool
