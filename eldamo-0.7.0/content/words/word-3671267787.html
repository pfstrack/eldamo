S. Malgalad m. “?yellow radiance”

S. Malgalad, m. “?yellow radiance”

The first named king of Lórien (UT/258), perhaps a combination of the root-form √MAL “yellow” and galad “radiance”. Elsewhere he is named Nan. Amdír (UT/240).
