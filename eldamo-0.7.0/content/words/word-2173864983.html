Ad. [i]

A vowel that survived from Primitive Adûnaic into Classical Adûnaic (SD/422). It also arose in some circumstances from the consonant “y” [j] (SD/419). Where circumstances or grammatical placed another vowel after [i], they were separated by a glide-consonant “y” (SD/424).

    Ad. adjacent like vowels contract and lengthen ī < ii ✧ SD/424 ([ii] > [ī])
