S. Lossoth coll. “Snowmen”

S. Lossoth, coll. “Snowmen”

The Snowmen of Forochel (LotR/1041). This name is a combination of loss “snow” and hoth “host” (RGEO/62; SA/hoth; PE17/39, 161).
