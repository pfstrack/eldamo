⚠️√BER root. “to mate, be mated, joined in marriage”
ᴺ√BES “to wed”
In earlier writings, the root of marriage-related words appeared as either √BES and √BED. The later forms Q. veru “husband” and Q. veri “wife” (VT49/45) could have been derived from either root, since both ✶[d] and ✶[s] became Q. [r] between vowels. If, however, we wish to preserve other derivatives of the earlier roots, which include some very important Noldorin/Sindarin words, the only reasonable option is to use √BES. @@@
