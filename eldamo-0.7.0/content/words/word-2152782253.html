Q. Coron Oiolairë loc. “Mound Ever-summer”

Q. Coron Oiolairë, loc. “Mound Ever-summer”

A fuller name of Corollairë, a combination of coron “mound”, oi(o) “ever” and lairë “summer” (WJ/401).
