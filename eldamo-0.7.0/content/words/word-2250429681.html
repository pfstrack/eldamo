ᴺS. [N.] ^galu n. “increase, growth; ⚠️good fortune, blessedness, blessings”

ᴺS. [N.] ^galu, n. “increase, growth; ⚠️good fortune, blessedness, blessings” [created by HSD, Paul Strack]
@@@ here the gloss is from the deleted form of the word N. galw (EtyAC/GAL²); in HSD the meaning is speculated to be the same as alma or almare: “?good fortune, blessedness, blessings”, but for these meanings I would suggest using ᴺS. alvar “(good) luck, fortune”, ᴺS. elias “blessedness” or ᴺS. eliad “blessing”.
