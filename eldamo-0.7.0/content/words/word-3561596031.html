Q. Noldóran [ñ] pn. “King of the Ñoldor”

Q. Noldóran [ñ], pn. “King of the Ñoldor”

A title of Finwë as king of the Noldor (PM/343). It is a compound of Noldo and aran “king”, where the o+a assimilated into a long ó.
