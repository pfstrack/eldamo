Q. Fíriel f. “She that Died; She that Sighed”

Q. Fíriel, f. “She that Died; She that Sighed”

A name given to Míriel after she became the first Elf to die of non-violent means (MR/250). This name is a compound of the verb fir- “expire” and the feminine suffix -iel.
