S. Laer Cú Beleg pn. “Song of the Great Bow”

S. Laer Cú Beleg, pn. “Song of the Great Bow”

The song of mourning for Beleg (S/209), a combination of laer² “song”, cû “bow” and beleg “great” (SA/beleg, cú).
