Q. Ailinel f.

Q. Ailinel, f.
The second child of Tar-Meneldur (UT/173). Her name seems to be a compound of ailin “lake” with the feminine suffix -(i)el.

References ✧ UT/210; UTI

Elements

ailin “a large lake”
-iel “feminine suffix; -daughter”
