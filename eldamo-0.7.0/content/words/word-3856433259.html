Q. Nísimaldar [þ] loc. “Fragrant Trees”

Q. Nísimaldar [þ], loc. “Fragrant Trees”

The region of Númenor around Eldalondë, known for its many flowering tree (UT/167). This name is a compound of nísima “fragrant” and the plural form of alda “tree”.
