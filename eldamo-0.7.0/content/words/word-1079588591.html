N. Thimbalt loc.

⚠️N. Thimbalt, loc.

A rejected name appearing only on the earliest Silmarillion map from the late 1920s or early 1930s, possibly a fortress of Morgoth according to Christopher Tolkien (SM/223). Its meaning is unclear.
