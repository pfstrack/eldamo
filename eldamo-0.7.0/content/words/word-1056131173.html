Q. wirnë n. “change”

⚠️Q. wirnë, n. “change”
Q. *vistë “change”
@@@ Despite its gloss, wirnë might be the archaic strong past tense of virya-: it is almost identical to the strong past tense form on PE17/189. For a noun form, vistë may be preferable.
