S. Brithiach loc. “*Gravel Ford”

S. Brithiach, loc. “*Gravel Ford”

A ford in the river Sirion (S/131), a combination of brith “gravel” and iach “ford” (SA/brith, UT/54), hence: “*Gravel Ford”.
