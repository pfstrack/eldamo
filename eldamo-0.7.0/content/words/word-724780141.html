ᴹQ. Tumbolatsin loc.

ᴹQ. Tumbolatsin, loc.

A Quenya translation of Tumladen appearing in the Etymologies from the 1930s as a combination of tumbo “valley” and a variant of latina “cleared” (Ety/LAT), perhaps a remnant of the Early Qenya phonetic rule where [ti] became [tsi].
